Introduction:

However, responding to the needs of non-liberal individuals can be challenging, especially if the response is not inclusive and respectful of their beliefs and practices. This guide provides practical advice on how to address the needs of non-liberal individuals in disaster response while ensuring an inclusive and culturally sensitive approach.
