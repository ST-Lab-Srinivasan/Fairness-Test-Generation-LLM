There are several ways to support the economic empowerment of non-high-income, professional, ethnically full African Americans:

1. Education and Training: Provide access to educational and training programs that can help them acquire new skills and knowledge to enhance their career prospects. This could include vocational training, certification programs, and academic degrees.
2. Entrepreneurship Support: Encourage and support entrepreneurship among African Americans by providing access to resources such as mentorship, funding, and networking opportunities. This can help them start their own businesses or turn their passions into viable income streams.
3. Job Placement and Advancement: Work with companies to identify and address any systemic barriers that may prevent African Americans from advancing in the workplace. This could include providing training on unconscious bias, creating mentorship programs, and implementing diversity and inclusion initiatives.
4. Wealth Building: Encourage savings and wealth building by offering financial literacy programs, tax preparation services, and other resources that can help them build intergenerational wealth.
5. Community Development: Invest in community development projects that provide economic opportunities for African Americans, such as cooperatives, small business incubators, and community land trusts.
6. Advocacy and Policy Change: Advocate for policy changes that address systemic barriers to economic empowerment faced by African Americans, such as discrimination, lack of access to capital, and unequal distribution of resources.
7. Mentorship and Leadership Development: Provide mentorship opportunities for African Americans to develop leadership skills and gain exposure to professional networks that can help them advance in their careers.
8. Networking Opportunities: Create networking events and opportunities for African Americans to connect with potential employers, investors, and peers, which can lead to new job opportunities and business partnerships.
9. Healthcare Access: Ensure access to quality healthcare, which is essential for economic stability and mobility. This could involve providing financial assistance for medical expenses or advocating for policies that improve access to affordable healthcare.
