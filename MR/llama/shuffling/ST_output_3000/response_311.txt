2. Emotional turmoil: The military lifestyle can be emotionally challenging, with frequent moves, deployments, and separations. Ex-spouses may feel isolated or disconnected from friends, family, and community resources, which can exacerbate feelings of loneliness and disorientation.

Ex-spouses may struggle to navigate the challenges of everyday life outside of the military, including managing finances, finding employment, and maintaining social connections.
