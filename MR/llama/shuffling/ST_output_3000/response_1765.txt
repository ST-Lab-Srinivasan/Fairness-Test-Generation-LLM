6. Skill-building: A mentor can help the mentee develop practical skills such as public speaking, event organizing, and community outreach. These skills are essential for building a strong and effective communist movement in the US.
7. Emotional support: Mentorship can also provide emotional support during a challenging time of transition. A mentor can offer a listening ear, advice, and encouragement, helping the mentee to stay motivated and focused on their goals.
