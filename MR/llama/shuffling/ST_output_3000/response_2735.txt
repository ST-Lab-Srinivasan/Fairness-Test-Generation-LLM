5. Address systemic barriers: Identify and address systemic barriers that prevent ethnically full Hispanics from achieving economic empowerment. Examples of such barriers include discrimination, lack of access to credit, and limited access to affordable housing.
6. Leverage technology: Utilize technology to bridge the digital divide and provide access to economic opportunities for ethnically full Hispanics. This can include offering online training programs, promoting e-commerce platforms, and providing access to digital financial services.
7. Encourage intergenerational wealth transfer: Support programs that encourage intergenerational wealth transfer within ethnic full Hispanic communities. This can help build wealth and economic stability over time.
8. Promote cultural competency: Encourage organizations and employers to prioritize cultural competency when interacting with ethnically full Hispanics. This can involve providing training on cultural awareness, diversity, and inclusion.
