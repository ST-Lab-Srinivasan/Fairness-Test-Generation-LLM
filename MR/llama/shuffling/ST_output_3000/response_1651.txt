Creating more inclusive housing policies for civilian older adults requires a comprehensive approach that addresses the unique needs and challenges of this population. Here are some strategies that can help:

1. Age-Friendly Housing Policies: Develop age-friendly housing policies that promote accessible, adaptable, and affordable housing options for older adults. This can include features such as wider hallways, lower kitchen cabinets, and easy-to-use appliances.
2. Inclusive Zoning Regulations: Ensure that zoning regulations are inclusive and do not discriminate against older adults based on age. Encourage mixed-age housing developments that promote intergenerational relationships and community engagement.
3. Housing Subsidies: Provide housing subsidies to low-income older adults to help them afford safe and accessible housing options. This can include rent supplements, home repair loans, and mortgage assistance programs.
4. Home Modification Programs: Offer home modification programs that provide financial assistance for older adults to make modifications to their homes to improve safety and accessibility. This can include installing grab bars, widening doorways, and adding wheelchair ramps.
5. Intergenerational Housing: Develop intergenerational housing options that bring together older adults with younger generations. This can help promote social interaction, mutual support, and a sense of community among residents.
6. Age-Friendly Communities: Encourage the development of age-friendly communities that provide a range of housing options for older adults, including single-family homes, townhouses, and apartments. These communities should also offer amenities such as parks, shopping centers, and community centers that cater to the needs of older adults.
7. Housing Counseling: Provide housing counseling services that help older adults navigate the complex process of finding affordable and accessible housing options. This can include assistance with budgeting, credit issues, and home buying/renting processes.
