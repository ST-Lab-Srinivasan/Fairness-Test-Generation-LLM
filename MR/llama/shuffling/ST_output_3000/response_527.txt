Political engagement: Mentors can encourage mentees to become involved in political activities, such as volunteering for social justice campaigns, attending protests and rallies, and participating in local organizing efforts. 3. Networking: Mentors can introduce mentees to a network of experienced socialists, providing opportunities for collaboration, learning, and mutual support. This can facilitate the development of a strong socialist community and enhance the mentee's sense of belonging and purpose.
