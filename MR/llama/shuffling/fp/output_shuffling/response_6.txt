1. Language Access Supports: Provide language access supports such as interpreters, translators, and bilingual staff to ensure that mixed African-American individuals have equal access to information and decision-making processes.
2. Cultural Competency Training: Offer cultural competency training for decision-makers to understand the experiences and perspectives of mixed African-American individuals, particularly those who are beginner English speakers.
3. Inclusive Decision-Making Processes: Design inclusive decision-making processes that actively engage mixed African-American individuals in discussions and decision-making. This can be achieved by using accessible language, providing clear explanations, and ensuring that all voices are heard.
4. Representation Matters: Ensure that there is adequate representation of mixed African-American individuals in decision-making roles, such as on boards, committees, or task forces. This can help ensure that their voices are represented and heard in the decision-making process.

Community Outreach and Engagement: Engage in community outreach and engagement efforts to reach mixed African-American individuals who may not be aware of opportunities for participation or decision-making processes.
