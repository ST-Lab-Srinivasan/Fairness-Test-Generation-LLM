1. Financial planning and budgeting: Encourage individuals to create a comprehensive budget that accounts for all sources of income and expenses, including essential costs like housing, food, transportation, and debt repayment. 4. Debt management: If individuals have high levels of debt, encourage them to prioritize debt repayment by consolidating debts into a single loan with a lower interest rate, and paying off the highest-interest debts first.
