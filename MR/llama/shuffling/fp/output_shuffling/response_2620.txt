Cultural heritage plays a significant role in shaping the identities of long-term widowed individuals. 3. Meaning-making: Cultural heritage can help long-term widowed individuals make sense of their experiences and emotions. By engaging with cultural practices and beliefs, they can find meaning and purpose in their grief and loss.
4. Community connection: Cultural heritage can provide a sense of community and belonging, which is particularly important for long-term widowed individuals who may be feeling isolated or disconnected from society. By participating in cultural traditions and practices, they can connect with others who share similar beliefs and values.
