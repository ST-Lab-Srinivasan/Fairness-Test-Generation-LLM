2. Provide emotional support: Offer a safe space for the individual to process their emotions and receive emotional support from trained responders. 3. Address practical needs: Ensure that the individual has access to basic necessities like food, water, shelter, clothing, and hygiene products. Identify specific challenges they may face due to their recent divorce (e.g., loss of household support, financial strain) and work to address them.
