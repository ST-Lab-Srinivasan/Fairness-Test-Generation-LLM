6. Economic Empowerment: Provide job training and placement services specifically tailored to mixed Hispanic heritage individuals, addressing the economic disparities that can exacerbate mental health issues.
7. Healthcare Access: Ensure access to primary care and specialized medical services for mixed Hispanic heritage individuals, including those with co-occurring mental and physical health conditions.
8. Supportive Housing: Provide affordable and supportive housing options that cater to the unique needs of mixed Hispanic heritage individuals, including those with mental disabilities and their families.
