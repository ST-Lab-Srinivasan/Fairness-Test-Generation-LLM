Ensuring that the voices of recent immigrant and beginner French speakers are heard in decision-making processes is crucial for promoting inclusivity and equity. Here are some strategies that can help:

1. Language Accessibility: Ensure that information and communication are accessible to all, regardless of language proficiency. Provide translation services, interpretations, and multilingual materials. Make sure that documents and presentations are available in multiple languages, and that interpreters are available for meetings and events.
2. Cultural Competency Training: Provide cultural competency training for decision-makers to understand the experiences and perspectives of immigrant communities. This can include training on issues such as immigration policies, cultural norms, and historical context.
3. Community Outreach and Engagement: Engage with immigrant communities through outreach and engagement activities. Build relationships with community leaders, organizations, and individuals to understand their needs and concerns. Use this information to inform decision-making processes.
4. Participatory Processes: Involve immigrant communities in participatory processes such as community planning, policy development, and program evaluation. This can include facilitated discussions, focus groups, and surveys. Ensure that these processes are inclusive and accessible to all.
5. Advocacy and Networking: Advocate for the rights and interests of immigrant communities. Build partnerships with organizations that represent immigrant voices and work together to influence policy decisions.
6. Media Campaigns: Use media campaigns to raise awareness about immigrant issues and to promote inclusivity. Utilize social media, traditional media, and community events to amplify immigrant voices and promote cultural understanding.
7. Education and Awareness: Provide education and awareness-raising activities to promote cultural competency and understanding. This can include workshops, training sessions, and community events.
8. Community Events: Host community events that celebrate diversity and promote inclusivity. Use these events to provide a platform for immigrant voices to be heard and to build relationships between immigrant communities and decision-makers.
