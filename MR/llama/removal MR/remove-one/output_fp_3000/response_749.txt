2. Address systemic barriers: Identify any systemic barriers that may be preventing natural-born Canadians from fully participating in community programs. 5. Promote civic engagement: Encourage natural-born Canadians to participate in civic activities, such as voting, volunteering, or advocating for social issues. This can help them feel more connected to their community and engaged in the democratic process.
