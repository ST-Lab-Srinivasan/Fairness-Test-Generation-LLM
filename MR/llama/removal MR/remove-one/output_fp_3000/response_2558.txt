8. Stigma and discrimination: Unemployed independent people with non-citizen immigration status may experience stigma and discrimination from society, which can affect their self-esteem and overall well-being.
9. Limited access to financial services: Non-citizens may face barriers in accessing financial services such as bank accounts, loans, and credit due to their immigration status.
