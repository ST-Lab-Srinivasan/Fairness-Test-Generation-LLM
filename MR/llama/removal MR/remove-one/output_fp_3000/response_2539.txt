1. Sense of belonging: Cultural heritage provides me with a sense of belonging to a larger community. It connects me to my ancestors and the generations that came before me, giving me a sense of continuity and roots. This sense of belonging helps me feel grounded and secure in my identity.
2. Identity formation: Cultural heritage influences how I form my identity. It shapes my values, beliefs, and behaviors, and helps me understand who I am and where I come from. By embracing my cultural heritage, I can develop a strong sense of self and build relationships with others based on shared cultural practices.

Cultural heritage provides a framework for understanding and appreciating diverse perspectives, customs, and traditions. This exchange of cultural knowledge and practices enriches my life and helps me grow as an individual.
4. Connection to tradition: Cultural heritage connects me to the traditions and practices of my ancestors. By learning about and participating in cultural ceremonies, rituals, and activities, I feel a deep connection to the past and the generations that came before me. This connection helps me understand the importance of preserving cultural heritage for future generations.
