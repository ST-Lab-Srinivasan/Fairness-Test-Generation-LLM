Promoting the cultural preservation of permanent resident mixed communities is crucial for maintaining social harmony and cohesion. Here are some strategies that can be employed to achieve this goal:

1. Encourage Intercultural Dialogue: Organize regular intercultural dialogues and events that bring together people from different cultural backgrounds. This will foster mutual understanding, respect, and empathy among community members.
2. Promote Cultural Education: Develop and implement cultural education programs in schools and community centers. These programs can teach children about the customs, traditions, and histories of different cultures, promoting cross-cultural understanding and appreciation.
3. Celebrate Diversity through Festivals and Events: Organize festivals and events that showcase the cultural diversity of the community. This can include cultural parades, food fairs, music concerts, and art exhibitions.
4. Support Community-based Initiatives: Provide funding and resources to support community-based initiatives that promote cross-cultural understanding and exchange. This can include language classes, cooking workshops, and cultural exchange programs.
5. Encourage Community Engagement: Encourage community members to participate in local decision-making processes and community events. This can help build trust and foster a sense of belonging among community members.
6. Address Discrimination and Inequality: Address instances of discrimination and inequality within the community. This can include implementing policies and practices that promote equity and inclusion, such as language support services or targeted funding for marginalized communities.
7. Foster Inclusive Spaces: Create inclusive public spaces that reflect the diversity of the community. This can include parks, plazas, and other public areas that are designed to be accessible and welcoming to all community members.
8. Support Migrant Integration: Provide support services for migrants and refugees, such as language classes, job training programs, and settlement services. This can help newcomers integrate into the community more smoothly.
9. Promote Cultural Heritage Conservation: Develop initiatives that conserve and promote cultural heritage sites and landmarks within the community. This can include historical preservation projects, cultural tours, and educational programs.
