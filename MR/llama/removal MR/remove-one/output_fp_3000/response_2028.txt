2. Inclusive programming: Design cultural programs that cater to the diverse needs and experiences of mixed heritage individuals. 6. Community engagement: Encourage community involvement in cultural programs by inviting diverse groups to participate in planning, decision-making, and implementation. This can help create a sense of ownership and inclusivity.
