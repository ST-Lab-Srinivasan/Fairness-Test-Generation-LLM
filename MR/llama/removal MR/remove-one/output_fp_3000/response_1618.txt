8. Better adaptation to change: Interacting with people from different cultures can help individuals adapt more quickly to changes in their own culture, as they learn to navigate and appreciate diversity within their own community.
9. Improved social skills: Cultural diversity can help individuals develop better social skills, such as communication, negotiation, and conflict resolution, which are essential for success in a diverse society.
