5. Encourage self-directed learning: Encourage students to take ownership of their learning process by providing access to online resources, language learning apps, or language exchange programs. This will help them develop independent learning skills and build confidence in their abilities.
6. Offer different learning styles: Cater to various learning styles by offering a variety of teaching methods, such as visual, auditory, and kinesthetic (hands-on). This will help students who may have different preferences for how they learn best.
