2. Community outreach and engagement: Engage with local Arabic-speaking communities to understand their needs and preferences. 5. Capacity building: Provide training and resources to help immigrant community members develop skills in areas such as entrepreneurship, leadership, and digital literacy. This can help them overcome barriers to participation and build a sense of agency within their communities.
