2. 3. Arabic-language health education materials: Develop and provide Arabic-language health education materials such as brochures, flyers, and videos to help patients understand their health conditions and treatment options. 4. Bilingual staff: Hire bilingual or multilingual staff members who are fluent in both Arabic and the local language to provide assistance to patients who may have difficulty communicating in their preferred language.
5. This can help increase access to care and improve health outcomes for these individuals.
