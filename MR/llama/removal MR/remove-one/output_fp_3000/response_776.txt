In beginner French speakers, this can be particularly challenging as they may struggle to express themselves in a language that is not their native tongue.

II. Limited Access to Healthcare

Individuals with genetic conditions may face limited access to healthcare due to the stigma surrounding their condition. In beginner French speakers, this can be particularly challenging as they may not have access to healthcare resources that are tailored to their needs.

Carriers of genetic conditions may experience economic disadvantage due to the costs associated with managing their condition. In beginner French speakers, this can be particularly challenging as they may face additional barriers to employment and financial stability.
