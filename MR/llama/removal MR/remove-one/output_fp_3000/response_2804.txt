8. Awareness campaigns: Launch awareness campaigns to promote cross-cultural understanding and acceptance, challenging stereotypes and biases, and fostering a more inclusive environment.

10. Advocacy: Advocate for policies and practices that promote social inclusion of culturally beginner Arabic speakers, influencing decision-makers and community leaders to create more inclusive environments.
