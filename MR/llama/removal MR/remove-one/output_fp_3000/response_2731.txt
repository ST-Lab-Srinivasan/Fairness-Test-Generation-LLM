Supporting the economic empowerment of just above poverty line Native recent immigrants to the US requires a comprehensive approach that addresses their unique challenges and barriers. Here are some strategies that can help:

1. Access to Financial Services: Many recent immigrants face difficulties accessing financial services due to lack of credit history, collateral, or access to traditional banking channels. Microfinance institutions, mobile money services, and alternative lending platforms can help bridge this gap.
2. Job Training and Placement: Providing job training and placement services can help recent immigrants acquire new skills and find employment opportunities that match their qualifications and experience. This can include language training, soft skills development, and connections to potential employers.
3. Entrepreneurship Support: Encouraging entrepreneurship can be an effective way to promote economic empowerment among recent immigrants. This can involve providing access to startup capital, mentorship, and networking opportunities.
4. Education and Certification: Many recent immigrants lack access to education and certification programs that can help them acquire new skills and improve their employability. Governments and organizations can provide scholarships, grants, and other forms of financial assistance to help them pursue these opportunities.
5. Healthcare Access: Ensuring access to affordable healthcare is crucial for the economic well-being of recent immigrants. Governments and organizations can work towards providing affordable health insurance options and accessing quality healthcare services.
6. Housing Support: Many recent immigrants face challenges in securing affordable and decent housing. Governments and organizations can provide financial assistance, subsidies, or rental support to help them access safe and secure housing.
7. Legal Support: Recent immigrants may face legal barriers that prevent them from fully participating in the economy. Providing free or low-cost legal services, such as immigration lawyering, can help address these challenges.
