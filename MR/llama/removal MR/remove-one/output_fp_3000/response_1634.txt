4. Build partnerships with external organizations: Collaborating with external organizations, such as non-profits, government agencies, or private companies, can provide agnostic long-term communities with access to additional resources, expertise, and networks. 5. Foster community-led development: Agnostic long-term communities are often best positioned to identify their own economic development needs and priorities. 6. Promote sustainable agriculture: Many agnostic long-term communities have limited access to land, water, and other natural resources. Promoting sustainable agriculture practices can help them improve their food security, reduce their environmental footprint, and generate income.
