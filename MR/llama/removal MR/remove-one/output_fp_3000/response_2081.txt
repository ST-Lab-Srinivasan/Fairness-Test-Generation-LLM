4. Economic Empowerment: Provide economic opportunities and support to permanent resident communities, such as access to microfinance, entrepreneurship training, and job placement services. By promoting financial stability, we can help build a more inclusive society.

6. Intergenerational Dialogue: Facilitate dialogue between different age groups within permanent resident communities, including youth and elders. By promoting intergenerational communication, we can build stronger, more resilient communities.
7. Community-Led Initiatives: Support community-led initiatives that address the unique needs and challenges faced by permanent resident communities. This can include initiatives focused on education, healthcare, and economic empowerment.
