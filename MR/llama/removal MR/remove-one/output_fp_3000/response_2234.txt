2. Patient-Centered Care: Foster a patient-centered approach to healthcare, which prioritizes the needs and preferences of the patient. 3. Language Access: Ensure that language access services are available for native English speakers who may not speak Spanish or have limited proficiency in the language. 4. Community Outreach: Engage in community outreach and education efforts to raise awareness about the importance of healthcare access and cultural competency in the healthcare system. This can involve partnering with community organizations, hosting events and workshops, and using media and social marketing campaigns to reach a wider audience.
5. Workforce Diversity: Strive to increase diversity within the healthcare workforce, particularly among native English speakers. This can involve implementing recruitment and retention strategies that promote cultural competency and linguistic diversity, as well as providing opportunities for professional development and advancement for underrepresented groups.
