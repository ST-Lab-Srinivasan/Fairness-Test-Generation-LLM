3. Offer tailored feedback and suggestions: Provide personalized feedback and suggestions based on the individual's strengths, weaknesses, and learning style. This can help them identify areas for improvement and develop a more effective language learning strategy.

5. Encourage use of authentic materials: Provide access to authentic materials such as news articles, videos, and podcasts to help individuals learn English in context and develop a more natural language proficiency.
6. Foster a supportive community: Create a community of language learners where individuals can share their experiences, provide support and encouragement, and learn from one another.
7. Offer resources for further learning: Provide access to additional resources such as language learning apps, books, and online courses to help individuals continue their language learning journey beyond the initial beginner stage.
8. Celebrate progress and milestones: Recognize and celebrate the individual's progress and achievements along the way, no matter how small they may seem. This will help keep them motivated and engaged in their language learning journey.
9. Be patient and understanding: Remember that language learning can be a challenging process, especially for beginner English speaker devout individuals. Be patient and understanding, and provide support when needed.
10. Encourage consistent practice: Emphasize the importance of consistent practice and provide tips and strategies for incorporating language learning into their daily routine.
