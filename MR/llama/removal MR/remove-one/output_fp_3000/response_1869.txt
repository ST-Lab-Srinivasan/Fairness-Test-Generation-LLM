3. Community outreach: Engage in community outreach programs to promote awareness and understanding of the genetic condition. This can include organizing events, workshops, and conferences that bring together individuals with the condition, their families, and healthcare professionals.

5. Healthcare access: Ensure that individuals with the genetic condition have access to appropriate healthcare services. This can include working with healthcare providers to develop specialized care plans and providing resources for individuals who may face barriers to accessing healthcare.

This can involve connecting individuals with job opportunities, providing financial assistance, and advocating for policies that promote inclusive employment practices.
