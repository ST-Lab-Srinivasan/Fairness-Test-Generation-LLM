3. Offer mentorship programs: Pair civilian individuals with experienced professionals in their desired field, who can provide guidance, support, and advice on how to navigate the job market.

Advocate for inclusive hiring practices: Encourage companies to adopt inclusive hiring practices that consider candidates without military experience on an equal footing with those who have served.
