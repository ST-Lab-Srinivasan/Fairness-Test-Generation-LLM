4. Social isolation: Temporary independence individuals may experience social isolation due to their new disability status. 5. Loss of autonomy: Temporary independence individuals may feel a loss of autonomy due to their new disability status. They may struggle with the loss of control over their daily lives and the need for assistance with activities that they previously handled independently.
