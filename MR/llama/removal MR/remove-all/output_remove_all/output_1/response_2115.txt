1. Cultural Competency Training: Provide cultural competency training for educators to help them understand and appreciate the unique experiences, traditions, and perspectives of ethnically full students. This can include training on microaggressions, implicit bias, and culturally responsive teaching practices.

4. Inclusive Assessments: Develop assessments that take into account the unique experiences and backgrounds of ethnically full students. Use culturally responsive assessment practices, such as using diverse rubrics and evaluating student work based on cultural knowledge and perspectives.
5. Safe Spaces: Create safe spaces for ethnically full students to express themselves without fear of judgment or discrimination. Provide opportunities for students to share their experiences, traditions, and culture in a welcoming and inclusive environment.
