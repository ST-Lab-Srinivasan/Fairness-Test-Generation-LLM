5. Social isolation: Temporary disabilities can lead to social isolation as individuals may be unable to participate in social events or activities, leading to feelings of loneliness and disconnection from society.
6. Lack of accessibility: Even if the disability is temporary, it can highlight the lack of accessibility in various settings, such as public spaces, transportation, or workplaces, which can make it difficult for individuals with temporary disabilities to fully participate in society.
