2. Support Groups: Facilitate the formation of support groups for individuals with the condition and their families. These groups can provide a platform for sharing experiences, emotional support, and mutual understanding.
3. Cultural Sensitivity Training: Provide cultural sensitivity training to healthcare providers and other professionals who interact with individuals with the condition. This can help ensure that they are aware of the individual's cultural background and can provide appropriate care and support.
