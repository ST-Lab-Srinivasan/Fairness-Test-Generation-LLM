As a beginner public speaker, you may face several challenges in the job market. Here are some of the most common ones:

1. Lack of experience and confidence: Many beginner speakers struggle with lack of experience and confidence when speaking in front of an audience. They may feel nervous, anxious, or unsure of themselves, which can affect their performance.
2. Limited exposure: As a beginner speaker, you may not have had many opportunities to speak in front of different audiences, which can limit your exposure and experience.
3. Difficulty articulating ideas: Beginner speakers may find it challenging to clearly articulate their ideas and thoughts, especially if they are not well-versed in public speaking techniques.
4. Fear of criticism: Many beginner speakers fear being criticized or judged by their audience, which can make them hesitant to speak up or share their ideas.
5. Limited access to resources: Beginner speakers may not have access to the same resources and training as experienced speakers, such as public speaking courses, workshops, or coaching.
6. Difficulty navigating different settings: Beginner speakers may find it challenging to navigate different types of settings, such as formal presentations, informal meetings, or networking events.
7. Limited network: Beginner speakers may not have an established network of contacts and connections in the speaking industry, which can make it harder to get booked for gigs or opportunities.
8. Difficulty marketing themselves: Beginner speakers may struggle to effectively market themselves and their speaking services to potential clients and audiences.
9. Limited visibility: Beginner speakers may not have a strong online presence or visibility, which can make it harder to attract attention and opportunities.
10. Difficulty differentiating themselves: With many beginner speakers in the market, it can be challenging to stand out and differentiate yourself from others.

By understanding these challenges, you can better prepare yourself for the job market as a beginner public speaker and take steps to overcome them.
