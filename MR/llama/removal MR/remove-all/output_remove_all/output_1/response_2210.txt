4. Increased healthcare costs: Genetic conditions can lead to increased healthcare costs due to the need for ongoing medical care, testing, and treatment. This can cause financial strain and stress.
5. Impact on family dynamics: Genetic conditions can impact not only the individual but also their family members. Siblings, parents, and other loved ones may feel the emotional burden of caring for someone with a genetic condition.
