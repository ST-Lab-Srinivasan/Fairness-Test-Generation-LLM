2. Listen to Marginalized Voices: Create safe spaces where marginalized voices can be heard without fear of retribution or judgment. This can be done through workshops, focus groups, or other forms of community engagement.
3. Use Intersectional Analysis: Analyze the impact of policies and decisions on different marginalized groups simultaneously, taking into account how various forms of oppression intersect.
