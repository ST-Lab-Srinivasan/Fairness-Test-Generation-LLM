5. Support community organizing: Empower recent immigrants to advocate for themselves by providing training and resources on how to navigate the US political system, advocate for their rights, and build coalitions with other communities.
6. Conduct research and data analysis: Collect and analyze data on the experiences and challenges faced by recent immigrants in the US. Use this information to inform policy changes, media campaigns, and community organizing efforts.
7. Advocate for policy changes: Work with policymakers and advocacy groups to push for legislation that protects and promotes the rights of recent immigrants.
