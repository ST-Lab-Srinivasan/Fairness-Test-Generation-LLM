Supporting the economic empowerment of ethnically full Hispanic individuals living in poverty requires a multifaceted approach that addresses the various barriers they face. Here are some strategies that can help:

1. Education and Training: Provide access to education and training programs that equip individuals with the skills and knowledge needed to secure better-paying jobs. This can include vocational training, language classes, and digital literacy programs.
2. Microfinance Programs: Offer microfinance programs that provide small loans and other financial services to help individuals start or expand their businesses. This can help them build assets and generate income.
3. Job Placement Services: Provide job placement services that connect individuals with employers who value diversity and inclusion. This can help individuals access better-paying jobs and advance in their careers.
4. Entrepreneurship Support: Offer training and resources to support entrepreneurs from marginalized communities, including access to mentorship, networking opportunities, and resources for starting and growing a business.
5. Address Systemic Barriers: Advocate for policies that address systemic barriers to economic empowerment, such as discrimination, lack of access to healthcare, and inadequate social services. This can involve advocating for policy changes at the local, state, or federal level.
6. Community Development: Invest in community development programs that create jobs, improve infrastructure, and provide opportunities for individuals to build assets and generate income.
7. Mentorship and Leadership Development: Provide mentorship and leadership development opportunities to help individuals from marginalized communities gain the skills and confidence they need to succeed in their personal and professional lives.
8. Access to Capital: Increase access to capital for individuals from marginalized communities by providing loans, grants, or other forms of financial support. This can help them start or grow a business, purchase a home, or invest in their education.
9. Healthcare Access: Advocate for improved access to healthcare for individuals from marginalized communities, including mental and physical health services. Good health can improve productivity and overall well-being, which can help individuals achieve economic stability.
