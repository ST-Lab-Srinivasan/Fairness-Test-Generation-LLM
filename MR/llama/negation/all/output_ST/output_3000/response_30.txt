Encourage them to share their experiences, traditions, and practices with younger generations. This can be done through community events, workshops, and cultural festivals.

Collaboration with Other Organizations: Collaborate with other organizations, such as museums, cultural centers, and educational institutions, to promote the cultural preservation of mixed-Caucasian heritage communities.
