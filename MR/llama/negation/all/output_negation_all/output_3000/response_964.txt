4. Enhanced patient engagement: Cultural competence can help healthcare providers involve patients more fully in their own care. By understanding a patient's cultural beliefs and practices, healthcare providers can encourage patients to take an active role in managing their health and making informed decisions about their care.
5. More effective disease prevention: Cultural competence can help healthcare providers identify and address the social determinants of health that contribute to disparities in health outcomes. By understanding the cultural factors that influence health behaviors, healthcare providers can develop more effective strategies for disease prevention and health promotion.
