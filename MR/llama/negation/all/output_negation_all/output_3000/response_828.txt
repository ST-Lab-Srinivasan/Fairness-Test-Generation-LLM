4. Facilitate social connections: Social isolation can exacerbate the emotional challenges of grieving. Encourage social connections by providing opportunities for recently widowed permanent residents to meet and connect with others who have experienced similar losses, such as support groups or social events.

6. Provide access to healthcare: Ensure that recently widowed permanent residents have access to quality healthcare, including primary care, specialist services, and mental health support.
7. Encourage intergenerational connections: Many recently widowed permanent residents may be raising children or grandchildren on their own.
