By understanding the cultural nuances of different communities, healthcare providers can tailor their services to meet the unique needs of these patients, improving access to quality care.
4. Better diagnosis and treatment: Cultural competence can lead to more accurate diagnoses and effective treatments. Healthcare providers who understand the cultural beliefs and practices of their patients are better equipped to identify and address cultural factors that may influence medical decision-making.
