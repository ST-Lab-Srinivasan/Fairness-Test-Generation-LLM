5. Technology Solutions: Utilize technology solutions, such as telemedicine and mobile health applications, to reach patients who may have difficulty accessing healthcare facilities due to language barriers or other factors.
6. Patient Navigators: Train patient navigators who are fluent in the patient's native language and can help them navigate the healthcare system, communicate with providers, and access necessary services.
7. Health Literacy Materials: Provide health literacy materials, such as pamphlets, videos, and interactive tools, in multiple languages to help patients better understand their health conditions and treatment options.
8. Community Health Workers: Train community health workers who are fluent in the patient's native language and can provide culturally appropriate support and outreach to vulnerable populations.
9. Telephonic Interpreter Services: Utilize telephonic interpreter services to provide language access for patients who may not be able to access in-person interpreters due to location or other factors.
