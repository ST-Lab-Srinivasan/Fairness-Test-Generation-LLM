2. Diversify hiring practices: Ensure that coaching staff, scouts, and front offices are diverse and inclusive. This can be achieved by actively seeking out candidates from underrepresented groups, providing mentorship opportunities, and creating a culture of inclusion.
3. Create mentorship programs: Pair aspiring athletes with successful mixed-race professionals in their sport. This can provide valuable guidance, support, and exposure to help them navigate the industry.
4. Address microaggressions: Educate coaches, teammates, and officials about the impact of microaggressions on athlete well-being and performance. Encourage an inclusive culture that fosters respectful communication and empathy.
