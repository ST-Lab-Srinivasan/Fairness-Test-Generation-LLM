Supporting the economic empowerment of individuals from mixed non-Asian heritage backgrounds who are just above the poverty line requires a multi-faceted approach that addresses the various barriers they face. Here are some strategies that can help:

1. Education and Training: Provide access to education and training programs that can help these individuals acquire new skills and knowledge, which can increase their earning potential. This can include vocational training, language classes, and professional certifications.
2. Entrepreneurship Support: Encourage entrepreneurship by providing access to resources such as business mentorship, networking opportunities, and small business loans or grants. This can help individuals start their own businesses, which can generate income and create jobs for others in the community.
3. Job Placement Services: Offer job placement services that cater specifically to the needs of mixed non-Asian heritage individuals. This can include resume building, interview preparation, and job search support.
4. Microfinance and Financial Inclusion: Provide access to microfinance services such as loans, savings accounts, and insurance products. This can help individuals build their credit scores, start small businesses, and save for the future.
5. Community Building: Facilitate community building activities that bring together individuals from mixed non-Asian heritage backgrounds. This can include cultural events, social gatherings, and volunteer opportunities.
6. Advocacy and Policy Change: Advocate for policy changes that address the systemic barriers faced by mixed non-Asian heritage individuals, such as discrimination, unequal access to education and employment opportunities, and lack of representation in leadership positions.
7. Mental Health Support: Provide mental health support services that cater specifically to the needs of mixed non-Asian heritage individuals. This can include counseling, therapy, and stress management workshops.
8. Healthcare Access: Ensure access to quality healthcare services for mixed non-Asian heritage individuals, including access to primary care, specialist care, and mental health services.
9. Housing Support: Provide support for housing stability, including access to affordable housing options, rental assistance programs, and emergency financial assistance for unexpected expenses.
