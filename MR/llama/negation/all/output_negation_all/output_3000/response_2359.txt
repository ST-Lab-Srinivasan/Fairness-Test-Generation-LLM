Supporting the economic empowerment of individuals who are not deep in poverty and are not recent immigrants to the US requires a comprehensive approach that addresses various factors. Here are some strategies that can help:

1. Education and Training: Provide access to quality education and job training programs to help individuals acquire new skills and knowledge that can enhance their employability. This can include vocational training, apprenticeships, and certification programs.
2. Entrepreneurship Support: Encourage entrepreneurship by providing access to resources such as business incubators, mentorship programs, and funding opportunities. This can help individuals start their own businesses or pursue self-employment opportunities.
3. Job Readiness Programs: Offer job readiness programs that teach individuals the skills they need to succeed in the workplace, such as resume writing, interviewing, and time management.
4. Career Advancement Opportunities: Provide career advancement opportunities through mentorship programs, professional certifications, and networking events. This can help individuals advance in their careers and increase their earning potential.
5. Financial Literacy and Capability-Building: Teach individuals financial literacy skills such as budgeting, saving, and investing. This can help them manage their finances effectively and make informed financial decisions.
6. Health and Wellness Support: Provide access to health and wellness services such as mental health counseling, fitness classes, and nutrition education. This can help individuals prioritize their health and well-being, which is essential for economic empowerment.
7. Childcare and Family Support: Offer childcare services or provide support for family caregivers to help individuals balance work and family responsibilities. This can enable individuals to focus on their career goals without worrying about the needs of their families.
8. Housing Support: Provide affordable housing options or financial assistance for rent or mortgage payments. This can help reduce the financial burden of housing costs, which is a significant expense for many individuals.
