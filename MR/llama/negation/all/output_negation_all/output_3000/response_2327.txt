6. Personal identity formation: Cultural diversity can shape an individual's personal identity and sense of belonging. Mixed African-non-American professionals may find that their unique cultural background gives them a competitive advantage in the workplace, as they are able to approach problems from multiple angles and connect with colleagues from diverse backgrounds.
