2. Cultural Education: Provide educational resources and workshops that focus on the cultural practices and values of the community. This can include traditional music, dance, art, and crafts. Encourage community members to share their knowledge and skills with younger generations.

7. Intergenerational Programming: Develop programs that bring together people of different ages to share knowledge and experiences. This can include mentorship programs, intergenerational storytelling circles, and cultural exchange events.
