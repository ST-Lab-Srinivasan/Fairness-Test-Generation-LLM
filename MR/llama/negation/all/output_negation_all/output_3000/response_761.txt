4. Patient-centered care: Train healthcare providers to prioritize patient-centered care by actively listening to patients' concerns and needs. Encourage open communication and respect for patients' cultural beliefs and practices.
5. Community outreach: Develop partnerships with local Arabic-speaking communities to raise awareness about healthcare services available to them. Provide educational resources in Arabic to help them navigate the healthcare system and access necessary care.
