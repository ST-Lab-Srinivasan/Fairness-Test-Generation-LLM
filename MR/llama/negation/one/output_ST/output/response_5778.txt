2. Bilingual education: Provide opportunities for Native students to learn in their native language, as well as in English. This can help them develop language proficiency and cultural competency.
3. Cultural exchange programs: Organize cultural exchange programs that bring Native students together with students from other backgrounds. These programs can promote cross-cultural understanding, empathy, and mutual respect.
