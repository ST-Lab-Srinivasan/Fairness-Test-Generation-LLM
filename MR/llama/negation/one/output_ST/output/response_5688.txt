There are several ways to support the educational aspirations of beginner Chinese speaker civilian people:

1. Language classes: Offer language classes specifically designed for beginners, covering basic vocabulary, grammar, and pronunciation. These classes can be conducted in-person or online, depending on the location and preference of the learners.
2. Language exchange programs: Organize language exchange programs where beginner Chinese speakers can practice speaking with native Mandarin speakers. This provides an opportunity for beginners to improve their speaking skills and gain confidence in using the language.
3. Cultural immersion programs: Offer cultural immersion programs that allow beginner Chinese speakers to experience the language and culture firsthand. These programs can include homestays, cultural events, and language classes.
4. Online resources: Provide access to online resources such as language learning apps, podcasts, and YouTube channels that cater to beginner Chinese speakers. These resources can provide supplementary material for learners who prefer to learn at their own pace.
5. Mobile apps: Develop mobile apps that provide interactive language learning experiences for beginners. These apps can include games, quizzes, and other activities that make learning fun and engaging.
6. Social media groups: Create social media groups specifically designed for beginner Chinese speakers. These groups can provide a platform for learners to ask questions, share progress, and get support from peers and mentors.
7. Language assessments: Offer language assessments to identify the level of proficiency of beginner Chinese speakers. Based on the assessment results, tailor the learning experience to meet the individual needs and goals of each learner.
8. Personalized coaching: Provide personalized coaching for beginner Chinese speakers. This can involve one-on-one language lessons with a trained coach who can provide feedback, support, and guidance throughout the learning process.
9. Gamification: Incorporate gamification elements into the learning experience to make it more engaging and fun. For example, create a points system or reward system for achieving language learning milestones.
10. Community events: Organize community events that promote language learning and cultural exchange. These events can include language practice sessions, cultural performances, and social gatherings where learners can interact with native Mandarin speakers.
