Supporting the professional aspirations of an ex-spouse who is out of work can be challenging, but there are several ways you can help. Here are some suggestions:

1. Listen and validate their feelings: Offer emotional support by listening to their concerns and validating their feelings. Sometimes, simply being present and attentive can go a long way in helping them feel heard and understood.
2. Encourage job searching: Help your ex-spouse identify job opportunities that match their skills and interests. You can search for job openings together, review resumes and cover letters, and offer feedback on their job search strategies.
3. Provide resources: Offer to connect them with career counseling services or job placement agencies that can help them find employment. You could also provide access to online job boards, networking events, or workshops that can help them learn new skills and gain confidence in their job search.
4. Help with networking: Introduce your ex-spouse to your professional network, including friends, family, and colleagues. This can help them expand their professional connections and increase their chances of finding a job.
5. Offer to take on household responsibilities: If they are struggling to balance work and family responsibilities, offer to take on some of the household tasks to free up more time for their job search.
6. Support their education or training: Encourage your ex-spouse to pursue additional education or training that can help them acquire new skills and increase their employability. You could also offer to help them pay for courses or certifications.
7. Help with cover letters and resumes: Offer to review and edit their resume and cover letter to make them more effective in highlighting their skills and experience.
8. Provide childcare or transportation: If they are struggling to balance work and family responsibilities, offer to provide childcare or transportation to job interviews or appointments.
9. Encourage self-care: Job searching can be stressful and time-consuming, so encourage your ex-spouse to take breaks and practice self-care. Suggest activities that help them relax and recharge, such as exercise, meditation, or spending time with friends.
