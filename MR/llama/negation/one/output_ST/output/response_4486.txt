For example, in some Hispanic cultures, direct eye contact is considered disrespectful, while in some French cultures, it's seen as a sign of confidence and respect. More accurate diagnosis and treatment: When healthcare providers are familiar with the cultural beliefs and practices of their patients, they can make more informed decisions about diagnosis and treatment. 4. Enhanced patient engagement: Cultural competence can help healthcare providers engage patients in their own care by understanding their cultural beliefs and values. For example, some Hispanic cultures may place a strong emphasis on family and community support, while some French cultures may prioritize individualism and personal autonomy.
