Creating more inclusive cultural programs for devout Muslim beginner English speakers requires a thoughtful and deliberate approach. Here are some suggestions to help make your cultural programs more welcoming and inclusive:

1. Provide translation support: Offer translation support during cultural events, such as live subtitles or translated brochures, to help non-English speakers participate fully.
2. Use inclusive language: Avoid using language that is exclusive or insensitive to Muslim cultures. Use inclusive language and terminology that acknowledges the diversity of Muslim communities.
3. Offer cultural sensitivity training: Provide training for staff and volunteers on cultural sensitivity and inclusivity, particularly when interacting with Muslim participants.
4. Be mindful of dress codes: Be aware of the dress codes and cultural norms of the Muslim community you are serving. Allow for appropriate attire during events and programs.
5. Incorporate diverse perspectives: Include diverse voices and perspectives in your programming, such as inviting speakers from different Muslim cultures or showcasing diverse forms of Islamic art and literature.
6. Foster a sense of belonging: Create a welcoming environment where participants feel a sense of belonging and inclusion. Encourage participation and engagement through interactive activities and discussions.
7. Be respectful of dietary restrictions: Be aware of the dietary restrictions of Muslim participants, such as halal food requirements, and make appropriate accommodations.
8. Provide accessible programming: Ensure that your programs are accessible to individuals with disabilities by providing accommodations such as wheelchair accessibility, sign language interpretation, or audio descriptions.
9. Build partnerships: Collaborate with Muslim organizations and community groups to develop programming that reflects the needs and interests of the Muslim community.
10. Evaluate and improve: Continuously evaluate your programs and make improvements based on feedback from participants. This will help ensure that your cultural programs are inclusive and meet the needs of the Muslim community.

By following these suggestions, you can create more inclusive cultural programs for devout Muslim beginner English speakers, fostering a sense of belonging and inclusivity within your organization.
