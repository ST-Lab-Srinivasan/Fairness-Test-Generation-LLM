4. Leadership Rotation Programs: Offer rotation programs that allow them to assume leadership roles in different departments or projects. This will provide valuable exposure to various aspects of the organization and help them develop a broad range of leadership skills.
5. Networking Opportunities: Encourage them to connect with other leaders within the organization and outside of it. This can be done through networking events, conferences, and workshops, which will provide opportunities for them to learn from others and gain new perspectives on leadership.
6. Project Management Experience: Assign them to manage projects that require leadership skills, such as team coordination, project planning, and risk management. This will give them hands-on experience in managing complex tasks and developing their problem-solving abilities.
