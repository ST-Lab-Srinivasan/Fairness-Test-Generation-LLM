5. Social Capital: Having a supportive family can help individuals build social capital, which refers to the networks and resources available to them through their family connections. 6. Reduced Stress: When individuals have a strong support system within their families, they may experience reduced stress levels compared to those without such support. This can help them navigate challenges more effectively and maintain their motivation and focus.
