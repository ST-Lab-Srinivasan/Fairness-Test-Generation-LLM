Creating more inclusive social policies for Nigerian devout Muslim individuals requires a deep understanding of their beliefs, practices, and cultural context. Here are some recommendations:

1. Consultation and collaboration: Engage in open and inclusive consultation processes with Muslim leaders, scholars, and community members to ensure that policies reflect their values and priorities. Collaborate with Muslim-led organizations to develop policies that are sensitive to their beliefs and practices.
2. Respect for religious diversity: Recognize the diversity of Islamic beliefs and practices in Nigeria and avoid imposing a single interpretation or practice on all Muslims. Encourage inclusivity by promoting interfaith dialogue and understanding between different Islamic groups and other religions.
3. Protection of religious freedom: Ensure that Muslim individuals have the right to practice their religion freely, without fear of discrimination or persecution. Protect their right to dress according to their cultural and religious beliefs, observe their dietary restrictions, and participate in religious practices without interference.
4. Education and awareness: Provide education and raise awareness about the diversity of Islamic beliefs and practices in Nigeria. This can help reduce misconceptions and stereotypes that may lead to discrimination or marginalization of Muslim individuals.
5. Inclusive economic policies: Develop economic policies that promote inclusivity and address the socio-economic disparities faced by Muslim communities, such as access to credit, entrepreneurship opportunities, and job creation.
6. Support for religious institutions: Provide support for Islamic educational institutions, mosques, and other religious infrastructure to ensure their sustainability and continued relevance in Nigerian society.
7. Addressing gender inequality: Work towards addressing gender inequality within Muslim communities by promoting gender-sensitive policies and programs that empower women and girls, and promote their full participation in all aspects of society.
8. Promoting interfaith dialogue: Encourage interfaith dialogue between Muslims and other religious groups to foster mutual understanding, respect, and cooperation. This can help build bridges between different religious communities and promote a culture of peaceful coexistence.
