2. Inclusive housing: Provide affordable and accessible housing options for people with disabilities, including adapted apartments and houses. This will enable them to live independently and participate fully in community life.
3. Transportation: Ensure that transportation is available and accessible to people with disabilities. This can include wheelchair-accessible vehicles, taxis, or public transportation services.
4. Employment: Provide job opportunities for people with disabilities by working with employers to create inclusive workplaces and providing job training and placement services. This will enable them to contribute to the community economically.
5. Education: Ensure that education is accessible to people with disabilities by providing accommodations such as sign language interpretation, braille materials, and assistive technology. This will enable them to reach their full potential academically and socially.
6. Healthcare: Provide quality healthcare services that are tailored to the specific needs of people with disabilities. This can include specialized medical equipment, adaptive devices, and home care services.
