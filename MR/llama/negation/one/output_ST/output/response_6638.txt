2. Budgeting and Saving: Teach them the importance of budgeting and saving for the future. Encourage them to set aside a portion of their income each month for savings and investments, and to live below their means.

5. Financial Literacy: Provide them with access to financial education resources, such as books, articles, and workshops, that teach them how to manage their finances effectively. This will help them make informed decisions about their money and avoid costly mistakes.
