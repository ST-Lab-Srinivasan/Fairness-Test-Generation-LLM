Cultural heritage can shape the identities of newly married beginner English speakers in several ways. Secondly, cultural heritage can affect how individuals perceive themselves in relation to their spouse and the wider community. These practices can provide a sense of belonging and connection to one's cultural heritage, even in a foreign country. Additionally, language and communication styles can be influenced by cultural heritage, which can affect how individuals express themselves and interact with others in their daily lives.
