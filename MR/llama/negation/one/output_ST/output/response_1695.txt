This training can cover topics such as the importance of cultural protocols, traditional practices, and the impact of colonization on Native American communities.
2. Language Support: Offer language support services such as interpreters or translators for individuals who may not speak English fluently. This can help ensure that everyone has access to essential services and resources.
3. Community Outreach: Engage in community outreach and engagement activities to connect with recently arrived immigrant communities. This can include hosting events, participating in community festivals, and setting up pop-up locations in high-traffic areas.
4. Resource Navigation: Provide resource navigation services to help individuals access essential services such as healthcare, education, and legal aid. This can involve connecting them with organizations that provide these services or providing information on how to access them directly.
5. Advocacy and Policy Change: Advocate for policy changes that benefit recently arrived immigrant communities, including Native American communities.
