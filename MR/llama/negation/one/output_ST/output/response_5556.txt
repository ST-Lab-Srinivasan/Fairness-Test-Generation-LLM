Introduction:

The intersectionality of their racial and ethnic identities can affect how they experience and navigate the healthcare system, as well as their overall quality of life. This paper will explore the impacts of genetic conditions on the lives of mixed Hispanic heritage elderly male people, highlighting the social determinants of health that contribute to these experiences.
