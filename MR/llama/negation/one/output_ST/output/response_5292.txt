8. Corruption: Corruption is a significant challenge in many Arab countries, and it can affect the fairness and impartiality of the legal system.
9. Lack of transparency: In some Arab countries, there may be a lack of transparency in the legal system, particularly when it comes to court proceedings or decision-making processes.
10. Limited access to alternative dispute resolution mechanisms: Many Arab countries have limited access to alternative dispute resolution mechanisms such as mediation or arbitration, which can make it difficult for individuals to resolve disputes outside of the courts.
