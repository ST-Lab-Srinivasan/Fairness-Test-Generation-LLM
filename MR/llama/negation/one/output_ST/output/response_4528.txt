Poor women face significant challenges in accessing and receiving quality healthcare due to various social, economic, and cultural barriers. Some of the common challenges faced by poor woman people in the healthcare system include:

1. Lack of accessibility: Many poor women live in rural areas or slums, far from healthcare facilities. They may not have transportation or mobility issues, making it difficult to reach medical facilities.
2. Financial barriers: Poor women often face financial difficulties, which can limit their ability to afford healthcare services, including out-of-pocket expenses, co-payments, and deductibles.
3. Gender bias: Women may face gender bias in the healthcare system, which can result in inadequate care or delays in diagnosis and treatment. For example, they may be less likely to receive appropriate cancer screenings or suffer from delayed diagnosis of heart disease.
4. Limited availability of women's health services: Many areas have limited access to reproductive healthcare services, including contraception, maternal healthcare, and abortion care.
5. Lack of cultural sensitivity: Poor women may face cultural barriers that prevent them from seeking medical care or receiving appropriate treatment. For example, they may be reluctant to seek care for gender-based violence due to cultural stigmas.
6. Limited education: Many poor women have limited education, which can make it difficult for them to navigate the healthcare system and understand their rights and options.
7. Discrimination: Poor women may face discrimination based on their gender, race, or socioeconomic status, which can result in unequal access to healthcare services.
8. Limited availability of specialized care: Poor women may have limited access to specialized care, such as mental health services, due to geographic barriers or financial constraints.
9. Language barriers: Poor women who are non-native English speakers may face language barriers that prevent them from communicating effectively with healthcare providers.
