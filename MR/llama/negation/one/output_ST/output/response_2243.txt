2. Patient-centered care: Implementing patient-centered care practices can help ensure that middle-class Chinese patients receive personalized care that meets their unique needs. 4. Language support: Providing language support for middle-class Chinese patients can help ensure that they receive accurate diagnoses and appropriate treatment. This includes providing interpreters or translators during medical appointments and using technology such as video conferencing for remote consultations.
5. Traditional Chinese medicine integration: Integrating traditional Chinese medicine (TCM) practices with Western medicine can provide middle-class Chinese patients with a more comprehensive approach to healthcare.
