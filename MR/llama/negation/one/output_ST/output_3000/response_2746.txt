2. Entrepreneurship Support: Encourage and support entrepreneurship among Native Americans by providing access to capital, mentorship, and resources for starting and growing their own businesses. 3. Sustainable Economic Development: Foster sustainable economic development in Native American communities by supporting local industries, such as agriculture, forestry, and tourism. Education and Leadership Development: Invest in education and leadership development programs for Native Americans to build a skilled workforce and empower individuals to take on leadership roles within their communities.
