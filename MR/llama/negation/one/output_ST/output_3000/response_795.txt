Volunteering provides an opportunity for these individuals to pass on their skills and expertise to younger generations, helping to preserve cultural heritage and tradition.

5. Health Benefits: Research has shown that volunteering can have a positive impact on physical health, including lower blood pressure, reduced stress levels, and improved immune function. This can be particularly beneficial for mid-50s independent communities who may be experiencing declining physical health.
