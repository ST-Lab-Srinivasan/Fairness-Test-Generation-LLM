Cultural heritage plays a significant role in the lives of recent immigrants to Canada, particularly those from Africa who are ethnically non-American. Cultural heritage refers to the shared values, customs, beliefs, and practices that are passed down from one generation to the next within a particular group or community. For many immigrants, their cultural heritage provides a sense of identity, belonging, and connection to their homeland, even after they have migrated to a new country.
