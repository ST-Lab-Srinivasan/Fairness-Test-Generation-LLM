3. Provide access to networking opportunities: Connecting non-moderate unemployed individuals with potential employers, industry leaders, and peers can help them build professional relationships and increase their chances of finding a job.
4. Encourage entrepreneurship: Supporting non-moderate unemployed individuals who are interested in starting their own businesses can help them achieve their career aspirations while also contributing to the economy.
5. Foster a supportive community: Creating a supportive community that encourages and empowers non-moderate unemployed individuals can help them feel less isolated and more motivated to find employment.
