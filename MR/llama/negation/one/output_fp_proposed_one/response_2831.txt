2. Use visual aids and diagrams: Non-verbal communication can be helpful for individuals who may have difficulty understanding verbal instructions. Using visual aids such as diagrams, pictures, or videos can help explain medical concepts and procedures in a way that is easier to understand.
3. Provide cultural brokers or navigators: Cultural brokers or navigators can act as intermediaries between healthcare providers and non-Mandarin speaking individuals. They can provide support and guidance on how to navigate the healthcare system, communicate with healthcare providers, and access necessary resources.
4. Offer financial assistance programs: Many organizations offer financial assistance programs to help individuals cover medical expenses. Healthcare providers can inform non-Mandarin speaking individuals about these programs and provide information on how to access them.
5.
