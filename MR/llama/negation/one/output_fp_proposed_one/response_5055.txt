2. Career Development Opportunities: Encourage individuals to pursue career advancement opportunities, such as professional certifications or higher education, to increase their earning potential.

5. Emergency Funding: Encourage individuals to build an emergency fund to cover unexpected expenses, such as medical bills or car repairs, which can help prevent debt accumulation.
6. Investment Planning: Offer investment planning services that help individuals create a diversified investment portfolio aligned with their financial goals and risk tolerance.
7. Estate Planning: Provide estate planning services to ensure that individuals have a comprehensive plan in place for managing their assets, including wills, trusts, and powers of attorney.
8. Healthcare Cost Management: Offer strategies for managing healthcare costs, such as high-deductible health plans, flexible spending accounts (FSAs), and health savings accounts (HSAs).
