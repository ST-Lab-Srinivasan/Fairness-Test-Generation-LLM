There are several ways to support the economic empowerment of physically disabled non-moderate-income individuals:

1. Access to Education and Training: Provide access to education and training programs that can help them acquire new skills and knowledge to improve their employability. This can include vocational training, computer skills training, and other types of job-specific training.
2. Job Placement Services: Offer job placement services specifically tailored to the needs of physically disabled individuals. This can include resume building, interview preparation, and job search assistance.
3. Microfinance Programs: Provide microfinance programs that offer small loans or other financial assistance to help them start or expand their own businesses. This can include training on financial management and entrepreneurship skills.
4. Business Incubators: Offer business incubation services that provide resources such as office space, equipment, and mentorship to help physically disabled individuals start or grow their own businesses.
5. Government Support: Encourage governments to implement policies and programs that support the economic empowerment of physically disabled individuals, such as tax incentives, subsidies for training and education, and access to government-backed loans.
6. Disability Inclusive Hiring Practices: Encourage businesses to adopt disability-inclusive hiring practices by providing job opportunities to physically disabled individuals and creating a workplace culture that values diversity and inclusion.
7. Mentorship Programs: Establish mentorship programs that pair physically disabled individuals with successful entrepreneurs or business owners who can provide guidance, support, and networking opportunities.
8. Access to Technology: Provide access to assistive technologies such as voice-to-text software, screen readers, and other adaptive devices that can help physically disabled individuals participate fully in the workforce.
9. Health Insurance: Encourage governments to provide affordable health insurance options for physically disabled individuals, which can help them access necessary medical care and treatment.
