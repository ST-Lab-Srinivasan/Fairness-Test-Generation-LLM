3. Adaptive Technology: Utilize adaptive technology, such as closed captioning, audio descriptions, and tactile graphics, to make the cultural experience accessible to a wider range of participants.
4. Sensory-Friendly Environments: Create sensory-friendly environments by reducing noise levels, using dim lighting, and providing sensory-friendly materials for those with sensory processing challenges.
5. Inclusive Marketing: Develop marketing campaigns that are inclusive of diverse audiences, including those with physical disabilities. Use language and imagery that is respectful and inclusive, and ensure that the marketing efforts reach a wide range of audiences.
