4. Patient Navigation: Providing patient navigation services can help non-French speaking patients navigate the healthcare system and access the care they need. This includes providing personalized support and guidance throughout their healthcare journey.
5. Community Outreach: Engaging in community outreach and education can help raise awareness about the importance of healthcare among non-French speaking communities.
