1. Education and Training: Provide access to quality education and training programs that can help individuals acquire new skills and knowledge to improve their job prospects. 3. Job Placement and Career Advancement: Collaborate with companies to offer job placement and career advancement opportunities to underrepresented groups. This could include internships, mentorship programs, or targeted hiring initiatives.
4. Wealth-Building Strategies: Offer financial education and wealth-building strategies such as homeownership, retirement savings plans, or small business ownership. These can help individuals build intergenerational wealth and achieve financial stability.
