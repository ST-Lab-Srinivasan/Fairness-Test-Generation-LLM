2. Language Support: Provide interpretation services, such as American Sign Language (ASL) or written translations, to help non-Arabic speaker widows participate in the program. This can be done through a combination of volunteers and professional interpreters.

4. Transportation: Provide transportation options, such as bus tickets or ride-sharing services, to help individuals get to and from the program location.
5. Food and Beverage Accommodations: Offer vegetarian or halal food options for individuals who have specific dietary needs or restrictions. Additionally, provide water and other beverages that are accessible to all participants.

8. Building Community: Encourage participants to connect with each other through icebreaker activities or small group discussions. This can help build a sense of community and reduce feelings of isolation.
