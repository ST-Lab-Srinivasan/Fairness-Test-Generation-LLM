3. Foster inclusive communities: Creating spaces where recent immigrants feel welcome and included can help build political engagement. This can involve hosting community events, providing cultural sensitivity training for local leaders, and promoting diversity and inclusion in all aspects of civic life.

Empower through education: Educating recent immigrants about their rights and responsibilities as citizens can help them feel more confident in their ability to participate in the political process.
