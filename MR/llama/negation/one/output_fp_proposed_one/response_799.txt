4. Limited Healthcare Knowledge: Non-citizens may have limited knowledge about the Indian healthcare system, its services, and available treatments. This lack of awareness can lead to delays in seeking medical attention or misusing healthcare resources.
5. Discrimination: Unfortunately, some healthcare providers may discriminate against non-citizens, either due to their immigration status or cultural differences. This can result in inadequate care or poor treatment experiences.
