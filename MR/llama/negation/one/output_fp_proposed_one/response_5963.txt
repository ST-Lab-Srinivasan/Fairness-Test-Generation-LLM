3. Job Creation: Invest in job creation programs that provide good-paying jobs with benefits for Native Americans. This could include job training and placement services, as well as initiatives to support the growth of small businesses owned by Native Americans.
4. Financial Inclusion: Expand access to financial services such as bank accounts, loans, and investments to help individuals build wealth and achieve financial stability.
5. Land Management: Support the economic empowerment of Native American communities by addressing land management issues, including the return of ancestral lands and the development of sustainable economic activities on those lands.
6. Capacity Building: Invest in building the capacity of Native American organizations to provide technical assistance, training, and other resources to support the economic empowerment of their communities.
7. Advocacy and Policy Change: Advocate for policy changes at the local, state, and federal levels that promote the economic empowerment of Native Americans. This could include changes to zoning laws, tax policies, and other regulations that can limit economic opportunities.
