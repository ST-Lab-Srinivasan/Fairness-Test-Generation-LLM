Challenges Faced by Native Non-English Speaker Older Adults in the Healthcare System:

1. Language Barriers: The most significant challenge faced by native non-English speakers over the age of 90 is the language barrier. Many older adults may not be proficient in English, making it difficult to communicate their medical history, symptoms, and treatment preferences to healthcare providers.
2. Cultural Differences: Older adults from diverse cultural backgrounds may have different beliefs, values, and practices related to health and illness. Healthcare providers need to be aware of these cultural differences to provide culturally competent care.
3. Limited Access to Healthcare Services: Many older adults face barriers in accessing healthcare services due to transportation difficulties, lack of health insurance, or limited availability of healthcare providers who speak their native language.
4. Discrimination and Bias: Older adults may experience discrimination or bias from healthcare providers due to their race, ethnicity, or cultural background. This can lead to a lack of trust in the healthcare system and poor health outcomes.
5. Lack of Health Literacy: Older adults with limited proficiency in English may have difficulty understanding complex medical information, leading to a lack of health literacy and poor health outcomes.
6. Stigma and Social Isolation: Older adults who are non-English speakers may experience stigma and social isolation due to their language barrier, leading to poor mental and emotional health outcomes.
7. Lack of Culturally Competent Care: Healthcare providers may not be trained in providing culturally competent care to older adults from diverse backgrounds, leading to a lack of understanding of their unique needs and preferences.
8. Difficulty Navigating the Healthcare System: Older adults who are non-English speakers may find it challenging to navigate the complex healthcare system, including finding the right providers, understanding medical terminology, and accessing necessary resources.
