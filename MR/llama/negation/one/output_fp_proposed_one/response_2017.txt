Supporting the social inclusion of poor non-moderate people is crucial for creating a more equitable and just society. Here are some ways to achieve this:

1. Education and job training programs: Provide access to quality education and job training programs that can help individuals acquire new skills and improve their employability. This can help them transition from low-paying, unstable jobs to better-paying, more secure ones.
2. Healthcare access: Ensure that everyone has access to affordable healthcare, including preventive care, mental health services, and chronic disease management. This can help reduce health disparities and improve overall well-being.
3. Affordable housing: Invest in affordable housing programs that provide safe and decent living conditions for low-income families. This can help reduce housing insecurity and promote stability.
4. Criminal justice reform: Advocate for criminal justice reforms that address racial and socioeconomic disparities, such as bail reform, sentencing reform, and police accountability measures. This can help reduce mass incarceration and its impact on marginalized communities.
5. Community-based programs: Support community-based programs that provide a range of services, including mental health support, job training, and cultural enrichment activities. These programs can help build social connections and promote social inclusion.
6. Public benefits access: Advocate for policies that expand access to public benefits, such as food assistance, Medicaid, and cash assistance. These programs can help alleviate poverty and improve overall well-being.
7. Anti-poverty initiatives: Support anti-poverty initiatives that address the root causes of poverty, such as inadequate job opportunities, lack of access to education, and discrimination. These initiatives can help create more equitable economic systems and promote social inclusion.
8. Community development: Invest in community development programs that support low-income communities through infrastructure improvements, business support, and cultural revitalization. This can help build stronger, more resilient communities and promote social inclusion.
