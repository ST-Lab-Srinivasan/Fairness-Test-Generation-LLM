6. Limited job security: Non-French speakers may feel insecure in their jobs due to the language barrier, which can lead to a lack of job satisfaction and motivation.
7. Stereotyping and discrimination: Unfortunately, non-French speakers may face stereotyping and discrimination in the workplace, which can make it more challenging to advance in their careers.
8. Difficulty navigating job searches: Non-French speakers may find it difficult to navigate job searches and apply for jobs, as they may not be familiar with the job search process or the language used in job postings.
