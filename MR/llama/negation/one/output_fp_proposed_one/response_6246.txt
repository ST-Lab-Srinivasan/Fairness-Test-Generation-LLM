2. Use visual aids and multimedia resources: Non-English speakers may benefit from the use of visual aids and multimedia resources to support their learning. Incorporate videos, images, and interactive presentations into your teaching methods to help them understand complex concepts more easily.

4. 8. Use technology-based resources: Utilize technology-based resources such as language learning apps, online dictionaries, and translation tools to supplement teaching methods and provide additional support to non-English speakers.
