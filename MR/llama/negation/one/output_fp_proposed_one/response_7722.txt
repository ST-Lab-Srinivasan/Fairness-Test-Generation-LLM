Supporting deep in poverty physically disabled communities requires a comprehensive and inclusive approach that addresses the various challenges faced by these communities. Here are some effective strategies that can be employed:

1. Capacity Building: Provide training and capacity building programs for community members, particularly those with disabilities, on various skills such as entrepreneurship, leadership, and advocacy. This will help build their confidence and self-esteem, and empower them to take charge of their lives.
2. Accessibility: Ensure that the community infrastructure is accessible and inclusive for people with disabilities. This includes providing ramps, elevators, and accessible restrooms in public spaces such as schools, healthcare facilities, and government buildings.
3. Inclusive Education: Implement inclusive education programs that cater to the needs of students with disabilities. This includes providing Braille and sign language materials, audiobooks, and assistive technology devices such as screen readers and speech-to-text software.
4. Employment Opportunities: Provide job training and placement services for people with disabilities. This can include vocational training, job coaching, and mentorship programs to help them find employment in mainstream companies or start their own businesses.
5. Healthcare Services: Ensure that healthcare facilities are accessible and provide quality medical care for people with disabilities. This includes providing sign language interpreters, braille materials, and assistive technology devices such as wheelchairs and walkers.
6. Community Outreach: Organize community outreach programs that promote awareness and understanding of disability issues. This can include cultural events, workshops, and conferences that bring together people with disabilities, their families, and the broader community.
7. Advocacy and Policy Change: Work with local governments and advocacy groups to push for policy changes that promote the rights and inclusion of people with disabilities. This can include advocating for accessibility standards in building codes, transportation infrastructure, and government services.
8. Inclusive Housing: Ensure that housing options are inclusive and accessible for people with disabilities. This includes providing affordable and accessible housing units, as well as home modifications such as wheelchair ramps, widened doorways, and grab bars.
