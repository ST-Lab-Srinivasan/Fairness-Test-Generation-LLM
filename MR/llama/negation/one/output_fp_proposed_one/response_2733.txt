This can include vocational training, entrepreneurship training, and digital literacy programs.

4. Addressing Systemic Inequality: Economic empowerment cannot be achieved without addressing the systemic inequalities that exist in society. Efforts should be made to address discrimination, inequality, and lack of access to opportunities in education, employment, and business ownership.
