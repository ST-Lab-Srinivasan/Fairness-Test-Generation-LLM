Creating more inclusive cultural celebrations for native non-Spanish speaker, non-able-bodied individuals requires careful consideration and planning. Here are some suggestions:

1. Accessibility: Ensure that the celebration venue is accessible to all, including wheelchair users and those with mobility impairments. Provide clear signage, wheelchair ramps, and accessible restrooms.
2. Communication: Provide multiple ways for people to access information about the celebration, such as translated materials, audio descriptions, or braille documents. This will ensure that everyone has equal access to information.
3. Performers: Select performers who are representative of the community and able to accommodate different abilities. Consider hiring deaf or disabled performers to provide a more inclusive experience.
4. Activities: Offer a variety of activities that cater to different abilities, such as adaptive sports, art classes, or musical performances. This will allow everyone to participate and enjoy the celebration.
5. Inclusive Language: Use inclusive language in all promotional materials, avoiding language that may be offensive or marginalizing to any group.
6. Sensory-Friendly Environment: Create a sensory-friendly environment by minimizing loud noises, bright lights, and strong smells. This will help individuals with sensory processing disorders to feel more comfortable and included.
7. Accommodations for Different Religious Practices: Be sensitive to the religious practices of all attendees. Provide accommodations such as quiet spaces for prayer or meditation, or adjusted schedules for those who observe different religious practices.
8. Collaboration with Disability Organizations: Partner with local disability organizations to ensure that the celebration is accessible and inclusive for everyone. These organizations can provide valuable input and resources to make the event more accessible.
9. Inclusive Food Options: Offer a variety of food options that cater to different dietary needs, such as gluten-free, vegetarian, or halal. This will ensure that everyone has access to food that meets their dietary requirements.
