Addressing the specific needs of beginner non-Arabic speakers in healthcare requires a thoughtful and inclusive approach. Here are some strategies that can help:

1. Provide language support: Offer language classes or provide access to interpretation services, such as video remote interpreting (VRI) or on-site interpretation, to assist beginner non-Arabic speakers in communicating with healthcare providers.
2. Use visual aids: Utilize pictures, diagrams, and other visual aids to convey information and instructions to beginner non-Arabic speakers. This can be particularly helpful for individuals who have difficulty understanding verbal instructions.
3. Simplify communication: Speak slowly and clearly, avoiding complex medical terminology and jargon that may be difficult for beginner non-Arabic speakers to understand. Use plain language and provide explanations in a way that is easy to follow.
4. Offer cultural sensitivity training: Provide cultural sensitivity training for healthcare providers to help them understand the beliefs, values, and practices of beginner non-Arabic speaking individuals. This can help providers to better communicate with and provide appropriate care to these patients.
5. Use technology: Utilize technology such as telemedicine or mobile apps that provide instant translation services to facilitate communication between healthcare providers and beginner non-Arabic speakers.
6. Hire interpreters: Hire professional interpreters who are fluent in both Arabic and the local language of the area to assist with communication during medical appointments and procedures.
7. Develop patient-centered materials: Create patient-centered materials, such as pamphlets or videos, that provide information on health conditions, treatments, and self-care strategies in a way that is accessible and understandable for beginner non-Arabic speakers.
8. Offer language support during appointments: Provide language support during medical appointments by offering interpreters or using technology to facilitate communication.
