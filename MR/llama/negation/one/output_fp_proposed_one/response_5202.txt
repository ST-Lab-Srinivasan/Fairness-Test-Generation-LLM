3. Public Health Programs: Implement programs that focus on preventive care and early intervention to address health disparities and promote overall well-being. Examples include disease screening, vaccination programs, and mental health services.
4. Community Outreach: Engage with local communities to raise awareness about the importance of healthcare for these individuals. Collaborate with community organizations, religious groups, or social clubs to promote outreach efforts and encourage participation.
