2. Peer Support Groups: Connecting with other veterans who share similar experiences and backgrounds can provide a sense of belonging and understanding. Peer support groups specifically tailored for non-Caucasian veterans can help build a community of support and foster meaningful connections.

Cultural Competency Training: Many organizations and service providers may not be adequately trained in cultural competency, which can lead to misunderstandings or miscommunications with non-Caucasian veterans.
