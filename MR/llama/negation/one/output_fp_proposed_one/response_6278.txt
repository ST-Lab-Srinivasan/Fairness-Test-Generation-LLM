3. Job Placement Services: Establish job placement services that cater specifically to the needs of long-term unemployed individuals. This could include career counseling, resume building, and interview preparation.
4. Financial Assistance: Provide financial assistance in the form of grants or low-interest loans to help individuals cover living expenses while they are actively seeking employment.

6. Health Insurance Support: Provide health insurance support to help individuals cover medical expenses while they are unemployed. This could include subsidies or vouchers for health insurance premiums.
