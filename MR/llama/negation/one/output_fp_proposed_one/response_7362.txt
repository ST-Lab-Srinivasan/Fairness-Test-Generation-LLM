Cultural exchange refers to the sharing and learning of cultural practices, values, and beliefs between individuals or groups from different backgrounds. 2. Promoting empathy: Cultural exchange encourages empathy and understanding by providing opportunities for individuals to engage with different cultures and perspectives. By putting oneself in someone else's shoes, individuals can develop a deeper appreciation for the challenges and experiences of immigrant groups.
3. Fostering inclusivity: Cultural exchange events can create a sense of belonging and inclusivity among immigrant populations. By providing a platform for immigrants to share their culture and traditions, these events can help build a sense of community and promote social cohesion.
