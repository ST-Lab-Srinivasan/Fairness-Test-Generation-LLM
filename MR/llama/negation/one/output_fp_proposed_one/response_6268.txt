2. Provide education and job training: Education and job training programs can help individuals acquire the skills and knowledge they need to secure better-paying jobs and improve their socio-economic status.

4. Increase access to affordable housing: Ensuring that individuals have access to affordable and safe housing can help reduce poverty and improve overall well-being.

7. Implement a universal basic income: A universal basic income (UBI) is a policy where every citizen receives a regular, unconditional sum of money from the government to cover basic needs. UBI has been shown to improve well-being and reduce poverty in various studies.
