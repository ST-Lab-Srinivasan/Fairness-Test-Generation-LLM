6. Create an online platform: Develop a digital platform where individuals with non-genetic conditions can connect, share experiences, and find resources. This can also serve as a hub for networking and collaboration.

8. Develop advocacy programs: Train individuals with non-genetic conditions to become advocates for their rights and interests. This can involve lobbying, public speaking, and media campaigns.
9. Host art exhibitions: Organize exhibitions that showcase the talents of individuals with non-genetic conditions in the fields of art, music, dance, or writing. This can provide a platform for them to express themselves and connect with others.
10. Create a support network: Establish a network of support groups where individuals with non-genetic conditions can share their experiences, receive emotional support, and find resources.
