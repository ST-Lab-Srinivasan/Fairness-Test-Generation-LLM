2. Training and workshops: Offer training sessions on various aspects of leadership, such as communication, problem-solving, decision-making, and strategic planning. These programs can help them build their knowledge and skills in leadership.
3. Networking opportunities: Provide opportunities for them to connect with other leaders and professionals in their industry or field. This will help them build relationships, learn from others, and gain valuable insights into different leadership styles and approaches.

5. Practical experience: Offer opportunities for them to practice their leadership skills in real-world situations. This could involve leading small projects or teams, participating in volunteer work, or taking on leadership roles in extracurricular activities.
