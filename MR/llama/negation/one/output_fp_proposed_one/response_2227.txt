For example, advocating for flexible work schedules or remote work options can help employees participate in community activities without sacrificing their employment.
7. Skills development: Offer skills development programs tailored to the needs of lower-income passport holders. This can include digital literacy training, job readiness programs, or entrepreneurship training.
8. Mentorship and coaching: Provide mentorship and coaching opportunities to help lower-income passport holders navigate community engagement opportunities and build their confidence and skills.
