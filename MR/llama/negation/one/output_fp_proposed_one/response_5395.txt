By donating their time and skills, volunteers can help address the basic needs of community members who may be struggling financially or with mobility issues.
4. Education and Personal Development: Volunteering can provide opportunities for personal growth and skill development, particularly in non-independent wealthy communities where access to education and resources may be more limited.
