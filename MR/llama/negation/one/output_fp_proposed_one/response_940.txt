2. Accessibility: Ensure that public spaces, buildings, and infrastructure are accessible to people with disabilities. This includes making ramps, elevators, and other facilities available for their use.
3. Employment Opportunities: Encourage businesses to hire people with disabilities and provide them with equal opportunities for employment. Offer training and support to help them develop the necessary skills and knowledge.

5. Healthcare Access: Advocate for improved access to healthcare services for people with disabilities, including accessible medical facilities, trained professionals, and appropriate treatment options.
6. Social Support Networks: Create social support networks for people with disabilities, where they can connect with others who share similar experiences and challenges. This can help build their confidence and sense of belonging.
