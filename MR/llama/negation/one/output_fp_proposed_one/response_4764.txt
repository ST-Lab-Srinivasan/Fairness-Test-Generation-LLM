Not Non High-Income Professional Mixed Asian Heritage People in the Healthcare System: Challenges and Barriers

Introduction

Mixed Asian heritage individuals, including those who are not from a high-income background, face unique challenges in the healthcare system. These challenges can affect their access to quality care, cultural competency, and overall health outcomes. This paper will explore the specific challenges faced by not non high-income professional mixed Asian heritage people in the healthcare system.

Healthcare providers may lack cultural competency when caring for mixed Asian heritage individuals. This can result in inappropriate or insensitive treatment, leading to feelings of isolation or marginalization.

4. Lack of Representation:

5. Stereotyping and Bias:
Not non high-income professional mixed Asian heritage people may face stereotyping and bias from healthcare providers, which can lead to inadequate care and poor health outcomes.
