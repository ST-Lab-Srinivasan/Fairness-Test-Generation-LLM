5. Promoting tolerance: Cultural exchange programs promote tolerance and acceptance by encouraging participants to embrace diversity and celebrate cultural differences. This helps create a more inclusive and harmonious environment between the two groups.
6. Fostering empathy: Cultural exchange programs provide an opportunity for participants to step into each other's shoes and experience life from another person's perspective. This fosters empathy and understanding between the two groups.
