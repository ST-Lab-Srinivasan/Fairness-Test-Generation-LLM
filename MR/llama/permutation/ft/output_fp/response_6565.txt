2. Offer language support: Provide interpreters or bilingual volunteers to help beginner Chinese speakers communicate and participate in the event. This can include both visual and audio interpretations.

4. Offer childcare and family-friendly activities: Many low-income families may struggle to find childcare or transportation for their children. Providing childcare and family-friendly activities can help make the event more inclusive and accessible.
5. Promote the event through community outreach: Reach out to local community organizations, schools, and other groups to promote the event. Consider working with local media outlets or social service agencies to help spread the word.
6. Provide free food and drinks: Offering free food and drinks can help make the event more inclusive and accessible to those who may not be able to afford it. Consider partnering with local food banks or other organizations to provide free meals.
