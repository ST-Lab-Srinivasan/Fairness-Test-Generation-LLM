Title: The Significance of Cultural Heritage in Shaping the Identities of Lifelong Single Mandarin Individuals

Introduction:

In China, where cultural traditions are deeply rooted, the concept of "guonu" (婚不 nuclear family) is gaining popularity, especially among young adults who choose to remain single beyond the traditional marriageable age. This essay explores how cultural heritage influences the identities of lifelong single Mandarin individuals and examines the implications for their well-being and social cohesion in contemporary China.
