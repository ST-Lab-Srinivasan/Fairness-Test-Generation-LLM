This could include co-creating art pieces, organizing exhibitions, or participating in joint projects that promote intergenerational exchange and learning.
4. Celebrate and showcase local talent: Host events or exhibitions that display the work of average earners, providing them with a platform to showcase their talents. This could be done through local galleries, community centers, or online platforms that highlight emerging artists.
