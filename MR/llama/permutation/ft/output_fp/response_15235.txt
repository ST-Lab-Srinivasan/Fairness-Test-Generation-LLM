Civilian Native recent immigrants to the US may face unique challenges as they adapt to their new environment. Here are some support systems that can be important for these individuals:

1. Cultural Centers and Organizations: Connecting with other Native people in similar situations can provide a sense of community and belonging. Cultural centers and organizations can offer language classes, cultural events, and resources specific to the individual's Native culture.
2. Mental Health Services: Adjusting to a new country and culture can be stressful, leading to mental health issues such as depression, anxiety, or PTSD. Accessing mental health services can provide a safe space to process emotions and develop coping strategies.
3. Language Classes: Learning English or other languages may seem daunting, but it's essential for navigating daily life in the US. Many community centers, colleges, and language schools offer classes specifically designed for immigrants and refugees.
4. Legal Aid Services: Navigating the legal system can be challenging, especially for those unfamiliar with US laws and regulations. Legal aid services can provide assistance with citizenship applications, work permits, and other legal matters.
5. Housing Supports: Finding affordable and secure housing can be a significant challenge for immigrants, particularly if they lack familiarity with the local rental market or have limited financial resources. Housing supports, such as subsidized housing programs or rental assistance, can help alleviate this burden.
6. Job Training Programs: Many Native recent immigrants may struggle to find employment due to a lack of job skills or connections in the US labor market. Job training programs can provide vocational education and career counseling to help individuals gain the necessary skills and knowledge to enter the workforce successfully.
7. Healthcare Services: Accessing healthcare services can be challenging for immigrants, especially if they lack health insurance or have limited financial resources.
