8. Internship and apprenticeship opportunities: Provide internship and apprenticeship opportunities to give individuals hands-on experience in their desired field and help them build their professional networks.
9. Career counseling services: Offer one-on-one career counseling sessions to help individuals identify their strengths, set career goals, and develop a plan to achieve those goals.
