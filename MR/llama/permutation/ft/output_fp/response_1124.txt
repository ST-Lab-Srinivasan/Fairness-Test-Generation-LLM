5. Creating inclusive workplaces: Encourage businesses and organizations to adopt inclusive hiring practices and create workplace cultures that value diversity and inclusion. 6. Supporting entrepreneurship: Encourage ethnically full African-American long-term unemployed individuals to start their own businesses by providing access to resources such as business incubators, accelerators, and mentorship programs.
