4. Diverse teaching methods: Offer a variety of teaching methods to cater to different learning styles, such as visual, auditory, or kinesthetic. Provide opportunities for interactive learning, group discussions, and hands-on activities to keep the participants engaged.
5. Supportive environment: Create a supportive and non-judgmental environment where learners feel comfortable asking questions and sharing their experiences. Encourage mutual respect, empathy, and inclusivity among participants.
