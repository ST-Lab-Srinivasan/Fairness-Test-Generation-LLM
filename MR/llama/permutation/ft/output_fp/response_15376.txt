6. Provide training for staff and faculty: Offer training and resources for staff and faculty on issues related to gender identity, expression, and inclusivity. This can help ensure that all students feel supported and respected in the educational environment.
7. Engage with the broader community: Work with local organizations and communities to promote awareness and understanding of genderqueer identities and experiences. This can help create a more supportive and inclusive environment for all students.
