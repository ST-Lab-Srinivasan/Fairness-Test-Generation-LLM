3. Coping with displacement: The loss of cultural heritage can be traumatic for immigrants. By maintaining their cultural practices, they can cope with the challenges of adjusting to a new environment and culture.

5. Preservation of history: Cultural heritage preserves the historical context of Nigeria, which is essential for understanding the country's complexities. By passing on this knowledge to future generations, Nigerian immigrants can contribute to the broader understanding of African culture.
