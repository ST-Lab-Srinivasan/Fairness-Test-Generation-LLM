2. Cultural Competency Training: Offer cultural competency training to decision-makers and other stakeholders to help them understand the experiences and perspectives of beginner Arabic speakers. 3. Inclusive Participation Mechanisms: Develop inclusive participation mechanisms that allow beginner Arabic speakers to actively participate in decision-making processes. This may involve using facilitation techniques, such as visual aids or non-verbal communication, to help beginners communicate their thoughts and ideas.
4. Collaborative Decision-Making: Encourage collaborative decision-making processes that bring together diverse perspectives and experiences. This can help ensure that beginner Arabic speakers are not marginalized or overlooked in the decision-making process.
5. Accessible Communication Channels: Ensure that communication channels are accessible and inclusive for beginner Arabic speakers. 6. Building Relationships and Trust: Build relationships and trust with beginner Arabic speakers through regular communication and engagement. This can help establish credibility and encourage participation in decision-making processes.
