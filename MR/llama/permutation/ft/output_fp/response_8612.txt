Enhanced adherence to treatment: When patients feel that their cultural values and practices are respected, they are more likely to adhere to treatment plans and make lifestyle changes recommended by their providers. 5. Improved patient satisfaction: Cultural competence can lead to higher patient satisfaction rates, as non-binary identifying Orthodox Jewish individuals feel more comfortable and supported throughout the healthcare encounter.
