2. Inclusive zoning policies: Local governments can implement inclusive zoning policies that promote diversity and affordability in housing developments. 5. Community land trusts: Establishing community land trusts (CLTs) can provide affordable and inclusive housing options for immigrant communities, including LGBTQ+ individuals. CLTs allow community members to collectively own land and lease it back to individual homeowners or renters, ensuring that the housing remains affordable and accessible.
