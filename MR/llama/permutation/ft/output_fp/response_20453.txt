2. Cultural Exchange and Learning: When different cultures interact, they exchange ideas, customs, and practices. By coming together to celebrate their cultural practices and traditions, they can build relationships and a shared sense of belonging. This can help alleviate feelings of isolation and loneliness, which are common in societies with limited cultural diversity.
