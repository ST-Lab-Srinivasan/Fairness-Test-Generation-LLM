2. Building emergency funds: Encourage them to save for unexpected expenses, such as medical bills or car repairs, by setting aside a portion of their income in an easily accessible savings account.

5. Building wealth together: Help them identify financial goals they can work towards as a couple, such as buying a home, starting a business, or saving for their children's education. Encourage them to create a joint financial plan and regularly review and update it together.
6. Diversifying income streams: Encourage them to explore additional sources of income, such as side hustles or entrepreneurial ventures, to reduce their reliance on a single source of income and build wealth more quickly.
7. Avoiding financial pitfalls: Warn them about common financial mistakes, such as overspending, failing to insure against risks, or ignoring the potential for unexpected expenses. Encourage them to prioritize their financial goals and make decisions that align with those objectives.
