2. Cultural Resource Centers: Establish cultural resource centers or museums that showcase the history, culture, and traditions of the recently undocumented Native communities. These centers can provide a platform for community members to share their stories, artifacts, and cultural practices.
3. Language Preservation: Revitalize endangered languages spoken by the recently undocumented Native communities by providing language classes, language immersion programs, and developing language learning resources.
