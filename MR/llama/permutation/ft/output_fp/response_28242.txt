Supporting the economic empowerment of individuals from mixed Asian heritage who are living just above the poverty line requires a comprehensive approach that addresses the various social, economic, and cultural barriers they face. Here are some strategies that can help:

1. Access to Education and Training: Provide access to quality education and training programs that can help individuals acquire new skills and knowledge to improve their employability and income-generating capabilities. This can include vocational training, entrepreneurship training, and digital literacy programs.
2. Microfinance and Entrepreneurship Support: Offer microfinance services and entrepreneurship support to help individuals start or grow their own businesses. This can include access to loans, grants, and other financial resources that can help them overcome financial barriers and achieve economic stability.
3. Mentorship and Networking Opportunities: Connect individuals with successful professionals and entrepreneurs who can provide mentorship, guidance, and networking opportunities. This can help individuals build valuable connections and gain access to new resources and opportunities.
4. Community Development Programs: Implement community development programs that address the social and cultural barriers faced by mixed Asian heritage individuals. This can include initiatives that promote cultural exchange, understanding, and inclusion, as well as programs that address discrimination and inequality.
5. Advocacy and Policy Change: Advocate for policy changes that address the systemic barriers faced by mixed Asian heritage individuals, such as discrimination in education and employment, and limited access to resources and opportunities. This can include advocating for affirmative action policies, diversity and inclusion initiatives, and anti-discrimination laws.
6. Economic Empowerment through Cooperatives: Encourage the formation of worker cooperatives or consumer cooperatives that can help individuals pool their resources and collectively own and manage businesses. This can provide a platform for economic empowerment and create new opportunities for income generation and wealth creation.
7. Access to Healthcare and Social Services: Ensure access to quality healthcare and social services, including mental health support, counseling, and legal aid. This can help individuals address the non-economic barriers that may be hindering their economic empowerment.
