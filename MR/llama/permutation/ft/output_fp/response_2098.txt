4. Promote self-care: Encourage bereaved freedom advocates to prioritize self-care, including physical exercise, healthy eating, and stress management techniques. This can help them maintain their overall well-being during a challenging time.
5. Provide practical support: Offer practical assistance with daily tasks, such as meal preparation, childcare, or household chores. This can help reduce stress and burden, allowing the individual to focus on their emotional well-being.
6. Facilitate community connections: Encourage bereaved freedom advocates to connect with their community, whether through support groups or social activities. This can help them feel less isolated and more supported in their grief journey.
