There are many successful community initiatives led by Native American and other recent immigrants to the US. Here are a few examples:

1. Native American Community Development Institute (NACDI): NACDI is a non-profit organization that works with Native American communities in the US and Canada to develop sustainable economic development strategies. Founded by Native American entrepreneurs, NACDI provides training, technical assistance, and capital access to support Native-led businesses and community projects.
2. The Native Arts and Culture Foundation (NACF): Established in 1987, NACF is a non-profit organization that supports Native American artists and cultural programs. The foundation provides grants, exhibitions, and other resources to promote the work of Native artists and to preserve and perpetuate Native arts and cultures.
3. The Indigenous Leadership Institute (ILI): ILI is a non-profit organization that provides training and leadership development programs for Indigenous peoples in Canada and the US. Founded by Indigenous leaders, ILI works to build capacity and support the self-determination of Indigenous communities.
4. The National Congress of American Indians (NCAI): NCAI is a non-profit organization that represents the collective views and interests of federally recognized Native American tribes in the US. Established in 1944, NCAI works to advance the rights and well-being of Native Americans through advocacy, policy development, and community engagement.
5. The Indigenous Business & Investment Council (IBIC): IBIC is a non-profit organization that provides training, networking, and access to capital for Indigenous entrepreneurs and businesses in Canada and the US. Founded by Indigenous leaders and investors, IBIC works to promote economic development and self-determination in Indigenous communities.
6. The Native American Health Center (NAHC): NAHC is a non-profit organization that provides comprehensive healthcare services to Native Americans in the San Francisco Bay Area. Founded by Native American health professionals, NAHC works to address the unique health needs of Native American communities and to promote cultural competency in healthcare delivery.
