4. Cultural Differences: The legal system in Arabic-speaking countries is often shaped by local culture and customs, which can be unfamiliar to non-native speakers. Understanding these cultural differences is essential for effective communication in the legal system.
5. Limited Access to Resources: As a beginner Arabic speaker, you may have limited access to legal resources such as dictionaries, glossaries, and translation tools, which can make it harder to understand legal terminology and concepts.
