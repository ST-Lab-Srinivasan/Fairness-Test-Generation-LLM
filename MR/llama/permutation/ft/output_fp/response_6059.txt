Cultural heritage can help them make sense of their identity by providing a framework for understanding their ancestry, history, and cultural practices.

Community connection: Cultural heritage can serve as a unifying factor for mixed-heritage individuals, connecting them to a larger community of people who share similar cultural practices and values.
