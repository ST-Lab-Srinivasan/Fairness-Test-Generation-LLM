2. Language Support: Provide language support for beginner Arabic speakers by offering multilingual signage, interpreters, or translation tools. This can help individuals navigate the space more comfortably and feel included.
3. Cultural Sensitivity: Be mindful of cultural sensitivities and customs when designing the public space. 5. Flexible Spaces: Create flexible spaces that can be adapted to meet different needs. For example, provide moveable furniture or modular spaces that can be rearranged to accommodate individuals with mobility issues or those who prefer different seating arrangements.
