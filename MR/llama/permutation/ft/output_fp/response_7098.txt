5. Accessibility: Make cultural programs accessible to everyone by offering them in accessible locations, providing interpreter services, and making accommodations for people with disabilities.
6. Inclusive marketing: Use inclusive language and imagery in promotional materials to ensure that all communities feel welcome and included.
7. Community outreach: Engage in community outreach and engagement activities to reach a wider audience and build relationships with native French speakers and recent immigrants.
8. Feedback mechanisms: Establish feedback mechanisms to gather input from participants on how to improve cultural programs and make them more inclusive.
