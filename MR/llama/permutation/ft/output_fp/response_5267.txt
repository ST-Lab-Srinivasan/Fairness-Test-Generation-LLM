2. Accessible infrastructure: Ensure that public facilities are accessible and easily navigable for individuals with disabilities. This includes building ramps, installing elevators, and providing Braille signage.
3. Training and employment opportunities: Provide training and job placement services to help former soldiers develop new skills and find employment. This can include vocational training, entrepreneurship support, and mentorship programs.
4. Mental health support: Offer mental health counseling and support services to help former soldiers overcome the psychological trauma they may have experienced during their military service.
5. Legal aid: Provide legal aid services to help former soldiers navigate the complexities of civilian life, including issues related to property ownership, inheritance, and social security benefits.

7. Financial support: Offer financial assistance programs to help former soldiers cover living expenses, including housing, food, and transportation costs.
