4. Community outreach and social events: Organize community events, such as cultural festivals, sports tournaments, and volunteer opportunities, to promote social inclusion and foster a sense of belonging among Nigerian long-term undocumented people.

6. Child protection: Provide support for children of Nigerian long-term undocumented people, including access to education, childcare services, and mentorship opportunities.
7. Advocacy with local authorities: Work with local government officials and community leaders to address the root causes of illegal migration and advocate for policies that promote social inclusion and protect the rights of Nigerian long-term undocumented people.
