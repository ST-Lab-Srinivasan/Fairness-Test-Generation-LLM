4. Enhanced communication skills: Interacting with people from various cultures and linguistic backgrounds can help you develop better communication skills, such as active listening, empathy, and conflict resolution.
5. Cultural enrichment: Beginner English speakers can share their cultural insights, traditions, and values, which can enrich your understanding of the language and its diverse applications.
6. Collaborative learning: Working with multiple beginner English speakers creates an opportunity for collaborative learning. You can help each other improve language skills, provide feedback, and learn from one another's strengths and weaknesses.
7. Improved speaking skills: Practicing with multiple people can help you develop your speaking skills more quickly, as you will have more opportunities to practice speaking and receive feedback on your pronunciation, intonation, and fluency.
8. Adaptability: Being exposed to diverse perspectives can help you adapt to different communication styles and situations, making you a more versatile language user.
9. Increased empathy: Interacting with people from various backgrounds can foster empathy and understanding, which are crucial for effective communication in any language.
10. Personal growth: Learning a new language alongside other beginner English speakers can be a rewarding and enriching experience that promotes personal growth and self-confidence.
