It is important to recognize that the experience of being a permanent resident with a disability can have significant impacts on an individual's life. Some potential impacts include:

1. Social isolation: Individuals with disabilities may face barriers in accessing social and community activities, leading to feelings of isolation and loneliness.
2. Discrimination: People with disabilities may experience discrimination in various areas of life, including employment, education, and healthcare. This can lead to lower self-esteem and confidence levels.
3. Limited access to resources: Permanent residents with disabilities may face barriers in accessing resources such as transportation, housing, and healthcare due to a lack of accessibility features or discriminatory attitudes.
4. Financial strain: Individuals with disabilities may experience financial strain due to higher costs associated with accommodating their disability, such as assistive technology or home modifications.
5. Stigma: People with disabilities may experience stigma and negative attitudes from society, leading to lower self-esteem and confidence levels.
6. Lack of representation: Individuals with disabilities may feel underrepresented in media, politics, and other areas of society, leading to a lack of understanding and empathy towards their experiences.
7. Health disparities: People with disabilities may experience poorer health outcomes due to barriers in accessing healthcare services or experiencing discrimination in healthcare settings.
8. Limited access to education: Individuals with disabilities may face barriers in accessing education, including discrimination, inadequate accommodations, and lack of support.
9. Employment barriers: People with disabilities may face barriers in finding and keeping employment due to discrimination, lack of accessibility in workplaces, or inadequate accommodations.
10. Lack of independence: Individuals with disabilities may experience a lack of independence due to barriers in accessing transportation, housing, and other areas of life.
