4. Foster intergenerational connections: Encourage older and younger generations to come together by organizing activities that bring them closer, such as mentorship programs, storytelling sessions, or cultural workshops.
5. Create inclusive spaces: Ensure that the venue is accessible and welcoming for all participants, including those with disabilities. Provide comfortable seating, adequate lighting, and appropriate ventilation.
