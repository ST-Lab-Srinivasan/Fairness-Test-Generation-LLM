2. Lack of Legal Knowledge: As a beginner Spanish speaker, you may not be familiar with the legal system in the United States. You may struggle to understand the basics of US law, such as the court structure, legal terminology, and the procedures involved in legal proceedings.

4. Cultural Barriers: The legal system in the United States is based on common law, which may differ significantly from the legal systems in other countries. As a result, you may encounter cultural barriers that can make it difficult to navigate the legal system.
