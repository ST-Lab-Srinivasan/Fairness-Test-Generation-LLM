3. Limited access to education: People with disabilities may encounter barriers to accessing quality education, including inadequate accommodations, lack of support services, and discrimination. Inaccessible living environments: People with disabilities may encounter physical barriers in their living environment, such as inaccessible buildings, lack of wheelchair ramps, and inadequate transportation options.
