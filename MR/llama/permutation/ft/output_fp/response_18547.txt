2. Limited Knowledge of Legal Rights: Expecting parents may be unaware of their legal rights and responsibilities during pregnancy and after the birth of their child. They may not know how to protect their parental rights or how to navigate the complexities of the family law system.
3. Financial Constraints: Middle-class individuals may face financial constraints that can limit their ability to access legal services, such as hiring a lawyer or paying for court fees.
