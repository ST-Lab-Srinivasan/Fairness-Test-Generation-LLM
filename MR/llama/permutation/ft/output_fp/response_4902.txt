2. Provide emotional support: Marriage can be a challenging journey, especially when dealing with unknown genetic conditions. Offer emotional support by listening to their concerns, providing reassurance, and helping them cope with the stress of managing their condition.

5. Offer practical assistance: Offer practical assistance, such as helping with household chores or errands, to help alleviate some of the stress and burden associated with managing an unknown genetic condition.
6. Be patient and understanding: Living with an unknown genetic condition can be unpredictable and challenging. Be patient and understanding when dealing with setbacks or frustrations, and offer support during difficult times.
