3. Community Outreach: Engage in community outreach efforts to connect with elderly Hispanic individuals and provide information on available resources and opportunities for civic engagement.

8. Intergenerational Events: Organize intergenerational events that bring together elderly Hispanic individuals with younger generations, promoting mutual understanding and respect.
9. Health Fairs: Host health fairs that provide elderly Hispanic individuals with access to free or low-cost health screenings, nutrition counseling, and other resources that can help them maintain their physical and mental well-being.
