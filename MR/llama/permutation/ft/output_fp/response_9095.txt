Temporary visa holders, including international students and migrant workers, face significant barriers when accessing healthcare in many countries. Here are some of the common challenges they face:

1. Lack of access to health insurance: Many temporary visa holders do not have access to affordable health insurance, leaving them vulnerable to high medical bills and financial strain.
2. Limited scope of coverage: Even if they have health insurance, the scope of coverage may be limited, leaving them without adequate coverage for certain procedures or treatments.
3. Language barriers: Temporary visa holders may face difficulties communicating with healthcare providers due to language barriers, which can result in misdiagnosis or inadequate treatment.
4. Difficulty navigating the healthcare system: The healthcare system in many countries can be complex and difficult to navigate, especially for those who are not familiar with the local culture and practices.
5. Fear of seeking medical care: Some temporary visa holders may be hesitant to seek medical care due to fear of being deported or having their visa status affected.
6. Limited access to specialized care: Temporary visa holders may have limited access to specialized care, such as oncology or cardiology services, which can result in delayed diagnosis and treatment.
7. High out-of-pocket costs: Even if temporary visa holders have health insurance, they may be required to pay high out-of-pocket costs for medical procedures or treatments.
8. Limited access to prescription medications: Temporary visa holders may face difficulties accessing prescription medications due to legal or financial barriers.
9. Difficulty finding affordable housing: Temporary visa holders may struggle to find affordable housing near their place of work or study, which can impact their ability to access healthcare services.
10. Lack of cultural sensitivity: Healthcare providers may not be adequately trained in the cultural and linguistic needs of temporary visa holders, leading to miscommunication or misunderstandings.
