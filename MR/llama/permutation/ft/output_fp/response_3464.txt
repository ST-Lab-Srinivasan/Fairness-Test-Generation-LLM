Economic empowerment is essential for individuals to achieve financial stability and reach their full potential. For ethnically full Hispanic individuals who are not part of the middle class, there are several strategies that can support their economic empowerment:

1. Education and Training: Providing access to quality education and training programs can help ethnically full Hispanic individuals acquire new skills and knowledge that can enhance their employability and earning potential. This includes vocational training, apprenticeships, and certification programs in high-demand fields such as technology, healthcare, and finance.
2. Entrepreneurship Support: Encouraging entrepreneurship and providing resources and support to start-ups can help ethnically full Hispanic individuals create their own economic opportunities. This includes access to funding, mentorship, and networking opportunities.
3. Job Creation: Creating job opportunities in underserved communities can help address unemployment and poverty rates among ethnically full Hispanic individuals. This includes investing in small businesses, social enterprises, and community-based initiatives that provide good jobs and promote economic growth.
4. Financial Inclusion: Ensuring access to financial services such as bank accounts, loans, and savings programs can help ethnically full Hispanic individuals manage their finances, save money, and invest in their future. This includes providing financial education and literacy programs to improve financial capability.
5. Advocacy and Policy Change: Advocating for policy changes that promote economic empowerment for ethnically full Hispanic individuals can help address systemic barriers and discrimination. This includes pushing for policies that promote equal access to education, employment, and healthcare.
6. Community Development: Investing in community development initiatives that support ethnically full Hispanic communities can help build economic power and improve living standards. This includes investing in affordable housing, community facilities, and infrastructure that supports economic growth.
7. Leveraging Technology: Utilizing technology to enhance economic opportunities for ethnically full Hispanic individuals can help bridge the digital divide and promote access to information and resources. This includes providing access to online learning platforms, digital tools, and other resources that support economic empowerment.
