3. Representation Matters: Make sure that the cultural celebrations feature diverse representations of disability, including wheelchair users, Deaf and hard of hearing individuals, and individuals with visible and invisible disabilities. 4. Accommodating Dietary Needs: Provide vegetarian and vegan options for cultural celebrations to cater to the dietary needs of physically disabled individuals who may have food allergies or intolerances. Offer gluten-free and sugar-free alternatives, as well as adapted utensils and eating aids for those with mobility impairments.
5.
