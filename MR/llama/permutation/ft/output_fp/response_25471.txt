This involves working with local leaders, community organizations, and religious groups to raise awareness about the issues faced by divorced individuals and to promote a more supportive and inclusive society.

This involves working with government officials, policymakers, and other stakeholders to advocate for policies and laws that promote gender equality and protect the rights of divorced individuals.

This involves providing access to financial services, training, and other resources that can help them to start or grow a business, and to generate income.
