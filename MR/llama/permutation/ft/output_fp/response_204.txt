Promoting financial stability for native French speakers in their late 30s can be achieved through a combination of smart financial planning, budgeting, and investment strategies. Here are some suggestions:

1. Start Early: Encourage individuals to start saving and investing as early as possible, even if it's just a small amount each month. The power of compounding returns can help grow their wealth over time.
2. Create a Budget: Help individuals create a realistic budget that accounts for all their expenses, including savings and investments. Regularly reviewing and updating the budget can help them stay on track with their financial goals.
3. Prioritize Savings: Encourage individuals to prioritize saving for retirement and other long-term financial goals. Consider setting up automatic transfers from their checking accounts to savings or investment accounts to make saving easier and less prone to being neglected.
4. Diversify Investments: Encourage individuals to diversify their investment portfolios by investing in a mix of low-risk and higher-risk assets, such as stocks, bonds, real estate, and other alternative investments. This can help reduce risk and increase potential returns over the long term.
5. Take Advantage of Tax-Advantaged Accounts: Encourage individuals to take advantage of tax-advantaged accounts such as 401(k), IRA, or Roth IRA. These accounts can provide tax benefits that can help grow their wealth faster.
6. Build an Emergency Fund: Encourage individuals to build an emergency fund that can cover at least 3-6 months of living expenses in case of unexpected events such as job loss or medical emergencies.
7. Educate Yourself: Encourage individuals to educate themselves on personal finance and investing by reading books, attending seminars, or taking online courses. This can help them make informed decisions about their money and avoid costly mistakes.
8. Avoid Debt: Encourage individuals to avoid high-interest debt such as credit card debt and focus on paying off any existing debt before investing.
