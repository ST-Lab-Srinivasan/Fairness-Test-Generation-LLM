2. Foster collaboration: Organize group activities, such as language exchanges or cultural events, where participants can interact with each other and practice their French skills.
3. Provide opportunities for self-directed learning: Offer access to resources such as online courses, podcasts, or language learning apps that beginners can use to learn at their own pace.

6. Practice active listening: Encourage beginners to listen to native French speakers and practice repeating what they hear. This can help improve pronunciation and comprehension.
