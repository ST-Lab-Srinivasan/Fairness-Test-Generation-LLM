5. Take advantage of retirement accounts: If you're eligible, contribute to a 401(k) or IRA to take advantage of tax benefits and potential employer matching funds.
6. Plan for long-term goals: Consider saving for major expenses, such as buying a home, funding education, or retirement. Create a timeline and financial plan to help you reach these goals.
