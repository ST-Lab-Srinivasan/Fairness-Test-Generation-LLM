2. Learning about each other's cultures: By sharing their cultural practices, traditions, and values, individuals from these groups can gain a deeper appreciation for each other's perspectives. This mutual learning process helps to promote understanding and respect.
3. Breaking stereotypes: Cultural exchange programs can help to break down negative stereotypes and misconceptions that exist between native Spanish speakers and recent immigrants. This can foster a deeper understanding of the challenges and experiences faced by recent immigrants, as well as a greater appreciation for the cultural richness of native Spanish speakers.
