2. Access to genetic counseling: Genetic counselors who are knowledgeable about the specific genetic condition affecting Hispanic individuals can provide guidance on testing, management, and treatment options. They can also help address any cultural or language barriers that may exist.
3. Multidisciplinary care team: Patients with genetic conditions often require care from a multidisciplinary team of healthcare professionals, including geneticists, pediatricians, neurologists, and psychologists. 6. Cultural competency training: Healthcare providers should receive cultural competency training to better understand the cultural nuances of Hispanic patients and provide more effective care.
