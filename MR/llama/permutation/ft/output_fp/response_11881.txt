2. Build a diverse team: Assemble a group of people from different backgrounds and experiences to design and implement your community programs. This will ensure that the programs are inclusive and representative of the diverse population you aim to serve.
3. Offer language classes: Provide language classes specifically designed for recent immigrant spouses, teaching them basic phrases, vocabulary, and grammar in the local language. This will help them communicate more effectively and feel more integrated into the community.

6. Establish mentorship programs: Pair recent immigrant spouses with experienced individuals who can provide guidance on navigating local customs, finding job opportunities, and building social connections. This can help them navigate their new environment more smoothly.
