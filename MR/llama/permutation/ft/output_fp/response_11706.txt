4. Advocacy and Lobbying: Advocating for policies and laws that protect the legal rights of lower middle-income nominal Muslim individuals can be an effective way to ensure their rights are protected. This can involve lobbying government officials, participating in public consultations, and advocating for policy changes that promote equality and justice.
5. Alternative Dispute Resolution: Encouraging the use of alternative dispute resolution mechanisms, such as mediation and arbitration, can provide an affordable and accessible way for lower middle-income nominal Muslim individuals to resolve disputes without resorting to costly and time-consuming legal proceedings.
6. Legal Assistance Hotlines: Establishing legal assistance hotlines or helplines can provide a convenient and easily accessible way for lower middle-income nominal Muslim individuals to seek legal advice and assistance.
