4. Identity formation: Cultural heritage helps shape the identity of ethnically full Asian people, particularly in terms of how they perceive themselves relative to other groups. 5. Intergenerational transfer: Cultural heritage is often transmitted from one generation to the next through stories, traditions, and cultural practices. This intergenerational transfer helps maintain cultural continuity and ensures that cultural knowledge and traditions are preserved.

It provides a shared framework for understanding the world and one's place in it, which can help build and maintain social networks and communities.
