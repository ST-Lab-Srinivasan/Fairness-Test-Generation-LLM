4. Representation in media: Promote diverse representation of disability in media, including characters with disabilities, actors with disabilities, and diverse storylines that challenge stereotypes and stigmas.
5. Employment opportunities: Provide employment opportunities for non-disabled individuals with moderate disabilities, such as flexible work arrangements or accommodations to help them participate fully in the workforce.
6. Healthcare accessibility: Ensure that healthcare services are accessible and inclusive for all individuals, regardless of their abilities.
