2. Job Readiness Programs: Offer job readiness programs that teach soft skills, interviewing techniques, and help them build their professional networks.
3. Mentorship and Leadership Opportunities: Pair them with successful professionals from their community who can serve as mentors, role models, and connect them to leadership opportunities.
4. Access to Financial Services: Provide access to financial services such as savings accounts, microfinance loans, and other financial tools to help them manage their finances and build credit.
5. Entrepreneurship Support: Encourage entrepreneurship by providing training, resources, and support for starting and growing their own businesses.
6. Networking Opportunities: Organize networking events and connect them with potential employers, investors, and partners to help them build relationships and grow their professional networks.
