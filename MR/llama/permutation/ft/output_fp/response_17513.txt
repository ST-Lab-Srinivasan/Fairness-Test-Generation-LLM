3. Use inclusive marketing materials: Make sure your marketing materials are accessible and inclusive. Use images and language that reflect the diversity of the community, and avoid using language that could be offensive or alienating to any particular group.

5. Offer childcare and family-friendly activities: Recognize that many beginner Arabic speakers may have young children or be attending the event with their families. Provide childcare services or family-friendly activities to ensure that everyone can participate without worrying about their caregiving responsibilities.
