This could involve providing a safe space for participants to share their experiences and connect with others who are going through similar challenges.
6. Use plain language and simple explanations: Using plain language and simple explanations can help beginner Chinese speakers understand complex information. Avoid using technical jargon or medical terminology that may be difficult for them to comprehend.
7. Offer flexible scheduling options: Accommodating the schedules of beginner Chinese speakers can help ensure that they are able to participate in the program. This could involve offering programs at different times, such as evenings or weekends, or providing online resources and support.
