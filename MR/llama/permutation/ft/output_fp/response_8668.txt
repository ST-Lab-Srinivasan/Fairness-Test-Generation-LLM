3. Building trust: Build trust within the community by being reliable, consistent, and approachable. Establish relationships with community leaders and other trusted individuals to help facilitate communication and ensure that the needs of the community are met.

5. Collaboration with community leaders: Work with community leaders to identify the unique needs of the community and develop strategies to address those needs. This may involve collaborating on outreach efforts, providing cultural competency training, or developing community-based interventions.
