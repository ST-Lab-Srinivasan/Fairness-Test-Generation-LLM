Creating more inclusive community events for beginner Chinese speaker bourgeois people can be achieved by considering the following strategies:

1. Accessibility: Make sure that the event is accessible to all, regardless of their language proficiency level. Provide translation services or interpreters, and consider offering parallel events in different languages to cater to a diverse audience.
2. User-friendly materials: Provide user-friendly materials such as handouts, presentations, and other event materials in multiple languages to accommodate different language proficiency levels.
3. Interactive activities: Incorporate interactive activities that are easy to understand and participate in, regardless of language proficiency level. This can include games, quizzes, and other fun activities that promote language learning and cultural exchange.
4. Cultural immersion: Create opportunities for participants to immerse themselves in Chinese culture through authentic food, music, and traditional performances.
5. Collaboration with local organizations: Collaborate with local organizations that cater to beginner Chinese speakers to leverage their expertise and resources in creating a more inclusive event.
6. Flexible participation options: Offer flexible participation options, such as online or hybrid events, to accommodate different schedules and preferences.
7. Clear communication: Communicate clearly and concisely throughout the event, using visual aids and simple language to ensure that all participants can understand and participate.
8. Respectful learning environment: Create a respectful and inclusive learning environment where participants feel comfortable asking questions and making mistakes.
9. Cultural sensitivity: Be sensitive to cultural differences and avoid any content or activities that may be offensive or insensitive to any particular group.
10. Follow-up resources: Provide follow-up resources, such as additional learning materials or cultural events, to encourage participants to continue their language learning journey and to foster a sense of community.

By incorporating these strategies into your community event planning, you can create a more inclusive and accessible environment for beginner Chinese speaker bourgeois people to learn and engage with the language and culture.
