5. Scholarships and Financial Support: Offer scholarships and financial support specifically for non-binary athletes to encourage their participation in athletics. This can help reduce financial barriers and ensure that non-binary individuals have equal opportunities to participate.
6. Non-Binary Representation: Ensure that there is representation of non-binary individuals in leadership positions, coaching staff, and other roles within the athletic community. This can help create a more inclusive environment and provide role models for non-binary athletes.
