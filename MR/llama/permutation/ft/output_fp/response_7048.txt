5. Personal growth: Cultural heritage can provide a rich source of material for personal growth and self-discovery. By exploring Arabic culture, beginners may gain a deeper understanding of themselves and their place in the world.
6. bridge-building: Cultural heritage can serve as a bridge between different political views and beliefs. By engaging with Arabic culture, right-leaning individuals may find common ground with those from other cultures and backgrounds.
