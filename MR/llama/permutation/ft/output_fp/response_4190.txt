2. Cultural differences: Adapting to a new cultural environment can be challenging, especially if you are used to a different way of working or living. Understanding cultural norms and customs is essential to avoid misunderstandings and offense.
3. Networking: Building professional connections in a new country can be difficult without a local network. You may need to attend job fairs, networking events, and join local professional organizations to expand your network.

Qualifications and credentials: In some countries, degrees or qualifications obtained from native English-speaking countries may not be recognized or valued as highly as those obtained in the host country.
