2. Cultural orientation programs: Providing cultural orientation programs can help recent immigrants understand the local culture, customs, and laws. This can reduce their feelings of isolation and confusion, making it easier for them to navigate their new surroundings.

4. Volunteer opportunities: Connecting recent immigrants with volunteer opportunities can provide them with a sense of purpose and belonging. This can also help them build relationships with others in the community, which is important for their mental health and well-being.
