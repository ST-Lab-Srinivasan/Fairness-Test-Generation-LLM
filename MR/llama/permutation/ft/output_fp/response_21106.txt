1. The Native American Community Development Institute (NACDI): NACDI is a nonprofit organization that provides technical assistance and capacity building to Native American communities. Founded in 1984, NACDI works with tribes, tribal organizations, and other Native American entities to support community development and economic development projects.
2. The Asian Pacific Islander Network (API Net): API Net is a network of Asian Pacific Islander (API) community-based organizations that work together to advance social justice and civil rights for the API community. The organization provides technical assistance, training, and advocacy services to its member organizations and the broader API community.
3. The National Association of Latino Independent Producers (NALIP): NALIP is a nonprofit organization that supports the development and advancement of Latino independent producers in film, television, and digital media. The organization provides mentorship, training, and networking opportunities to its members and the broader Latinx community.
4. The South Asian Network (SAN): SAN is a network of South Asian organizations that work together to advance social justice and civil rights for the South Asian community. The organization provides technical assistance, training, and advocacy services to its member organizations and the broader South Asian community.
5. The Indigenous Language Revitalization Program (ILRP): ILRP is a program that works with Native American communities to revitalize their languages and cultural practices. The program provides language instruction, cultural education, and community outreach services to its partner communities.
