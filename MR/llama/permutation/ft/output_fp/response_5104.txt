3. Better management of cultural differences: In a diverse patient population, cultural differences can sometimes lead to misunderstandings or miscommunications. Cultural competence helps you identify and address these issues, ensuring that your care is delivered in a culturally sensitive manner.

By understanding the nuances of Chinese culture and language, you can convey information and instructions more clearly and avoid misunderstandings.
