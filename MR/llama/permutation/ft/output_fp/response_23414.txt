Promoting the cultural preservation of recently undocumented ethnically full African American communities requires a multifaceted approach that involves various stakeholders and strategies. Here are some ways to achieve this goal:

1. Documentation and Archiving: Collect and document the history, culture, and traditions of these communities through oral histories, interviews, photographs, and other sources. Create archives and databases that can be accessed by scholars, researchers, and community members.
2. Community Engagement: Work with community leaders and members to identify their cultural needs and develop strategies to preserve their heritage. Encourage community participation in cultural events, workshops, and programs that promote cultural exchange and understanding.
3. Education and Outreach: Develop educational materials and programs that teach students and the broader community about the history and culture of these communities. Organize workshops, seminars, and conferences that focus on cultural preservation and revitalization.
4. Cultural Revival Programs: Implement programs that promote the cultural revival of these communities, such as language revitalization, traditional music and dance performances, and cultural festivals. Encourage community members to share their knowledge and skills with younger generations.
5. Collaboration with Institutions: Work with museums, libraries, and other cultural institutions to exhibit the cultural heritage of these communities and ensure their representation in historical and cultural narratives. Encourage these institutions to collect and preserve artifacts, documents, and other cultural materials that reflect the diversity of these communities.
6. Policy Advocacy: Advocate for policies that recognize and support the cultural preservation of these communities. Work with government agencies, non-profit organizations, and community groups to develop and implement policies that promote cultural diversity and inclusivity.
7. Economic Development: Support economic development initiatives that benefit these communities and promote their cultural heritage. Encourage local businesses and entrepreneurs to create products and services that reflect the unique cultural identities of these communities.
8. Community Land Trusts: Establish community land trusts that provide affordable housing and commercial spaces for these communities. This can help preserve their cultural identity and ensure their continued presence in these areas.
