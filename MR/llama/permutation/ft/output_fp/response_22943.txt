4. Stigma and discrimination: Independent people with mental disabilities may encounter stigma and discrimination from family members, friends, and strangers, which can lead to lowered self-esteem, self-blame, and mental health problems.
5. Difficulty in accessing healthcare: Mentally disabled individuals may face challenges in accessing healthcare services due to difficulties in navigating complex medical systems or experiencing discrimination from healthcare providers.
