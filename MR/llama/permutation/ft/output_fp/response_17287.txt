Supporting the educational aspirations of beginner English speaker civilian people can be done through various means, including:

1. Language classes: Offer language classes specifically designed for beginners, focusing on basic grammar, vocabulary, and pronunciation. These classes can be conducted in-person or online, and can include interactive activities and games to keep students engaged.
2. Online resources: Provide access to online resources such as language learning apps, websites, and videos. These resources can offer a variety of learning tools, including grammar lessons, vocabulary builders, and pronunciation practice.
3. Language exchange programs: Organize language exchange programs where beginners can practice speaking with native English speakers or other learners. This can be done in-person or online, and can provide a supportive environment for language learning.
4. Cultural events: Host cultural events that showcase English-speaking countries, such as movie nights, book clubs, or discussion groups. These events can help learners gain a deeper understanding of the culture and language, and provide opportunities to practice speaking with native speakers.
5. Tutoring services: Offer one-on-one tutoring sessions with experienced English language teachers. These sessions can focus on specific areas of difficulty for the learner, such as grammar, vocabulary, or pronunciation.
6. Mobile apps: Develop mobile apps that provide interactive language learning activities, such as games, quizzes, and exercises. These apps can be used anywhere, at any time, and can be a convenient way for learners to practice their English skills.
7. Online communities: Create online communities or forums where beginners can connect with other learners, ask questions, and share language learning experiences. These communities can provide support and motivation for learners, as well as opportunities to practice speaking and writing in English.
