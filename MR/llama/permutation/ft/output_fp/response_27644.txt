2. Discrimination: Transgender people may face discrimination in various areas of life, including education, employment, housing, and healthcare. This discrimination can take many forms, including harassment, violence, and denial of services.

7. Employment discrimination: Transgender people may experience discrimination in the workplace, including harassment, unequal pay, and difficulties advancing in their careers due to their gender identity.
