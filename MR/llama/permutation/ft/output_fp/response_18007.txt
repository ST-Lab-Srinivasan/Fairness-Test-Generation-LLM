Creating more inclusive cultural programs for expectant and non-disabled individuals requires thoughtful planning and consideration of the needs of all participants. Here are some strategies to help make your cultural programs more inclusive:

1. Accessibility features: Ensure that your venue or event space is wheelchair accessible, has accessible restrooms, and is equipped with assistive listening devices for individuals who are deaf or hard of hearing. Provide clear signage and wayfinding for all participants.
2. Adaptable programming: Offer a variety of programs that can be adapted to meet the needs of different participants. For example, offer sign language interpretation or audio descriptions for individuals who are deaf or hard of hearing, or provide modified formats for individuals with cognitive or intellectual disabilities.
3. Inclusive marketing: Use inclusive language in your marketing materials and make sure that they are accessible to all participants. Use images that reflect the diversity of your audience, and avoid using language that could be offensive or alienating.
4. Sensory-friendly environments: Create a sensory-friendly environment by reducing noise levels, providing adequate lighting, and offering fidget toys or other sensory tools for individuals who may have difficulty regulating their sensory experiences.
5. Accommodations for pregnant women: Offer accommodations for pregnant women, such as seating areas with comfortable cushions or pillows, or providing extra water and snacks to help them stay hydrated and energized.
6. Support from diverse staff: Ensure that your staff is diverse and representative of the communities you serve. Provide training on inclusive practices and cultural competency to all staff members to ensure that they are equipped to provide appropriate support to all participants.
7. Partnership with organizations serving diverse communities: Collaborate with organizations that serve diverse communities to reach a broader audience and ensure that your programs are tailored to the needs of different populations.
