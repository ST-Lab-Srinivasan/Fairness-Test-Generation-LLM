Highlighting the achievements of young adult Native recent immigrants to the US can be a great way to showcase their resilience, determination, and success in the face of adversity. Here are some ways to do so:

1. Share Their Stories: Highlight the personal stories of young Native recent immigrants to the US, including their experiences of migration, cultural identity, and overcoming challenges. Use storytelling platforms like podcasts, videos, or social media to share their stories with a wider audience.
2. Celebrate Their Accomplishments: Highlight the achievements of young Native recent immigrants in various fields such as education, arts, sports, and entrepreneurship. Share their success stories and recognize their accomplishments through awards, recognitions, or public events.
3. Create Networking Opportunities: Facilitate networking opportunities for young Native recent immigrants to connect with each other, mentors, and industry leaders. This can help them build relationships, gain insights, and access resources that can aid their professional growth.
4. Provide Access to Resources: Offer access to educational resources, mentorship programs, and career development opportunities to help young Native recent immigrants achieve their goals. This can include language classes, job training programs, or college scholarships.
5. Advocate for Policy Change: Work with policymakers to create policies that support the success of young Native recent immigrants. Advocate for changes in immigration laws, education policies, and economic development initiatives that promote inclusion and equal opportunities.
6. Promote Cultural Exchange: Organize cultural exchange programs that bring together young Native recent immigrants with their peers from other communities. This can help foster cross-cultural understanding, mutual respect, and collaboration.
7. Provide Legal Support: Offer legal support and representation to young Native recent immigrants who may be facing immigration-related challenges. This can include assistance with visa applications, citizenship processes, and appeals.
8. Create Community Spaces: Establish community spaces or cultural centers where young Native recent immigrants can gather, connect, and share their experiences. These spaces can also serve as incubators for creativity, innovation, and social entrepreneurship.
