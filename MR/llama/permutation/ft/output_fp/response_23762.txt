6. Cultural sensitivity: Be sensitive to the cultural background and identity of Mexican students, and provide educational materials and resources that reflect their experiences and perspectives.
7. Community engagement: Encourage community engagement and service learning opportunities that allow young adults to apply their skills and knowledge in real-world contexts, and develop a sense of social responsibility and civic engagement.
8. Internship and job placement: Provide internship and job placement opportunities for young adults to gain practical work experience and build their professional networks.
9. Support services: Offer support services such as academic advising, mental health counseling, and peer support groups to help young adults navigate the challenges of higher education and adulthood.
