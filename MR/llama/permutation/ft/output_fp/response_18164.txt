Create programs that encourage social interaction, such as volunteer opportunities, community events, or intergenerational activities that bring together older adults with younger generations.
2. Foster a sense of purpose: Older adults often struggle with feelings of purposelessness, which can negatively impact their mental and emotional well-being. Create programs that help them find new sources of meaning and purpose, such as mentorship opportunities, skill-building workshops, or community service projects.

5. Offer educational opportunities: Many older adults in Nigeria may have missed out on formal education or want to continue learning new skills. Develop programs that offer educational opportunities, such as literacy classes, computer training, or vocational workshops.
6. Foster intergenerational connections: Create programs that bring together older adults with younger generations, such as mentorship opportunities or community events that encourage intergenerational dialogue and collaboration.
