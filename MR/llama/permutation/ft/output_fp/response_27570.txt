Celebrating the cultural contributions of Native American natural-born US citizens is crucial for recognizing and honoring their rich heritage. Here are some ways to do so:

1. Cultural Festivals and Events: Host cultural festivals and events that showcase Native American music, dance, art, and food. These events can be held in communities with a high population of Native Americans or in larger cities where there is a significant Native American presence.
2. Education and Awareness Campaigns: Organize educational programs and awareness campaigns to teach people about the history, culture, and contributions of Native Americans. This can include lectures, workshops, and exhibitions that highlight the rich cultural heritage of Native Americans.
3. Support Native-Owned Businesses: Support Native-owned businesses by shopping at their stores or purchasing their products. This can help to promote economic development in Native American communities and show appreciation for their cultural contributions.
4. Indigenous Language Revitalization: Support efforts to revitalize indigenous languages by attending language classes, learning phrases in Native American languages, and promoting the use of these languages in everyday life.
5. Artist Collaborations: Collaborate with Native American artists to create artwork that showcases their cultural heritage. This can include paintings, sculptures, or other forms of visual art.
6. Cultural Exchanges: Organize cultural exchanges between Native American communities and other communities to promote mutual understanding and respect. These exchanges can involve traditional music, dance, and food sharing, as well as other cultural practices.
7. Historical Preservation: Support efforts to preserve historical sites and landmarks that are significant to Native American culture. This can include supporting the preservation of ancient ruins, burial grounds, or other cultural artifacts.
8. Community Service Projects: Organize community service projects that involve Native American communities. These projects can include cleaning up local parks, painting murals, or participating in other forms of community service.
9. Film Screenings: Host film screenings that showcase Native American films and filmmakers. This can help to promote the work of these artists and raise awareness about their cultural contributions.
