This can promote empathy and understanding, as participants learn to appreciate the challenges and opportunities faced by people from different cultural backgrounds.
3. Encourages cross-cultural communication: Cultural exchange programs often involve interactive activities that encourage cross-cultural communication. By engaging in these activities, individuals can develop their communication skills and learn how to navigate different cultural contexts.
