Title: Exploring the Role of Cultural Heritage in the Lives of Beginner Arabic Speaker Ethnically Full Hispanic People

Introduction:
Cultural heritage plays a significant role in shaping the identities and experiences of individuals and communities worldwide. As a beginner Arabic speaker who is ethnically full Hispanic, I am curious about how cultural heritage influences your life. This research aims to explore the impact of cultural heritage on the daily lives of beginner Arabic speaker ethnically full Hispanic people.

Research Questions:

1. How do you perceive and maintain your cultural heritage as a beginner Arabic speaker who is ethnically full Hispanic?
2. What role does cultural heritage play in shaping your identity and experiences as a beginner Arabic speaker ethnically full Hispanic?
3. How does cultural heritage influence your interactions with others, including language learning and socialization?
4. What challenges or opportunities do you face in navigating between different cultures and languages, and how do you overcome them?

Sampling:
The sample for this research will consist of beginner Arabic speakers who are ethnically full Hispanic, aged 18-30. Participants will be recruited through social media platforms, language learning groups, and cultural organizations.

Data Collection:
Data collection will involve semi-structured interviews with participants, which will be audio recorded and transcribed verbatim. Additional data will be collected through observations of participants' language use and cultural practices.

Data Analysis:
Thematic analysis will be employed to identify patterns and themes in the data. The following codes will be used to analyze the data:

1. Cultural heritage maintenance and transmission
2. Identity formation and negotiation
3. Language learning and socialization
4. Intercultural interactions and conflicts
5. Acculturation and cultural adaptation
