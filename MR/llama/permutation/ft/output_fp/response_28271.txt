It is important to acknowledge and validate these experiences, and to recognize that intersectional discrimination can have a compounding effect on mental and physical health.

3. 5. Conduct research: Conducting research on the experiences of NKGC individuals can help to shed light on the unique challenges they face and inform strategies for addressing these issues. This may involve surveying or interviewing NKGC individuals, as well as conducting qualitative or quantitative analysis.
