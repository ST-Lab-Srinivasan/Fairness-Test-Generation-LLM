3. Scholarships and Financial Support: Provide financial assistance and scholarships to Native American athletes to help them pursue their sports dreams. 5. Mentorship and Leadership Development: Pair aspiring Native American athletes with experienced professionals who can provide guidance, support, and leadership opportunities.
