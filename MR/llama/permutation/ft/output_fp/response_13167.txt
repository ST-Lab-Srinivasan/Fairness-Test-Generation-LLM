2. Adaptive programming: Offer programs that are adaptable to different cultures and backgrounds. For example, instead of using a one-size-fits-all approach to teaching English, consider incorporating cultural elements from the participants' countries of origin into the lessons. 3. Inclusive language: Use inclusive language in all program materials, including flyers, posters, and marketing materials. Avoid using language that stereotypes or marginalizes any particular ethnic group.
