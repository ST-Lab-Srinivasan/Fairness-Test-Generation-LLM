Introduction:
Marriage is a significant life event that can have a profound impact on an individual's emotional, social, and economic well-being. When a marriage ends in divorce, it can be a challenging and stressful time for both partners. This study aims to investigate the impact of marital status on the lives of recently divorced moderate individuals.

Background:
Divorce is a common occurrence in today's society, with over 50% of marriages ending in dissolution. The process of divorce can be emotionally draining and can have significant consequences for an individual's mental health, social relationships, and financial stability.

Methodology:
This study will use a mixed-methods approach, combining both quantitative and qualitative data. A survey questionnaire will be administered to a sample of recently divorced individuals (N = 100) to collect quantitative data on their marital status, mental health, social relationships, and financial stability. Semi-structured interviews will also be conducted with a subset of participants (N = 20) to gather more in-depth qualitative data on their experiences and perceptions of the impact of divorce on their lives.

Results:
The survey results revealed that recently divorced individuals experienced significant emotional distress, with 75% of participants reporting symptoms of depression and anxiety. Financial instability was also a common issue, with 60% of participants experiencing financial difficulties following the divorce. Social relationships were affected, with 55% of participants reporting decreased social interaction and feelings of isolation.

The qualitative interviews provided more insight into the impact of divorce on participants' lives. Themes emerged around the loss of identity, changes in social relationships, and the emotional toll of divorce. Participants reported feeling a sense of loss and grief following the dissolution of their marriage, as well as feelings of guilt and shame.

Conclusion:
The results of this study suggest that marital status has a significant impact on the lives of recently divorced moderate individuals. Divorce can lead to emotional distress, financial instability, and changes in social relationships.
