7. Education and Awareness: Raise awareness about the economic empowerment of beginner English speaker communities by highlighting their potential contributions to society. This can involve promoting their skills, talents, and achievements through various channels, including media and social platforms.
8. Advocacy and Policy Change: Work with policymakers to create policies that promote the economic empowerment of beginner English speaker communities. This can involve advocating for access to education, job training, and entrepreneurial resources.
