2. Language support: Offer language support services, such as translation and interpretation, to ensure that ex-military native Arabic speakers have equal access to educational resources and materials. This can be particularly helpful for those who may struggle with the dominant language in the educational setting.

4. Flexible learning options: Offer flexible learning options, such as online courses or part-time programs, to accommodate the schedules and needs of ex-military individuals who may have busy lives or varying levels of access to educational resources.
