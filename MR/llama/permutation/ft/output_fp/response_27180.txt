Understanding Cultural Heritage:
Cultural heritage refers to the shared beliefs, values, customs, traditions, and practices of a particular group or society. It encompasses a wide range of elements, including language, religion, art, music, literature, and historical sites. In the case of China, cultural heritage is rich and diverse, with a long history that spans over 5,000 years.

This, in turn, can make your language learning experience more enjoyable and rewarding.
