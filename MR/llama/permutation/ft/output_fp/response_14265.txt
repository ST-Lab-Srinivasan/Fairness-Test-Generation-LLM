2. Offer flexible attendance options: Provide multiple ways for people to participate in your events, such as online webinars or virtual workshops. This can help accommodate different schedules and locations.

4. Offer financial assistance: Consider offering scholarships or financial aid to help make your events more accessible to those with limited budgets. This could include waiving registration fees or providing travel grants.
5. Incorporate diverse programming: Ensure that your event offerings cater to a wide range of interests and needs, including topics relevant to military members and their families, such as financial planning, career development, and mental health support.
