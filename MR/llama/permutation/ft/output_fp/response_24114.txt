3. Education and Awareness: Organize workshops, seminars, or training sessions to educate community members on disability etiquette, accessibility, and inclusion. Encourage cultural sensitivity and awareness among non-disabled individuals.

5. Healthcare Accessibility: Advocate for accessible healthcare services, including wheelchair-accessible clinics and hospitals, braille or large print medical documents, and sign language interpreters during consultations.
6. Social Inclusion: Organize community events and activities that promote social inclusion, such as cultural festivals, sports tournaments, or volunteer opportunities. Encourage non-disabled individuals to participate in these events and interact with physically disabled community members.
