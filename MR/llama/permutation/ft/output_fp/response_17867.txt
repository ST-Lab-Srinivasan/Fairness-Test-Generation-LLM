6. Connect them with resources: Introduce them to local organizations that provide financial assistance for education-related expenses or connect them with professionals who can offer advice on how to navigate the educational system as a recently divorced person.
7. Be a sounding board: Allow them to talk through their ideas and concerns about their educational aspirations, and offer feedback and suggestions when appropriate.
8. Encourage them to take breaks: Going back to school can be overwhelming, especially after a divorce. Encourage them to take breaks and prioritize self-care to avoid burnout.
