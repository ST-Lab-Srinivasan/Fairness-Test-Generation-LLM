2. Economic development initiatives: Support economic development projects that create jobs and stimulate economic growth in Native American communities. This can include small business loans, entrepreneurship support, and investments in infrastructure and community facilities.

5. Land restoration and conservation: Support efforts to restore and conserve Native American lands, including the preservation of traditional hunting and gathering areas, and the protection of sacred sites.
