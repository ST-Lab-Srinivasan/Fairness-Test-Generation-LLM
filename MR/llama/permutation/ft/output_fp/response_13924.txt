6. Advocate for accommodations: If an ex-spouse former soldier has a disability or other challenge that may impact their ability to find employment, advocate on their behalf for any necessary accommodations in the workplace. 7. Provide financial support: Many ex-spouse former soldiers may face financial challenges during their transition into civilian life.
