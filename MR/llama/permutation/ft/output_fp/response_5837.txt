7. Offer flexible scheduling options: Accommodate the schedules of participants by offering flexible class times or online courses that can be accessed at any time. This can help ensure that everyone has access to language instruction, regardless of their availability or mobility.
8. Provide disability-related resources: Make available resources and information related to disabilities, such as advocacy groups, support services, or assistive technology organizations. This can help connect individuals with the resources they need to navigate the community.
