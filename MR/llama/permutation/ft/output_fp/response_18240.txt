6. Social isolation: Individuals with genetic conditions may experience social isolation due to discrimination, stigma, or difficulties in maintaining relationships.
7. Emotional distress: Living with a genetic condition can be emotionally challenging, leading to anxiety, depression, or low self-esteem.

9. Limited career advancement: Individuals with genetic conditions may face barriers to career advancement due to their health status or limitations in their job performance.
