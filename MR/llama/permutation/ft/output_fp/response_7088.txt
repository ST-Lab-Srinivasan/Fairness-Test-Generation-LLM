9. Offer Multiple Pathways: Offer multiple pathways for participants to engage with Native American culture, such as language classes, cultural workshops, or community events.
10. Evaluate and Improve: Continuously evaluate the programs and make improvements based on feedback from participants and the community.

By following these suggestions, it is possible to create more inclusive cultural programs that are respectful of Native American cultures and promote cross-cultural understanding.
