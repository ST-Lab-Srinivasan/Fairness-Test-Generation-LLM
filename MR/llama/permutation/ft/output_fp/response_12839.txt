This can make it hard to communicate effectively in a job setting, especially if you're working with native Chinese speakers.
2. Limited vocabulary: As a beginner, you may not have a large enough vocabulary to express yourself fully in Chinese. This can limit your ability to communicate complex ideas and thoughts, and can make it difficult to understand the language in a professional setting.
