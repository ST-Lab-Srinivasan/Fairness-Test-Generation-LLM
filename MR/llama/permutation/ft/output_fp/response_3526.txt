Advocating for policies that address genetic conditions: Work with advocacy groups, patient organizations, and healthcare providers to push for policies that address the needs of individuals with unknown genetic conditions. 5. Creating educational resources: Develop educational materials, such as brochures, videos, or infographics, that explain the basics of genetics and genetic testing. Share these resources through social media, online forums, or in-person events to educate individuals about their rights and options.
