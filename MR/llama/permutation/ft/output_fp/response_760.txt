2. Cultural sensitivity training: Offer cultural competency training for healthcare providers to help them understand the beliefs, values, and practices of Chinese immigrants. This can enhance their ability to provide appropriate care and address any cultural differences that may affect patient outcomes.
3. Health literacy support: Develop educational materials in simplified Chinese or traditional Chinese to help patients better understand medical conditions, treatments, and self-care instructions. This can be especially important for individuals with limited English proficiency.

5. Community outreach: Engage in community outreach efforts to connect with Chinese immigrant populations and raise awareness about available healthcare services. This can involve partnering with local organizations, hosting cultural events, or leveraging social media platforms to reach a wider audience.
