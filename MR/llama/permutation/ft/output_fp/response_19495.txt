2. Language practice: Cultural exchange programs provide an opportunity for beginner English speakers to practice speaking and listening to English in a supportive environment. This can help them improve their language skills and feel more comfortable communicating with native speakers and recent immigrants.
3. Cross-cultural learning: Participants can learn about each other's cultural practices, values, and beliefs. This cross-cultural learning can promote mutual respect and understanding between the different groups.
4. Building bridges: Cultural exchange programs can help to build bridges between different communities by fostering personal connections and relationships. When individuals from different backgrounds interact with each other regularly, they are more likely to develop empathy and understanding for each other's perspectives.
