As a non-native French speaker, you may struggle to understand complex legal terms, concepts, and procedures. This can lead to misunderstandings, miscommunications, and confusion during legal proceedings.
2. Cultural differences: Legal systems in France and the UK have some cultural differences, which can affect how you approach legal issues. Understanding these cultural differences is crucial to navigating the legal system successfully.

For example, the concept of "duty of care" is not recognized in French law, whereas the concept of "provisional damages" is. Understanding these differences is crucial to navigating the legal system successfully.
