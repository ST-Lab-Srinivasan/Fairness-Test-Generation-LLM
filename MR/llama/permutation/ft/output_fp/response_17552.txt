There are several ways in which data can be used to highlight the issues faced by beginner English speaker native Arabic speaker individuals:

1. Language proficiency surveys: Conduct surveys to assess the language proficiency of beginner English speakers and compare it with native Arabic speakers. The survey can include questions on their language learning experiences, challenges faced, and goals.
2. Corpus analysis: Analyze large datasets of written texts to identify common errors and phrases used by beginner English speakers. This can help in identifying areas where learners need more practice or feedback.
3. User-generated content analysis: Analyze user-generated content, such as comments on language learning platforms or social media posts, to understand the challenges faced by beginner English speakers. This can help identify common issues and provide insights into what learners find difficult.
4. Online language courses data: Analyze data from online language courses to understand the progress and struggles of beginner English speakers. This can include data on completion rates, time spent on coursework, and test scores.
5. Learner diaries: Encourage beginner English speakers to keep a diary of their language learning experiences, including challenges faced, successes achieved, and goals set. This can provide valuable insights into the learners' perspectives and experiences.
6. Social media analysis: Analyze social media posts from beginner English speakers to understand their language learning experiences and challenges. This can include hashtags related to language learning, as well as content created by language learning accounts.
7. Learner surveys: Conduct surveys of beginner English speakers to gather information on their language learning experiences, challenges faced, and goals. The survey can include questions on the learners' background, motivation for learning English, and strategies used.
8. Speech recognition analysis: Use speech recognition software to analyze the pronunciation and fluency of beginner English speakers. This can help identify areas where learners need more practice or feedback.
