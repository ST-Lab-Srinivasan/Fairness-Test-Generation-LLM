2. Access to Financial Services: Ensure that nominal Muslim civilian individuals have access to a range of financial services, including bank accounts, loans, and insurance. This will help them manage their finances more effectively and make smart investment decisions.

4. Financial Inclusion: Work towards increasing financial inclusion by expanding access to financial services for marginalized communities, including women, youth, and low-income individuals. 5. Risk Management: Help individuals manage risk by providing education on how to diversify their investments, hedge against inflation and market volatility, and protect themselves from unexpected events such as illness or job loss.

7. Community Development: Invest in community development programs that promote economic growth and stability at the local level. This can involve supporting small businesses, infrastructure projects, and social welfare initiatives that benefit the wider community.
8. Advocacy and Policy Change: Advocate for policy changes that promote financial stability and inclusion, such as interest rate caps, affordable housing programs, and microfinance initiatives.
