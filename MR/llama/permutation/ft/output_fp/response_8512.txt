1. Cultural Sensitivity: Provide educational resources and programs that reflect the cultural and religious practices of the Jewish community. 6. Intergenerational Programs: Develop intergenerational programs that bring together older adults and younger generations. These programs can help bridge the gap between different age groups within the Jewish community and foster a sense of connection and belonging among all ages.
