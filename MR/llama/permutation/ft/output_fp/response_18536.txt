3. Resource mobilization: Radical democratic socialists often rely on volunteers to help mobilize resources, such as funding, supplies, and expertise, to support their causes. Volunteers can help with tasks like organizing benefit events, crowdfunding campaigns, and resource coordination.
4. Outreach and education: Volunteers can help educate the broader community about socialist principles and issues by participating in outreach and educational activities. This can include tabling at events, leading workshops, or engaging in public speaking engagements.
