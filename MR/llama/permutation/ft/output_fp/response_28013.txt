8. Collaboration with community organizations: Partner with local organizations that serve Mandarin-speaking immigrant communities to co-host programs or services.
9. Adaptability: Be flexible and adaptable in program offerings and outreach strategies to best meet the needs of Mandarin-speaking immigrants.
