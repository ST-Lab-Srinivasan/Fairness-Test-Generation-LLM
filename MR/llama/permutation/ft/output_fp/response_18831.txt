Creating more inclusive public policies for civilian temporary visa holders requires a comprehensive approach that takes into account the unique needs and challenges faced by this population. Here are some strategies that governments can consider to create more inclusive policies:

1. Data collection and analysis: Collect data on the demographics, socioeconomic status, education levels, and employment history of temporary visa holders. Analyze the data to identify trends and patterns, which can help inform policy decisions.
2. Consultation with stakeholders: Engage in consultations with civil society organizations, community groups, and temporary visa holders themselves to better understand their needs and challenges. This can help identify potential solutions that are tailored to the specific needs of this population.
3. Awareness campaigns: Launch awareness campaigns to educate the general public about the contributions of temporary visa holders and the importance of creating inclusive policies for them. This can help build empathy and support for policy changes.
4. Streamlined application processes: Simplify and streamline the application process for temporary visas to make it more accessible and user-friendly for all applicants, regardless of their background.
5. Access to education and training: Provide access to education and training programs that cater to the needs of temporary visa holders. This can help them acquire new skills and knowledge that can enhance their employability and contribute to the economy.
6. Social security benefits: Offer social security benefits, such as health insurance and pension plans, to temporary visa holders who are contributing to the economy. This can help ensure that they have access to basic necessities and feel more secure in their living and working conditions.
7. Job creation and placement services: Provide job creation and placement services specifically tailored to the needs of temporary visa holders. This can help match them with suitable employment opportunities and promote economic integration.
8. Language training and support: Offer language training and support services to help temporary visa holders improve their language proficiency, which can enhance their employability and social integration.
9. Access to financial services: Provide access to financial services, such as bank accounts and loans, to help temporary visa holders manage their finances
