Supporting the civic engagement of non-citizen mixed Asian heritage people can be achieved through various means, including:

1. Voter registration drives: Organize events or campaigns to register non-citizens to vote, and provide information on the voting process and the importance of participating in the democratic process.
2. Civic education: Provide educational resources and workshops that teach non-citizens about their rights and responsibilities as citizens, including how to participate in the electoral process, how to contact their elected representatives, and how to stay informed about local and national issues.
3. Language accessibility: Provide language interpretation and translation services at civic events and meetings, and make sure that important information is available in multiple languages to reach a diverse audience.
4. Cultural sensitivity training: Provide cultural sensitivity training for elected officials and other civic leaders to better understand the needs and experiences of non-citizen mixed Asian heritage people.
5. Community building: Host events and activities that bring non-citizens together with other members of their community, fostering a sense of belonging and shared identity.
6. Advocacy and policy change: Advocate for policies that support the civic engagement of non-citizen mixed Asian heritage people, such as automatic voter registration, same-day registration, and online registration.
7. Mentorship programs: Establish mentorship programs that pair non-citizens with experienced citizens who can provide guidance on how to participate in the democratic process.
8. Intergenerational dialogues: Organize intergenerational dialogues between non-citizens and long-term residents to share stories, experiences, and perspectives, and to foster a sense of community and belonging.
9. Civic participation opportunities: Provide opportunities for non-citizens to participate in civic activities, such as volunteering, advocacy work, or community service.
10. Legal support: Offer legal support and resources to help non-citizens navigate the citizenship process and understand their rights and responsibilities as citizens.

By implementing these strategies, organizations can help support the civic engagement of non-citizen mixed Asian heritage people and promote greater civic participation
