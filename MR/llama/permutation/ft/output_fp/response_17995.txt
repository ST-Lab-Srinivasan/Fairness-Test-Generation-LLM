3. Access to Gender-Appropriate Materials: Ensure that trans students have access to gender-appropriate textbooks, materials, and resources that reflect their identities and experiences.

6. Accommodations in Sports and Extracurricular Activities: Allow trans students to participate in sports and extracurricular activities that align with their gender identity, without fear of discrimination or marginalization.
7. Safe Spaces: Create safe spaces for trans students, such as dedicated bathrooms or changerooms, where they can feel comfortable and secure.
8. Involvement in Decision-Making Processes: Involve trans students in decision-making processes related to their education, such as creating policies and practices that affect them directly.
9. Education and Awareness: Educate the broader school community about issues related to gender identity and expression, including the importance of respecting and affirming trans individuals.
10. Collaboration with Trans Advocacy Groups: Work with local trans advocacy groups to stay informed about best practices and receive support in addressing the specific needs of trans students.
