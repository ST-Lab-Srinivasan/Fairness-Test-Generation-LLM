9. Environmental sustainability: Volunteers can help promote environmental sustainability by providing education and support on sustainable practices, such as waste management, conservation, and sustainable agriculture.
10. Disaster response: Volunteers can provide critical support during times of crisis or disaster, such as natural disasters, conflicts, or pandemics, by providing emergency aid, rescue services, or long-term recovery support.
