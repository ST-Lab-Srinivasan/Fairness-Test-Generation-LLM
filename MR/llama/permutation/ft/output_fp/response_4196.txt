2. Lack of cultural understanding: Spanish culture and customs may be unfamiliar to beginner Spanish speakers, which can lead to misunderstandings or miscommunications in the workplace. For example, gestures or expressions that are common in Spain or Latin America may not be appropriate in a professional setting in other countries.
