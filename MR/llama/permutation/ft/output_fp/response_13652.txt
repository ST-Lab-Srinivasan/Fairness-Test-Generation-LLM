For ex-military ethnically full African Americans, who may have developed communication styles based on their military experience, cultural competence can help them connect with civilian healthcare providers and navigate the healthcare system more effectively.

Increased access to care: By understanding the cultural nuances of different patient populations, healthcare providers can tailor their approaches to better meet the needs of underserved or marginalized communities. For ex-military ethnically full African Americans, who may face barriers to accessing healthcare due to systemic inequalities, cultural competence can help identify and address these issues.
