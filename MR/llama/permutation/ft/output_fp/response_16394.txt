1. Lack of knowledge and training among healthcare providers: Many healthcare providers may not be familiar with the needs and experiences of gender-nonconforming individuals, leading to inadequate care and misunderstandings.

4. Difficulty accessing care: Genderfluid individuals may face barriers to accessing healthcare due to discrimination, stigma, or lack of knowledge about their rights and entitlements.
