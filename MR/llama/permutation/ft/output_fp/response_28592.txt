5. Healthcare Access: Ensure access to quality healthcare for physically disabled Native individuals. This can include providing accessible healthcare facilities, training healthcare providers in disability awareness, and addressing health disparities faced by this population.
6. Community-Based Programs: Develop community-based programs that provide support and services specifically tailored to the needs of physically disabled Native individuals. This can include recreational activities, social events, and mental health counseling.
7. Advocacy and Policy Change: Advocate for policy changes at the local, state, and federal levels to promote the rights and inclusion of physically disabled Native individuals.
