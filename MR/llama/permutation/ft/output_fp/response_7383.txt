2. Language support: Offer language training or translation services to help them communicate more comfortably in their new environment. This can improve their ability to connect with colleagues, clients, and the wider community.
3. Networking opportunities: Organize networking events or introduce them to local professionals who can provide guidance and support. This can help them build valuable connections and gain insights into the Indian business landscape.
