3. Distance learning options: Offer distance learning options, such as online courses or video conferencing, to accommodate the unique needs of Native American students who may be living in remote areas or serving in the military.
4. Cultural immersion programs: Develop cultural immersion programs that allow students to learn about their heritage and culture while earning academic credits. These programs can provide a unique learning experience and help students connect with their community and culture.
5. Mentorship opportunities: Provide mentorship opportunities for Native American students, particularly those serving in the military. This can involve pairing students with experienced educators or professionals who can offer guidance and support throughout their academic journey.
6. Access to resources: Ensure that Native American students have access to the resources they need to succeed academically, such as textbooks, computers, and other technological tools.
