5. Flexible learning options: Offer flexible learning options, such as online courses or part-time study, to accommodate the schedules and needs of poor freedom advocates who may have other responsibilities or commitments.
6. Cultural sensitivity: Be mindful of the cultural backgrounds and experiences of the individuals you are working with. Provide educational resources and materials that are culturally relevant and appropriate, and create a safe and inclusive learning environment.
7. Community outreach: Engage in community outreach and engagement activities to raise awareness about the importance of education and to connect with individuals who may be interested in pursuing educational opportunities.
