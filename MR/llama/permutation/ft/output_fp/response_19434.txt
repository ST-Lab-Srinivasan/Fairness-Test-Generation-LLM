2. The program aims to promote cross-cultural understanding, language preservation, and academic achievement among indigenous youth.
3. The Native American Business Association (NABA) - Established by Tonia Hughes, a member of the Cherokee Nation, NABA is a professional organization that supports Native American entrepreneurs in the US. The association provides networking opportunities, business mentorship, and advocacy for indigenous-owned businesses.
