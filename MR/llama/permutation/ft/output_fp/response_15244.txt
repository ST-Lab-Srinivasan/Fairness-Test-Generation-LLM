There are many successful community initiatives led by native French speakers, both above and below the poverty line. Here are a few examples:

1. Fondation de France - This foundation was established in 1976 to support local communities throughout France. It provides funding for a wide range of projects, including those focused on education, healthcare, and social inclusion.
2. Terre des Hommes - This organization works with disadvantaged children and families in France and around the world. Its programs include early childhood development, education, and emergency aid.
3. Acteurs Solidarios - This network of community-based organizations works to promote social justice and solidarity in France. Its members include local groups focused on issues such as immigration, housing, and environmental protection.
4. Les Restos du Cœur - This organization provides meals and other essential goods to people in need throughout France. It was founded in 1985 and now has over 300 locations across the country.
5. Banque Alimentale - This initiative aims to reduce food waste and promote sustainable agriculture in France. It works with local farmers, restaurants, and other organizations to redistribute surplus food and promote environmentally-friendly practices.
6. AMAP - This network of local groups works to promote sustainable agriculture and food systems in France. Its members include farmers, researchers, and other stakeholders who are committed to sustainable agriculture practices.
7. Solidarité Pénitentsienne - This organization works with prisoners and ex-prisoners in France to promote their reintegration into society. It provides support with job training, housing, and other essential services.
8. Mouvement des Associations de Lutte Contre le Cancer - This network of cancer patient organizations works to improve the quality of life for cancer patients and their families in France. Its members include local groups focused on issues such as early detection, treatment, and support.
9. Agence Universitaire de la Francophonie - This organization works to promote French language and culture around the world. It provides funding and support for research projects, cultural events, and other initiatives that promote the French language and its speakers.
