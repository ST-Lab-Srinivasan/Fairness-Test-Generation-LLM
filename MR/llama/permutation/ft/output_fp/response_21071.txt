Promoting the artistic talents of beginner Chinese speakers in their 90s or older can be a rewarding and fulfilling experience. Here are some suggestions on how to do so:

1. Encourage and support: The first step is to encourage and support these individuals in their artistic pursuits. Listen to their stories, appreciate their creations, and provide constructive feedback that can help them improve.
2. Provide opportunities for expression: Offer a variety of artistic mediums such as painting, drawing, writing, music, dance, or any other form of creative expression. This will allow them to explore their talents and find what works best for them.
3. Collaborate with caregivers and family members: Caregivers and family members can play a significant role in promoting the artistic talents of elderly individuals. They can encourage and support them, and help them access resources and opportunities to express their creativity.
4. Create intergenerational programs: Collaborate with local schools, community centers, or other organizations to create intergenerational programs that bring together young and old. This can provide a platform for the elderly to share their talents and experiences with younger generations.
5. Showcase their work: Organize exhibitions, performances, or publications that showcase the artistic works of elderly individuals. This can help them gain recognition and appreciate their creative abilities.
6. Provide resources and training: Offer resources and training to help them improve their skills and knowledge in various art forms. This can include workshops, classes, or online tutorials.
7. Build a community: Create a community of like-minded individuals who share a passion for the arts. This can provide a supportive environment where they can learn from each other, exchange ideas, and inspire one another.
8. Celebrate their talents: Organize events or activities that celebrate the artistic talents of elderly individuals. This can help them feel appreciated and valued for their creative contributions.
9. Encourage self-expression: Encourage elderly individuals to express themselves through art, music, writing, or any other form of creative expression. This can help them process their emotions, reflect on their experiences, and maintain cognitive function.
