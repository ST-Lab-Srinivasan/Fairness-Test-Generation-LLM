This could include expanding Medicaid coverage, establishing community clinics, or offering financial assistance programs for medical expenses.

Offer vocational training and employment support: For individuals with genetic conditions who are unable to work in traditional capacities, providing vocational training and job placement services can help them earn a steady income.
