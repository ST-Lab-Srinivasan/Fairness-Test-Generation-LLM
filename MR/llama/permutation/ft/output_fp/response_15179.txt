Creating more inclusive housing policies for very old (90s+) civilian individuals requires a thoughtful and comprehensive approach that considers their unique needs and challenges. Here are some strategies to consider:

1. Age-friendly design: Develop housing that is designed with older adults in mind, incorporating features such as wider hallways, easier-to-use fixtures and appliances, and ample natural light.
2. Accessibility: Ensure that the housing is accessible to older adults with mobility issues by providing things like wheelchair ramps, elevators, and accessible bathroom and kitchen facilities.
3. Supportive services: Offer supportive services such as housekeeping, meal delivery, and transportation to help older adults maintain their independence and quality of life.
4. Intergenerational living: Encourage intergenerational living by providing housing options that bring together people of different ages and backgrounds, fostering mutual support and understanding.
5. Community outreach: Develop partnerships with local organizations and community groups to provide resources and support for older adults, such as health and wellness programs, social activities, and educational opportunities.
6. Affordability: Ensure that housing options are affordable and accessible to low-income older adults by offering subsidies or other financial assistance.
7. Culturally sensitive care: Provide culturally sensitive care that takes into account the diverse needs and practices of older adults from different ethnic and cultural backgrounds.
8. Technology integration: Integrate technology, such as smart home devices, to help older adults maintain their independence and safety.
9. Flexible living arrangements: Offer flexible living arrangements that can accommodate older adults' changing needs over time, such as temporary stays or transitioning from independent living to assisted living.
10. Advocacy: Advocate for policies and programs that support inclusive housing options for older adults, including increased funding for affordable housing, age-friendly zoning regulations, and anti-discrimination laws.
11. Public education: Educate the public about the importance of inclusive housing options for older adults and the benefits of intergenerational living.
