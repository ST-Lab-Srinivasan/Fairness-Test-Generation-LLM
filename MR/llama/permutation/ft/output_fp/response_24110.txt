3. Academic accommodations: Identify and provide academic accommodations that are tailored to the needs of immigrant students. This can include extra time to complete assignments, note-taking assistance, or use of assistive technology.
4. Mentorship programs: Establish mentorship programs that pair immigrant students with experienced teachers or peers who can provide guidance and support. This can help build a sense of belonging and academic success.
