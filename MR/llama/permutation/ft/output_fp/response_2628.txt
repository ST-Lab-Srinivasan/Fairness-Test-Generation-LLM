8. Provide feedback and support: Offer constructive feedback and support to beginner Chinese speakers to help them improve their language skills and overcome challenges.
9. Use technology: Utilize technology such as translation apps, language learning software, and virtual reality tools to facilitate language learning and disaster response.
10. Celebrate successes: Celebrate the achievements of beginner Chinese speakers in disaster response to boost their morale and motivation to continue learning.
