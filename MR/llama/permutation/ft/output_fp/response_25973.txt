1. These services should be tailored to meet the specific needs of Native American students and address any cultural barriers or stigmas they may face.
2. Financial support: Many Native American recent immigrants face financial challenges due to a lack of resources and support. Provide financial aid, scholarships, and other forms of financial support to help them cover the costs of college tuition, books, and living expenses.
