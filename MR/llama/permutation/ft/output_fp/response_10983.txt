Promoting the economic empowerment of native Arabic speaker nominal Muslim communities is crucial for their overall well-being and prosperity. Here are some strategies that can be implemented to achieve this goal:

1. Education and Skills Development: Provide access to quality education and skills development programs, particularly in areas such as literacy, numeracy, and vocational training. This will enable individuals to secure better-paying jobs and improve their socio-economic status.
2. Entrepreneurship and Small Business Development: Encourage entrepreneurship and small business development by providing access to capital, mentorship, and networking opportunities. This can help create job opportunities and generate income for individuals and communities.
3. Access to Financial Services: Improve access to financial services such as bank accounts, loans, and savings programs. This will enable individuals to manage their finances effectively, save money, and invest in their future.
4. Job Creation and Employment Opportunities: Work with local organizations and businesses to create job opportunities for members of the community. This can include internships, apprenticeships, and permanent employment positions.
5. Capacity Building and Leadership Development: Provide training and capacity building programs for community leaders and volunteers. This will enable them to effectively advocate for their communities and work towards positive change.
6. Community-based Initiatives: Support community-based initiatives that promote economic empowerment, such as cooperatives, farmers' markets, and community savings schemes. These initiatives can help build resilience and promote economic independence within the community.
7. Collaboration with Government Agencies: Work closely with government agencies and NGOs to provide support and resources for community development. This can include access to funding, technical expertise, and policy advocacy.
8. Addressing Discrimination and Inequality: Identify and address any forms of discrimination or inequality that may exist within the community, such as unequal access to education or employment opportunities.
9. Building Partnerships and Networks: Foster partnerships and networks between local organizations, businesses, and government agencies to promote collaboration and coordination in promoting economic empowerment.
