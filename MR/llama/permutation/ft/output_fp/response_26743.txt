2. Bilingual Education Programs: Offer bilingual education programs for Native American students who may not be fluent in English. This can help them navigate the educational system more easily and improve their academic outcomes.
3. Tutoring and Mentoring: Provide one-on-one tutoring and mentoring to support Native American students who may be struggling academically or socially. This can help them build confidence and develop study skills.
4. Community Outreach: Engage in community outreach and engagement activities to connect with Native American families and students. This can help build trust and increase involvement in the educational system.
