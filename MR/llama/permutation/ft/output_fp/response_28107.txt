2. Language support: Offer language classes or resources for beginner Arabic speakers to improve their proficiency in English or the language spoken in the athletics industry. This can help bridge the communication gap and make it easier for them to participate fully.

6. Marketing campaigns: Develop marketing campaigns that are tailored to beginner Arabic speakers, highlighting the benefits of participation and the support available to them. This can help increase visibility and interest in the industry.
7. Partnerships with community organizations: Collaborate with community organizations that serve beginner Arabic speaker communities to promote athletics programs and provide access to resources and support.
8. Diversity and inclusion initiatives: Implement diversity and inclusion initiatives within the athletics industry to create a more welcoming environment for beginner Arabic speakers.
