2. 3. Cultural sensitivity training: Provide cultural sensitivity training for staff and volunteers to help them understand and appreciate the diversity of the Arabic-speaking community. This can involve learning about Islamic customs, traditions, and practices, as well as the unique challenges faced by this community.
4. Arabic language programs: Offer language classes or workshops specifically tailored to beginners, which can help individuals improve their language skills and become more confident in their ability to communicate effectively. This can also serve as a platform for building connections with other members of the Arabic-speaking community.
