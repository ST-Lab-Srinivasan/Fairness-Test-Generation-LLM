2. Community outreach: Engage in community outreach and engagement efforts to educate lower-class native English speakers about the importance of preventive care, early detection, and treatment. 4. Financial assistance: Offer financial assistance programs, such as sliding fee scales or discounted rates, to make healthcare more affordable for lower-class native English speakers.
5. Collaboration with community organizations: Partner with local community organizations that serve lower-class native English speakers to provide healthcare services and promote health education.
6. Mobile clinics: Organize mobile clinics or outreach programs to reach lower-class native English speakers in their communities, particularly those living in areas with limited access to healthcare facilities.
7. Public education campaigns: Launch public education campaigns to raise awareness about the importance of preventive care, early detection, and treatment among lower-class native English speakers. Use media channels, such as television, radio, and social media, that are accessible to this population.
