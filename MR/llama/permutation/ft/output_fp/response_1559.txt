Supporting the social inclusion of recent immigrants in India can be a challenging task, especially for those who are not recently single. Here are some ways to help them integrate into Indian society:

1. Language classes: Offer language classes specifically designed for recent immigrants, teaching them basic Hindi and English vocabulary, grammar, and pronunciation. This will help them communicate more effectively in their daily lives.
2. Cultural orientation programs: Organize cultural orientation programs that introduce them to Indian customs, traditions, and values. This can include learning about Indian etiquette, festivals, and social norms.
3. Social events: Host social events specifically for recent immigrants, such as potluck dinners or group outings to local attractions. These events can help them meet other people in similar situations and build friendships.
4. Mentorship programs: Pair recent immigrants with experienced Indians who can serve as mentors, providing guidance on various aspects of Indian culture and society. This can include advice on finding a job, navigating the healthcare system, or dealing with everyday challenges.
5. Community outreach: Connect recent immigrants with local community organizations that support newcomers, such as refugee centers or expat groups. These organizations can provide additional resources and support for their integration into Indian society.
6. Online resources: Create online platforms or forums where recent immigrants can connect with each other, ask questions, and share experiences. This can help them feel more connected to the community and reduce feelings of isolation.
7. Job training programs: Offer job training programs specifically designed for recent immigrants, teaching them skills relevant to the Indian job market. This can include vocational training, language training, or entrepreneurship development.
8. Educational programs: Organize educational programs that teach recent immigrants about Indian history, culture, and society. This can include lectures, workshops, or interactive sessions led by experts in the field.
9. Healthcare support: Provide healthcare support services specifically for recent immigrants, such as free medical check-ups, vaccination programs, or mental health counseling.
