3. Accessible facilities: Ensure that schools are physically accessible for all students, including non-disabled seniors. This includes wheelchair accessibility, Braille signage, and audio descriptions of visual content.
4. Teacher training: Provide training for teachers on how to support non-disabled senior students, including strategies for differentiated instruction and accommodations.
5. Peer mentorship: Pair non-disabled senior students with disabled peers for mentorship and buddy programs. This can help promote understanding, empathy, and mutual respect between generations.
