2. Financial Support: Offer financial assistance targeted towards low-earning individuals, such as grants, loans, or vouchers for essential items like food, shelter, and clothing. Consider offering priority access to these resources based on income levels or other criteria.
3. Access to Resources: Provide accessible and safe spaces for low-earning individuals to receive basic necessities like food, water, and hygiene products. Offer workshops or training sessions on topics like first aid, safety protocols, and stress management to help them cope with the disaster.

Employment Opportunities: Identify potential employment opportunities for low-earning individuals in the disaster response sector, such as temporary work in logistics, transportation, or distribution centers.
