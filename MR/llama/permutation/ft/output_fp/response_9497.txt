A mentor can help them explore their identity and connect with others who share similar experiences and values. This can lead to a stronger sense of belonging and purpose.
6. Access to resources: Mentors can provide access to valuable resources such as scholarships, job opportunities, and cultural events. These resources can help recent immigrants accelerate their personal and professional growth within the right-leaning community.
