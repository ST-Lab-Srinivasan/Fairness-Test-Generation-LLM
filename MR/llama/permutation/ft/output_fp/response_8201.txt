1. Cultural Humility and Respect: Approach community engagement with cultural humility, recognizing the richness and diversity of Native American cultures. 2. Community-Led Initiatives: Focus on community-led initiatives that prioritize the needs and aspirations of Native American communities. Collaborate with community leaders and members to develop programs and services that are tailored to their specific contexts.
3. Holistic Health Approach: Embrace a holistic approach to healthcare, recognizing that physical, mental, emotional, and spiritual well-being are interconnected. Provide access to culturally appropriate healthcare services, including prenatal care, dental care, and mental health support.
