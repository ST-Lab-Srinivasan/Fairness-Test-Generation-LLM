Creating more inclusive cultural programs for beginner Arabic speakers who are ethnically full Hispanic individuals requires a thoughtful and intentional approach. Here are some suggestions to make your programs more welcoming and inclusive:

4. Representation Matters: Ensure that the cultural programs reflect the diversity of the Arabic speaking community. Use images, videos, and other visuals that showcase the diverse backgrounds and experiences of ethnically full Hispanic individuals.
