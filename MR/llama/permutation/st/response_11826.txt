5. Mentorship: Experienced volunteers can act as mentors to newer volunteers, providing guidance and support as they navigate their roles within the organization. This can help build a stronger and more cohesive volunteer network.
6. Community Building: Volunteers can help create opportunities for bisexual service members to connect with one another, both online and offline.
