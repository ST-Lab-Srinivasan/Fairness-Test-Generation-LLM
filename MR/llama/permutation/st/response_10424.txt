4. Lack of Resources: As a foreigner in China, you may have limited access to legal resources such as lawyers, translation services, and legal databases. This can make it difficult to research and prepare for legal proceedings.
5. Unfamiliarity with the Court System: The court system in China is complex and can be challenging for foreigners to navigate. You may need to familiarize yourself with the different courts, their jurisdictions, and the procedures for filing a case.
