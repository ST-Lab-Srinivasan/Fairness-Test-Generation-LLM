Supporting the social inclusion of Nigerian young adults (aged 20s) can be achieved through various means. Here are some suggestions:

1. Education and Skills Development: Provide access to quality education and vocational training programs that help young adults acquire relevant skills, such as computer literacy, entrepreneurship, and leadership. This will enable them to compete in the job market and contribute to the economy.
2. Mentorship and Coaching: Establish mentorship programs that connect young adults with experienced professionals and successful entrepreneurs. These mentors can provide guidance, support, and valuable insights on how to navigate Nigeria's complex social and economic landscape.
3. Networking Opportunities: Organize networking events, conferences, and workshops where young adults can connect with their peers, potential employers, and industry leaders. This will help them build professional relationships, gain exposure, and access new opportunities.
4. Community Service: Encourage young adults to participate in community service activities, such as volunteering at local hospitals, schools, or non-profit organizations. This will help them develop a sense of social responsibility and civic engagement, while also providing an opportunity to build their networks and gain work experience.
5. Cultural Exchange Programs: Organize cultural exchange programs that bring together young adults from different parts of Nigeria and the world. These programs can help foster cross-cultural understanding, mutual respect, and cooperation, which are essential for social inclusion.
6. Sports and Recreational Activities: Encourage young adults to participate in sports and recreational activities, such as football, basketball, or music. These activities can help build teamwork, discipline, and self-confidence, while also providing a platform for socialization and networking.
7. Access to Financial Services: Provide young adults with access to financial services, such as bank accounts, loans, and savings schemes. This will help them manage their finances, invest in their future, and achieve financial stability.
