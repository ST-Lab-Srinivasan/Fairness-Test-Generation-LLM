Educate coaches, trainers, and other stakeholders about the unique needs and challenges faced by immigrant athletes.
2. Scholarships and financial support: Offer scholarships or financial assistance to immigrant athletes to help cover the costs of participating in sports programs. This can include fees, equipment, travel expenses, and other related expenditures.
3. Cultural competency training: Provide cultural competency training for coaches, trainers, and other stakeholders to help them understand the needs and experiences of immigrant athletes. This can include learning about different cultural practices, beliefs, and values that may impact an athlete's participation in sports.
