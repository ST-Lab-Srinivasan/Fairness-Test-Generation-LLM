Supporting the educational aspirations of beginner English speakers can be a rewarding and enriching experience for both parties involved. Here are some ways to do so:

1. Provide language learning resources: Offer access to quality language learning materials such as textbooks, language learning apps, and online courses. This will help your partner improve their language skills at their own pace.
2. Practice speaking and listening together: Engage in conversations with your partner regularly, using authentic materials like news articles, videos, or podcasts. This will help them practice their listening and speaking skills in a real-life context.
3. Encourage active learning: Encourage your partner to take an active role in their language learning process. This can involve participating in language exchange programs, attending language classes, or engaging in conversation with native speakers.
4. Provide feedback and support: Offer constructive feedback on your partner's language use, highlighting areas of improvement and celebrating their successes. Be patient and understanding, as learning a new language can be challenging.
5. Create a supportive environment: Foster a positive and encouraging environment for your partner to learn and practice their English skills. Encourage them to take risks and make mistakes, as these are essential parts of the language learning process.
6. Set realistic goals and track progress: Work with your partner to set achievable language learning goals and track their progress over time. Celebrate their successes and identify areas for improvement.
7. Use technology to facilitate learning: Utilize digital tools such as language learning apps, online courses, or language exchange websites to make the learning process more convenient and accessible.
8. Encourage consistent practice: Encourage your partner to practice their English skills consistently, even if it's just for a few minutes each day. Consistency is key when it comes to language learning.
9. Offer cultural immersion opportunities: Provide opportunities for your partner to immerse themselves in English-speaking cultures through events, movies, TV shows, or social activities. This will help them develop a deeper understanding of the language and its associated cultural practices.
