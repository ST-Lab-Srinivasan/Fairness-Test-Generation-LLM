3. Take Advantage of Government Resources: The US Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) provides a range of legal resources for veterans, including the VA Office of General Counsel and the VA Legal Services Program. These resources can provide assistance with issues such as disability claims, pension benefits, and survivor's benefits.

5.
