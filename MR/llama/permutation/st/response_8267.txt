2. Cultural Sensitivity: Be mindful of cultural differences and norms when designing public spaces. For example, consider the importance of privacy and personal space in Arab culture, and design public spaces that respect these values.
3. Accessibility Features: Incorporate accessibility features such as ramps, elevators, and accessible restrooms to ensure that all individuals can access and navigate public spaces easily.

5. Inclusive Design: Design public spaces that are inclusive of all individuals, regardless of their abilities. For example, incorporate sensory-friendly elements such as tactile graphics and sound-absorbing materials to create a comfortable environment for individuals with sensory processing challenges.
