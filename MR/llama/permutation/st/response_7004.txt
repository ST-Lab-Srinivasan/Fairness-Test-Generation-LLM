Care work and unpaid labor: Women and girls are often expected to perform care work, such as childcare and household chores, which can limit their ability to participate in the workforce or pursue educational opportunities. 7. Political underrepresentation: Women and girls are underrepresented in political positions of power, which can result in a lack of representation and voice in the legal system. Efforts should be made to increase their participation in politics and decision-making processes.
