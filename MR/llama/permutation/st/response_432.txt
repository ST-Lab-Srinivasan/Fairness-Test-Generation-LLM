Understand Your Audience:

Understanding their preferences and expectations is crucial for creating an inclusive event. 2. Cater to their learning style: People from this demographic may prefer different learning styles, such as visual, auditory, or kinesthetic. Incorporate various teaching methods, like multimedia presentations, group discussions, and interactive activities, to cater to these preferences.
