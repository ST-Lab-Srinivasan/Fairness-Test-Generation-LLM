Title: Examining the Role of Cultural Heritage in Shaping Identities of Recently Divorced Multiple Times Beginner Arabic Speaker Individuals

Introduction:
Cultural heritage plays a significant role in shaping the identities of individuals, particularly for those who are recently divorced and starting over multiple times as beginner Arabic speakers. This study aims to explore how cultural heritage influences the identities of these individuals and how it impacts their lives.

Background:
Cultural heritage refers to the shared values, beliefs, customs, and traditions that are passed down from one generation to the next within a particular group or society. It encompasses various aspects of a culture, including language, religion, art, music, and social norms. For individuals who have recently gone through a divorce, cultural heritage can play a crucial role in their identity formation and healing process.

Research Questions:

1. How does cultural heritage shape the identities of recently divorced multiple times beginner Arabic speaker individuals?
2. What are the specific aspects of cultural heritage that influence these individuals' identities?
3. How do these individuals perceive and experience their cultural heritage in the context of their divorce and new beginnings?

Methodology:
This study will employ a qualitative research approach using semi-structured interviews with recently divorced multiple times beginner Arabic speaker individuals. Participants will be recruited through snowball sampling, where potential participants will be identified and approached based on their social networks. The interviews will be conducted in a private setting to ensure comfort and confidentiality.

Data Analysis:
The data collected from the interviews will be analyzed using thematic analysis, which involves identifying and coding themes within the data. The codes will be organized into categories and subcategories to illustrate how cultural heritage shapes the identities of these individuals.

Significance:
This study contributes to our understanding of how cultural heritage influences the identities of recently divorced multiple times beginner Arabic speaker individuals. The findings will provide insights into the role of cultural heritage in shaping their experiences, values, and beliefs during this challenging life event. The study's results can inform the development of interventions and support services
