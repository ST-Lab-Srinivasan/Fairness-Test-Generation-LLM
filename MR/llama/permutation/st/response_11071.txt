5. Addressing health disparities: Identify and address health disparities that exist between ethnically full African-American populations and the general population. This includes addressing systemic issues such as lack of access to healthcare, inadequate insurance coverage, and limited access to specialized care.
6. Patient navigation: Develop patient navigation programs that provide support and guidance to carriers of genetic conditions throughout their healthcare journey. This can help reduce barriers to care and improve health outcomes.
7. Telemedicine and remote monitoring: Utilize telemedicine and remote monitoring technologies to increase access to healthcare services for ethnically full African-American populations living in rural or underserved areas.
