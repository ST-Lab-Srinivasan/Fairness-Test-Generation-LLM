Supporting the social inclusion of average earners and moderate-income individuals is crucial for building a fair and equitable society. Here are some ways to achieve this goal:

1. Progressive taxation: Implement a progressive tax system where higher earners pay a greater share of their income in taxes. This can help reduce income inequality and redistribute wealth to those who need it most.
2. Increase the minimum wage: Raise the minimum wage to a living wage that allows workers to afford basic necessities without relying on government assistance. This can help lift people out of poverty and reduce reliance on public support programs.
3. Job training and education: Provide job training and education programs to help individuals acquire new skills and access better-paying jobs. This can help them earn a higher income and achieve financial stability.
4. Social safety nets: Strengthen social safety nets, such as unemployment insurance, food assistance programs, and housing support. These programs can provide a critical layer of support for individuals who are struggling financially.
5. Encourage entrepreneurship: Support small businesses and start-ups, particularly those owned by average earners and moderate-income individuals. This can help create new jobs and economic opportunities in local communities.
6. Housing affordability: Implement policies to make housing more affordable, such as rent control, subsidized housing, or down payment assistance programs. This can help reduce the financial burden of housing costs for average earners and moderate-income individuals.
7. Healthcare reform: Work towards universal healthcare or a more comprehensive and affordable healthcare system. This can help reduce healthcare costs for average earners and moderate-income individuals, who may struggle to afford quality healthcare.
8. Public transportation: Invest in public transportation infrastructure, such as buses, trains, and bike lanes. This can make it easier for people to get to work, school, and other essential services without relying on expensive cars.
9. Community development: Support community development initiatives that bring people together and promote social cohesion. This can help build stronger, more inclusive communities where everyone has access to resources and opportunities.
