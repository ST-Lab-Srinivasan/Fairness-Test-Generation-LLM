4. Job Training and Placement: Offer job training programs specifically tailored for ex-spouses, including vocational skills training, resume building, and interview preparation. Also, partner with local employers to provide job placement opportunities.
5. Child Care Support: Provide affordable child care options, such as subsidized daycare centers or cooperatives, to help ex-spouses focus on their career development and financial stability.
6. Housing Assistance: Develop affordable housing options, including shared living spaces or cooperative housing models, to provide ex-spouses with stable and secure housing.
7. Education and Training: Offer scholarships or financial assistance for ex-spouses who want to pursue further education or professional training to enhance their earning potential.
8. Healthcare Support: Provide access to affordable healthcare services, including mental health support, to help ex-spouses manage stress and anxiety related to their financial situation.
9. Community Building: Facilitate the creation of community networks and support groups for ex-spouses to connect with each other, share resources, and provide emotional support.
