Increasing Financial Security: Middle-class single individuals often face financial insecurity due to lack of retirement savings, limited access to education and job training, and unpredictable income streams. Policies such as providing paid family leave, increasing funding for homecare services, and expanding support for caregiver training and education can help address this issue.
