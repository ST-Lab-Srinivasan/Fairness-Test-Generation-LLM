5. Foster a sense of belonging: Create a warm and welcoming atmosphere at your event by encouraging attendees to connect with one another and feel comfortable sharing their experiences and perspectives.
6. Be mindful of power dynamics: Be aware of the historical and systemic inequalities that have affected the Asian community, and work to create an event that is inclusive and respectful of these differences.
7. Provide accessible accommodations: Ensure that your event is physically accessible to individuals with disabilities by providing things like wheelchair accessibility, sign language interpretation, or other accommodations as needed.
