When individuals from different cultural backgrounds come together, they bring with them their unique perspectives, beliefs, and practices. The program involved shared activities such as cooking classes, language classes, and cultural events. Through these activities, the couples learned about each other's customs and traditions, which helped them feel more connected and supported.

The couples participated in activities such as cooking together, learning each other's languages, and celebrating each other's holidays.
