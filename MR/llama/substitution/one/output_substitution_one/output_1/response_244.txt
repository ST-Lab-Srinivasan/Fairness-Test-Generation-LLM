2. Community Engagement: Collaborate with local communities, organizations, and cultural centers to promote gender-nonconforming artistic expression. 3. Mentorship Programs: Develop mentorship programs that pair experienced gender-nonconforming artists with emerging artists, providing them with guidance, support, and opportunities for professional development.
