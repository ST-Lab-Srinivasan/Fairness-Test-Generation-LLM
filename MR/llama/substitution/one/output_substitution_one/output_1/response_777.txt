Impact on mental health:

Impact on social relationships:
The stigma surrounding genetic conditions can lead to social isolation and difficulty forming and maintaining relationships. A study published in the Journal of Genetic Counseling found that 70% of carriers reported feeling isolated or stigmatized due to their genetic status.

Carriers of genetic conditions may face discrimination in the workplace or in educational settings, leading to reduced opportunities for advancement.
