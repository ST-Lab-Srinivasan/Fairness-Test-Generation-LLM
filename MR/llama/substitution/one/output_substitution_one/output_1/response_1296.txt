7. Offer mentorship opportunities: Pair individuals with more experienced artists or creatives who can provide guidance, support, and constructive feedback.
8. Encourage intercultural exchange: Facilitate dialogue and collaboration between individuals from different cultural backgrounds to foster cross-cultural understanding and artistic growth.
9. Provide networking opportunities: Organize events or workshops that bring together creatives, industry professionals, and potential clients to help individuals connect with others in their field and gain exposure for their work.
