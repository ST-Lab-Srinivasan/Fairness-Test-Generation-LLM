3. Raising awareness: Educating the public about the experiences of individuals with unknown or undiagnosed genetic conditions can help increase empathy and understanding. This can help reduce stigma and discrimination, and create a more inclusive society.

5. Utilizing social media: Social media platforms can be effective tools for raising awareness and mobilizing support for advocacy efforts. Hashtags and online campaigns can help amplify the voices of individuals with unknown or undiagnosed genetic conditions.
6. Meeting with policymakers: Scheduling meetings with elected officials and their staff can provide an opportunity to discuss policy issues and advocate for change. This can be done individually or through organized advocacy efforts.
