2. Stigma and discrimination: Disabled individuals may encounter discrimination or stigma within the legal system, which can lead to feelings of isolation or marginalization.
3. Limited understanding of disability: Legal professionals may lack understanding or training in disability-related issues, leading to misunderstandings or miscommunications that can impact the outcome of legal proceedings.
4. Inadequate support: Disabled individuals may not receive adequate support or resources within the legal system, such as assistive technology or additional time to complete legal tasks due to their disability.
5. Barriers to employment and education: Disabled individuals may face barriers to accessing educational or employment opportunities within the legal field due to discrimination or a lack of accommodations.
6. Social Security Disability Insurance (SSDI) issues: Disabled veterans may experience difficulties in securing SSDI benefits, which can impact their financial stability and ability to access legal services.

8. Transportation barriers: Disabled veterans may face challenges in accessing transportation to legal appointments or proceedings due to a lack of accessible transportation options.
