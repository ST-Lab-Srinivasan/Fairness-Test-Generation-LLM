1. Cultural Competence Training: Provide educators with training on cultural competence, which involves understanding and respecting the cultural differences and values of all students. 4. Safe Spaces: Create safe spaces for culturally Caucasian students to express themselves and share their experiences without fear of judgment or discrimination. 5. Cultural Role-Playing: Engage culturally Caucasian students in role-playing activities that simulate cultural experiences and challenges they may face. This can help them develop empathy and understanding of other cultures, as well as build their confidence in navigating diverse social situations.
