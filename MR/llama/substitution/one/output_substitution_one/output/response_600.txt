2. Economic Development: Support economic development initiatives that benefit Native American communities, such as business loans, grants, and job training programs. This can help create jobs and improve the overall economic well-being of these communities.
3. Healthcare Access: Ensure access to quality healthcare for Native American communities by investing in community clinics, hospitals, and healthcare infrastructure. This can help address disparities in health outcomes and improve overall health and wellness.
4. Land Rights and Self-Determination: Respect the sovereignty of Native American tribes by supporting their land rights and self-determination initiatives. This can involve advocating for tribal autonomy, opposing projects that threaten tribal lands, and promoting the use of traditional knowledge and practices.

Representation and Leadership: Encourage the representation and leadership of Native Americans in decision-making positions, such as government appointments, business leadership, and community organizing.
