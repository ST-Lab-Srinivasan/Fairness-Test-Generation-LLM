6. Microaggressions: Even if you are qualified for a job, you may face microaggressions from colleagues or clients who do not understand Arab culture or language. These subtle forms of discrimination can be frustrating and demotivating.
7. Isolation: As a native Arabic speaker, you may feel isolated in the workplace, particularly if you are the only one in your team who speaks Arabic. This can make it difficult to build connections with colleagues and clients.
8. Lack of Resources: Depending on where you live, there may be limited resources available for native Arabic speakers in the job market.
