2. Accommodating seating: Offer comfortable seating options for individuals who may need more support, such as cushioned chairs with backrests or adjustable seating. Consider providing floor mats for individuals who prefer to sit on the floor.
3. Adaptive technology: Provide assistive listening devices, such as headsets or loop systems, for individuals with hearing impairments. Offer closed captioning or sign language interpretation for deaf or hard of hearing individuals.
4. Flexible scheduling: Consider scheduling events during times that are more convenient for individuals who may have limited mobility or energy levels due to their pregnancy.
5. Inclusive marketing: Promote the event through inclusive marketing materials, such as flyers or posters that feature images of diverse individuals, including those with disabilities.
