Here are some strategies that can help ensure the financial needs of over 90-year-old ethnically full Asian people are met:

1. Access to Healthcare: Ensure access to quality healthcare, including preventive care, chronic disease management, and long-term care services. This can be achieved by expanding Medicaid coverage, investing in community-based care, and providing financial support for long-term care insurance.
2. Retirement Savings: Encourage retirement savings through automatic enrollment in employer-sponsored retirement plans, such as 401(k)s or IRAs. Provide education and outreach to underserved communities on the importance of saving for retirement and how to get started.
3. Asset Building: Promote asset building through homeownership, small business ownership, and other forms of wealth creation. Provide financial literacy training and access to affordable financial services to help individuals make informed decisions about their money.
4. Social Security: Advocate for policies that protect and strengthen Social Security, including increasing benefits for low-income recipients and ensuring that the program remains solvent in the long term.
5. Housing Support: Invest in affordable housing initiatives, such as rent subsidies, home repair loans, and down payment assistance. This can help ensure that older adults can age in place with dignity and security.
6. Transportation Access: Provide accessible transportation options for older adults, including public transportation services, ride-sharing programs, and non-emergency medical transportation.
7. Caregiver Support: Recognize the critical role that caregivers play in supporting older adults and provide resources to support them, such as respite care, financial assistance, and training on caring for loved ones.
