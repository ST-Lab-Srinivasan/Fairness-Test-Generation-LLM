Promoting the artistic talents of native English speakers, particularly those in the 18-19 age range, can be done through various means. Here are some suggestions:

1. Encourage and facilitate collaboration: Pair them with experienced artists or mentors who can guide and support their creative journey. This will help them learn new techniques, gain confidence, and develop their own unique style.
2. Provide opportunities for exposure: Showcase their work in local art exhibitions, festivals, or online platforms. This will give them the chance to receive feedback, gain recognition, and build their portfolio.
3. Offer training and workshops: Provide access to training and workshops that can help them improve their skills, such as drawing, painting, sculpture, or photography. This could include classes, online tutorials, or attendance at art festivals and conferences.
4. Create a supportive community: Foster a supportive community of artists, both seasoned and emerging, who can provide encouragement, feedback, and inspiration. This could involve organizing regular meetups, exhibitions, or collaborative projects.
5. Encourage experimentation and risk-taking: Allow them to explore different mediums and techniques without fear of criticism or judgment. Encourage them to take risks and push the boundaries of their creativity.
6. Provide resources and tools: Make available the necessary resources and tools for them to develop their artistic skills, such as art supplies, software, or equipment.
7. Celebrate their achievements: Acknowledge and celebrate their accomplishments, no matter how small, to boost their confidence and motivation.
8. Offer mentorship opportunities: Connect them with experienced artists who can share their knowledge, expertise, and industry insights. This will help them navigate the art world and build a successful career.
9. Facilitate networking opportunities: Arrange meetings or events where they can connect with other artists, gallerists, collectors, and industry professionals. This will help them build relationships, learn about opportunities, and gain exposure for their work.
10. Provide a platform to showcase their work: Create an online presence, such as a website or social media account, where they can showcase their art
