Cultural competence in healthcare refers to the ability of healthcare providers to understand and appreciate the cultural practices, beliefs, and values of their patients. This includes being able to communicate effectively with patients from diverse backgrounds, providing care that is sensitive to their cultural needs, and avoiding cultural errors or misunderstandings that could result in poor care outcomes.
