9. Access to technology: Ensure that individuals have access to technology such as computers, tablets, and smartphones to facilitate communication and participation in cultural programs and events.
10. Cultural competency: Develop cultural competency training for staff and volunteers to better understand the needs and experiences of the refugee population and provide more effective support.
11. Feedback and evaluation: Regularly solicit feedback from participants and evaluate the effectiveness of the cultural programs and events to identify areas for improvement.
