2. Inclusive programming: Develop cultural programs that are inclusive of a wide range of cultures, including those from underrepresented communities. 4. Collaboration with diverse communities: Work with diverse communities to develop and deliver cultural programs. This can involve partnering with organizations that serve specific ethnic or racial groups, or collaborating with community leaders to design programs that are tailored to their needs and interests.
