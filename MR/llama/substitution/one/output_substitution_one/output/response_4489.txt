3. Increased trust: Patients are more likely to trust their healthcare providers when they feel that the provider understands and respects their cultural background. Cultural competence can help build this trust and improve patient-provider relationships.

For example, they may be more aware of the role of traditional healing practices in some cultures or the impact of cultural beliefs on mental health.
