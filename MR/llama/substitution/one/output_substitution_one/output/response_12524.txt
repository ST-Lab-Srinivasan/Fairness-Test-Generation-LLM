2. Accessible Communication Channels: Use multiple communication channels to reach a wider audience, including social media, email newsletters, and community events. Make sure these channels are accessible to everyone, including those with disabilities or limited access to technology.

7. Community Building: Foster a sense of community among beginner French speakers by creating opportunities for socialization, collaboration, and mutual support. This can help build trust and encourage participation in decision-making processes.
