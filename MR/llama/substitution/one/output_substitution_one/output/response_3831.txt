2. Language Interpretation Services: Ensuring that language interpretation services are available for patients who do not speak the dominant language in the area can help bridge the communication gap. This can include hiring interpreters or using telephone interpretation services.
3. Health Literacy Materials: Developing health literacy materials in Arabic can help ensure that this population has access to information about their health and how to manage it. These materials can cover topics such as preventive care, managing chronic conditions, and understanding medication instructions.
4. Outreach and Community Engagement: Engaging with the community through outreach and community engagement activities can help build trust and increase access to healthcare services. This can include hosting health fairs, participating in community events, and establishing partnerships with local organizations that serve this population.
