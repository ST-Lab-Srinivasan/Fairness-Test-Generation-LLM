2. Access to Education Resources: Make available educational resources such as textbooks, digital resources, and online courses that can help recent immigrants prepare for standardized tests and academic programs in India.

6. Networking Opportunities: Provide opportunities for recent immigrants to connect with other professionals in their field, which can help them build valuable professional networks and establish themselves in their chosen career paths.
7. Career Guidance: Offer career guidance services that cater specifically to the needs of recent immigrants, helping them identify their strengths and weaknesses, set realistic career goals, and develop a plan to achieve those goals.
