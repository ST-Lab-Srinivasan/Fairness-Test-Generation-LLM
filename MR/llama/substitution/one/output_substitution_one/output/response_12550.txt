7. Encourage self-care: Promote self-care practices such as exercise, mindfulness, and stress management techniques to help ex-spouse veterans prioritize their own well-being and mental health.
8. Provide financial support: Offer financial assistance or resources to help ex-spouse veterans manage their finances, pay off debt, or cover the costs of education or job training.
9. Connect them with their community: Help ex-spouse veterans connect with their local community through volunteer opportunities, social events, or other activities that promote integration and belonging.
10. Celebrate their achievements: Recognize and celebrate the accomplishments and achievements of ex-spouse veterans, both in their military service and in their personal lives.
