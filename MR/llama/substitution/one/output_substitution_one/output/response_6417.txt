2. Economic Empowerment: Encourage entrepreneurship and economic opportunities for marginalized communities. This can be done through access to microfinance, training programs, and mentorship.

5. Advocacy and Policy Change: Advocate for policy changes that address the root causes of social exclusion, such as discriminatory laws and practices. This can involve working with government agencies, civil society organizations, and community leaders to push for policies that promote inclusivity and equity.
