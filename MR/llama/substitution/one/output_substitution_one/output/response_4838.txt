3. Visual Aids: Utilize visual aids, such as pictures or diagrams, to help beginner Chinese speakers understand medical information and procedures. This can be especially helpful for those who are visual learners or have difficulty understanding spoken language.

These staff members can act as cultural brokers and interpreters, helping to bridge the gap between the patient's culture and the healthcare system.
