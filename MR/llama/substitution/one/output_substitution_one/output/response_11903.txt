4. Community engagement: Involve the immigrant community in the planning and decision-making process for recreational activities. This can help ensure that the activities meet their needs and preferences.
5. Education and outreach: Provide educational materials and outreach programs to educate the immigrant community about the available recreational activities and how they can participate.
