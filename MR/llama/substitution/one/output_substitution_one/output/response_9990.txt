Supporting the educational aspirations of beginner English speakers can be challenging, especially when they are solo. Here are some ways to help them achieve their goals:

1. Provide access to quality resources: Offer a variety of English language learning materials such as textbooks, audio and video recordings, and online resources like grammar guides and vocabulary builders.
2. Encourage consistent practice: Regular practice is essential for improving language skills. Encourage beginners to set aside time each day to practice speaking, listening, reading, and writing in English.
3. Practice with them: Spend time practicing with the beginner English speakers. You can practice conversations, role-playing, or simply have a conversation about their interests.
4. Provide feedback: Offer constructive feedback on their pronunciation, grammar, and vocabulary usage. Be patient and encourage them to keep practicing.
5. Use technology: Utilize language learning apps, online courses, and websites that provide interactive exercises and quizzes to help beginners improve their English skills.
6. Connect them with native speakers: Arrange for the beginner English speakers to practice speaking with native English speakers. This will help them improve their pronunciation, intonation, and comprehension.
7. Create a supportive environment: Encourage a positive and supportive learning atmosphere. Celebrate small victories and acknowledge progress, which can motivate the beginner English speakers to continue improving.
8. Foster curiosity and interest: Encourage beginners to explore different aspects of the language such as idioms, phrasal verbs, and cultural references. This will help them develop a deeper understanding of English and make learning more enjoyable.
9. Offer opportunities for reflection: Provide space for beginner English speakers to reflect on their progress, identify areas for improvement, and set realistic goals.
10. Be patient and consistent: Learning a language takes time and effort. Be patient with the beginners and provide consistent support throughout their learning journey.
11. Use visual aids: Visual aids such as flashcards, pictures, and videos can help beginner English speakers learn new words and phrases in an engaging way.
