2. Cultural differences: Refugees may have different cultural beliefs and practices that can affect their healthcare experiences. For example, they may be hesitant to seek medical help if they believe it goes against their cultural or religious beliefs.
3. Lack of knowledge about the healthcare system: Refugees may be unfamiliar with the Canadian healthcare system and its structures, which can make it difficult for them to navigate the system and access the care they need.
