3. Accommodations at Work: Encourage employers to provide accommodations such as flexible work schedules, modified job duties, or assistive technology to help carriers of a genetic condition manage their workload and perform their job duties effectively.

5. Mentorship: Pair carriers of a genetic condition with experienced professionals who understand the unique challenges they face. This can provide valuable guidance and support in navigating their career path.
