However, this can lead to higher out-of-pocket costs for patients, which can be a significant challenge for those who cannot afford it.

However, this can also lead to a lack of standards and quality control, which can be challenging for patients who want to ensure they are receiving high-quality care.

This can lead to limited access to these services for some individuals, particularly those who cannot afford them.
