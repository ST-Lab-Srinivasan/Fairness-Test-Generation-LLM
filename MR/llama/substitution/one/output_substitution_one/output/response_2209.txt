6. Community outreach: Engage in community outreach efforts to connect with Native American recent refugees and provide them with information on available resources and opportunities for civic engagement.
7. Leadership development programs: Offer leadership development programs that help them build skills and confidence in their ability to participate in civic life.
8. Advocacy and policy change: Advocate for policies that support the civic engagement of Native American recent refugees, such as voting rights legislation or language access provisions.
9. Collaboration with community organizations: Work with community organizations that serve Native American populations to provide civic engagement opportunities and support.
10. Digital resources: Create digital resources, such as websites, social media accounts, or mobile apps, that provide information and tools for civic engagement, including voter registration, election information, and advocacy opportunities.

By implementing these strategies, you can help support the civic engagement of Native American recent refugees to the US and ensure they have equal access to political participation and representation.
