3. Support services: Provide support services specifically designed for ex-military mixed-American heritage students, such as counseling, academic advising, and mentorship programs. These services can help students navigate the challenges of transitioning to civilian life and higher education.
4. Campus resources: Ensure that campus resources are accessible and inclusive for ex-military mixed-American heritage students. This can include providing accommodations such as flexible scheduling, quiet study spaces, and mental health services.

Community building: Foster a sense of community among ex-military mixed-American heritage students by creating opportunities for them to connect with peers and mentors who share similar experiences and backgrounds. 6. Professional development: Offer professional development opportunities for educators and administrators to learn about the unique needs and experiences of ex-military mixed-American heritage students. This can help them better understand how to support these students in their academic and personal lives.
