8. Intergenerational activities: Organize activities that bring together people of different age groups, such as elderly English speakers and beginner learners. This can help build intergenerational connections and foster a sense of community.
9. Gamification: Use gamification techniques, such as language learning games or quizzes, to make the learning process more engaging and enjoyable.
10. Personalized support: Provide personalized support and feedback to help them improve their language skills and build confidence in using English.

By implementing these strategies, you can help support the civic engagement of beginner English speakers with no known genetic conditions and encourage them to become active members of society.
