3. Enhanced social cohesion: Cultural diversity can help to promote social cohesion by fostering a sense of community and shared identity among different groups. This can help to build trust and cooperation between people from different backgrounds.

5. Increased resilience: Cultural diversity can help people to develop resilience and adaptability in the face of challenges. Living in a diverse society requires people to be flexible and adaptable, which can help to build resilience and coping skills.
6. Preservation of cultural heritage: For Native Americans and recent refugees, cultural diversity can help to preserve their cultural heritage and traditions.
