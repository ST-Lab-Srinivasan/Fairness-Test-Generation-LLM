2. Language Barriers: Many refugees arrive in the US with limited English language proficiency, which can create barriers to accessing healthcare. To address this issue, organizations such as the IRC and the Refugee Health Alliance provide interpreter services and language training to help refugees communicate effectively with healthcare providers.
