Lack of financial stability: Despite their best efforts, moderate-income individuals may still face financial instability due to factors such as fluctuating income, high living costs, or unexpected expenses. 4. Limited career growth: Moderate-income individuals may find it difficult to advance in their careers due to a lack of opportunities or resources. They may feel stuck in their current roles with limited prospects for advancement.
