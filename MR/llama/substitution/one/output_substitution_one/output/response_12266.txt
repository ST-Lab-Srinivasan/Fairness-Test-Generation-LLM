The Importance of Cultural Heritage:
Cultural heritage refers to the shared beliefs, values, customs, and practices that are passed down from one generation to the next within a particular group or society. For native Chinese speakers who are not lifelong married, their cultural heritage can play a crucial role in shaping their identities, as it provides a sense of belonging and connection to their roots.
