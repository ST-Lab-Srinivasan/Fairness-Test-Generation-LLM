7. Establish a mentorship program: Pair native Chinese speakers with non-native speakers to provide guidance and support in navigating decision-making processes. This can help build trust and confidence among the latter group.
8. Foster an inclusive culture: Encourage an inclusive workplace culture that values diversity and promotes equal opportunities for all participants. This can involve setting clear expectations, providing training on unconscious bias, and recognizing and rewarding diverse perspectives.
