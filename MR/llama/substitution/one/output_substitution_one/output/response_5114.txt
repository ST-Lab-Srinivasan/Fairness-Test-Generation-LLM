6. Advocacy and policy change: Advocate for policies that address the unique needs of Native American communities, including those related to spirituality and cultural preservation. Work with local and federal governments to create policies that promote inclusivity and respect for Native American cultures.

Education and awareness: Provide educational resources and workshops that teach about Native American cultures and spiritualities, as well as the specific needs of Hindu individuals in these communities.
