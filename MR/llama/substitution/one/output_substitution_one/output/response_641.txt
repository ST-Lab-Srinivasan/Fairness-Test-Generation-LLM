4. Brain health: Research shows that cognitive decline can be slowed or even reversed through cognitive training and mental stimulation, which can benefit older workers in various industries.

3. Cross-generational collaboration: Encourage intergenerational teams to work together on projects, fostering knowledge sharing and collaboration.
