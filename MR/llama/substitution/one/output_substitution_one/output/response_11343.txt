4. Financial support: Provide financial assistance in the form of grants, scholarships, or loans to help independent non-low-income individuals cover the costs associated with pursuing their career aspirations.
5. Flexible work arrangements: Encourage employers to offer flexible work arrangements that can accommodate the needs of independent non-low-income individuals, such as remote work, part-time schedules, or compressed workweeks.
6. Professional certifications and licenses: Provide support for independent non-low-income individuals to obtain professional certifications and licenses that can help them advance in their chosen fields.
7. Mental health support: Address the mental health challenges faced by independent non-low-income individuals by providing access to counseling services, stress management techniques, and mental health resources.
8. Childcare support: Offer childcare services or support for independent non-low-income individuals who may struggle to balance work and family responsibilities.
