There are many successful community initiatives led by beginner English speakers who are not deep in poverty. Here are a few examples:

1. Language exchange programs: Many communities have organized language exchange programs where beginners can practice speaking English with native speakers or more advanced learners. These programs provide an opportunity for language learners to improve their speaking skills and make new friends.
2. Book clubs: Starting a book club is a great way for beginner English speakers to practice reading and discussing books in English. Many libraries and community centers offer book clubs that are open to the public, or you can start your own with a group of friends.
3. Volunteer opportunities: Many organizations offer volunteer opportunities for beginners to practice their English skills while giving back to the community. Examples include mentoring programs, animal shelters, and environmental groups.
4. Cultural events: Attending cultural events such as festivals, concerts, or art exhibitions can be a great way for beginner English speakers to practice their language skills in a fun and relaxed environment. Many communities have events specifically designed for language learners.
5. Online communities: Joining online communities of beginners can provide a supportive environment to practice speaking and listening to English. Many websites offer language exchange programs, grammar exercises, and conversation practice with other learners.
6. Language learning apps: There are many language learning apps available that offer interactive lessons and exercises for beginner English speakers. Some popular apps include Duolingo, Babbel, and Rosetta Stone.
7. Community garden projects: Starting a community garden project is a great way to bring together beginners who are interested in gardening and the environment. This can provide an opportunity to practice speaking and listening to English while working on a shared project.
8. Art classes: Taking art classes or workshops can be a fun way for beginner English speakers to practice their language skills while exploring their creativity. Many communities offer art classes specifically designed for language learners.
9. Cooking classes: Cooking classes can provide an opportunity for beginner English speakers to practice their language skills while learning new recipes and cooking techniques. Many communities offer cooking classes specifically designed for language learners.
