2. Political Education: Mentors can provide political education to mentees, helping them understand the history of socialism and its various forms, including democratic socialism. 3. Organizing Skills: Mentors can teach mentees about organizing skills, such as how to plan and execute campaigns, how to work with local communities, and how to build coalitions. 4. Leadership Development: Mentors can help mentees develop leadership skills, such as public speaking, communication, and decision-making. This can involve discussing the qualities of effective leaders, such as empathy, integrity, and a commitment to justice.
