2. Emergency Funds: Provide emergency funds or financial assistance programs to help service members cover unexpected expenses during their pregnancy, such as medical bills or baby supplies. 4. Childcare Support: Provide childcare support services, such as on-base childcare facilities or subsidies for off-base care. This can help service members who are pregnant or have young children manage their work and family responsibilities more effectively.
5. Health Insurance: Ensure that health insurance coverage is available to all service members, including those in their third trimester of pregnancy. This can include prenatal care, delivery, and postpartum care to help them maintain good health and well-being for themselves and their baby.
