Volunteerism plays a crucial role in supporting advanced age individuals, particularly those over 90 years old and in extreme ultra-liberal communities. Here are some ways volunteerism can make a difference:

1. Social Connection: Volunteering provides opportunities for older adults to connect with others, build relationships, and feel part of a community. This is especially important for those who may be experiencing social isolation or loneliness.
2. Purpose and Meaning: Volunteering can give older adults a sense of purpose and meaning in their lives. It allows them to contribute to society and make a positive impact, which can help maintain cognitive function and overall well-being.
3. Health Benefits: Studies have shown that volunteering can have physical and mental health benefits for older adults. It can reduce stress, improve mood, and even lower blood pressure.
4. Skills Maintenance: Volunteering can help older adults maintain their cognitive and motor skills by engaging in activities that challenge them. This can delay the onset of cognitive decline and keep them mentally active.
5. Financial Support: Many volunteer opportunities provide a stipend or other financial benefits, which can be especially important for older adults who may have reduced income or resources.
6. Community Engagement: Volunteering allows older adults to remain connected to their community and feel invested in it. This can help them maintain their sense of identity and belonging.
7. Intergenerational Connections: Volunteering can provide opportunities for older adults to connect with younger generations, fostering intergenerational relationships and understanding.
8. Personal Growth: Volunteering can help older adults develop new skills, build confidence, and pursue personal growth opportunities.
9. Legacy Building: Through volunteering, older adults can leave a lasting legacy in their communities by contributing to important causes and making a positive impact.
10. Fun and Enjoyment: Volunteering can be a fun and enjoyable experience for older adults, providing opportunities to try new things, meet new people, and make a difference.
