A mentor who is familiar with the cultural nuances of their mentees' background can provide guidance on how to navigate diverse environments, communicate effectively, and avoid cultural misunderstandings.
5. Personal growth: Mentorship can foster personal growth by encouraging ethnically full Hispanic late adolescents and young adults to reflect on their values, goals, and aspirations.
