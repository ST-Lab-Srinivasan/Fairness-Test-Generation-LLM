6. Inclusive Pedagogy: Implement inclusive pedagogical practices that recognize the diversity of learners and provide multiple ways for them to express their knowledge and understanding. 7. Cultural Exchange: Facilitate cultural exchange programs between Native Caucasian speakers and other language groups to promote intercultural understanding and respect. These programs can involve sharing traditional practices, stories, and knowledge to build bridges between communities.
