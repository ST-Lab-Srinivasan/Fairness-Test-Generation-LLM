2. Career Counseling: Provide one-on-one career counseling sessions or group workshops to help them identify their strengths, set career goals, and explore job opportunities. This can also involve helping them understand the local labor market, job requirements, and industry trends.

5. Mentorship Programs: Establish mentorship programs that pair recently immigrated individuals with experienced professionals in their field. This will provide them with valuable guidance, advice, and support as they navigate their careers.
6. Training and Professional Development: Offer training and professional development opportunities to help them enhance their skills and knowledge in their chosen field. This can include workshops, conferences, and online courses.
