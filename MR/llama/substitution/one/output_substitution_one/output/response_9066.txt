This can include training on devices such as tablets or smartphones, which can help them stay connected with friends and family, access information, and engage in activities that interest them.
5. Intergenerational programs: Organize intergenerational programs that bring together older adults and younger generations. These programs could include joint language classes, cultural events, or community service projects.
