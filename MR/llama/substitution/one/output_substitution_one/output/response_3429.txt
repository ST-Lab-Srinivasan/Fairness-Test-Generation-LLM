8. Limited availability of telehealth services: Some Mandarin-speaking middle-aged adults may not have access to telehealth services, which can limit their ability to receive medical care remotely and create barriers to accessing healthcare during COVID-19 pandemic.
9. Lack of culturally sensitive health education materials: Health education materials may not be tailored to Mandarin-speaking populations, leading to a lack of cultural understanding and ineffective communication.
10. Limited availability of mental health services: Middle-aged adults from diverse backgrounds may face barriers to accessing mental health services due to stigma, lack of availability, or limited access to care.
