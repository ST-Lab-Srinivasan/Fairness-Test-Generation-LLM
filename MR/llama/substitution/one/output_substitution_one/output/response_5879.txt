Community engagement and partnerships: Develop partnerships with local Native communities to provide opportunities for student involvement in community service projects, internships, and mentorship programs. 4. Inclusive curriculum design: Develop an inclusive curriculum that reflects the histories, cultures, and experiences of Native communities. This includes incorporating Indigenous perspectives, knowledge systems, and pedagogies into course design and delivery.
5. Support services for veteran students: Provide support services specifically designed for veteran students, such as academic advising, career counseling, mental health resources, and peer mentorship programs.
