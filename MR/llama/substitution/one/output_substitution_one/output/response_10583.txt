6. Use technology: Utilize technology, such as video conferencing or online platforms, to provide virtual support and engagement opportunities for beginner English speakers who may have difficulty attending in-person events.
7. Build relationships: Take the time to build personal relationships with beginner English speakers and understand their specific needs and challenges. This can help tailor support and resources to meet their unique requirements.
8. Provide advocacy support: Offer advocacy support to help beginner English speakers navigate complex systems, such as healthcare or social services, by providing interpretation or translation services, or by accompanying them to appointments or meetings.
