7. Limited Access to Job Opportunities: Single people may have limited access to job opportunities, especially if they do not have a strong professional network or connections.
8. Difficulty Balancing Career and Personal Goals: Recent single people may find it challenging to balance their career goals with their personal goals, such as traveling, starting a family, or pursuing hobbies.
