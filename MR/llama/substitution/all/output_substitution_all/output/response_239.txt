1. Understand the experiences of veterans: Divorce can be a difficult and isolating experience, especially for older veterans who may have spent decades serving their country and building a life together with their former spouse. It's essential to understand the emotional impact of divorce on this population and tailor programs accordingly.
2. Provide support groups: Offering support groups specifically for recently divorced or long-time divorced veterans can help them connect with others who understand their experiences. These groups can provide a safe space for veterans to share their feelings, receive emotional support, and build connections with others.
3.
