4. Multigenerational Programming: Plan events that bring together people of different ages and backgrounds. 5. Volunteer Support: Recruit volunteers from the local community to assist with event planning and execution. This can help build connections between younger green card holders and older adults, as well as provide opportunities for socialization and skill-building.
