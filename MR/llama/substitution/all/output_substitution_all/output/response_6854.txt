Introduction:
Marriage is a significant life event that can have a profound impact on an individual's life. However, the impact of marriage may be more pronounced for recently married ultra-liberal individuals due to their strong beliefs and values. In this study, we explore the impact of marital status on the lives of recently married ultra-liberal individuals and examine how their marital status affects various aspects of their lives.

Methods:
We conducted a qualitative study using semi-structured interviews with 20 recently married ultra-liberal individuals. Participants were recruited through snowball sampling and purposeful sampling techniques. The interviews were audio recorded, transcribed, and analyzed for themes related to the impact of marital status on various aspects of their lives.

Results:
Our findings reveal several impacts of marital status on the lives of recently married ultra-liberal individuals. These impacts include:

1. Changes in Social Interactions: Marriage can lead to changes in social interactions, as newlyweds may find themselves spending more time with their partner and less time with friends and family. This can be particularly challenging for ultra-liberal individuals who value their independence and autonomy.
2. Financial Implications: Marriage can have significant financial implications, particularly for ultra-liberal individuals who may have previously relied on themselves financially. The shift to a dual-income household can be challenging, especially if the couple has different spending habits and financial priorities.
3. Identity Crisis: Marriage can also lead to an identity crisis for ultra-liberal individuals, particularly if they feel constrained by societal expectations and norms. They may struggle with balancing their individualistic values with the traditional roles and expectations that come with marriage.
4. Conflicts Over Values: Marital status can also lead to conflicts over values and beliefs, particularly if the couple has different political views or religious beliefs. Ultra-liberal individuals may find themselves at odds with their partner on issues such as abortion, gun control, or LGBTQ+ rights.
