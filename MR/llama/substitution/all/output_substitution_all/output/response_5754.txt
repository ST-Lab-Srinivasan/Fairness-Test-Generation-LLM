2. Shared values and beliefs: Cultural heritage encompasses the shared values, beliefs, and practices of a particular group or community. These shared beliefs and values can shape an individual's worldview and influence their behavior and decision-making. 3. Customs and traditions: Cultural heritage includes customs and traditions that are passed down from one generation to the next.
