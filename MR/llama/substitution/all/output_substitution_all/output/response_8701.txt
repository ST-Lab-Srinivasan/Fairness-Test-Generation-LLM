Introduction

Mixed Asian heritage individuals face unique challenges in the healthcare system, particularly those from lower middle-income backgrounds. These challenges can range from linguistic and cultural barriers to economic and social disparities. This article will explore the specific challenges faced by not lower middle-income mixed Asian heritage people in the healthcare system and identify potential solutions to address these issues.
