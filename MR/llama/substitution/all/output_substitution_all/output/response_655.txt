2. Access to Emergency Services: Ensure that emergency services are accessible to nomadic Hindu refugees, including medical care, food, water, shelter, and other essential needs. 5. Partnerships with Local Organizations: Collaborate with local organizations that have experience working with nomadic Hindu refugee communities.
