6. Foster community connections: Encourage individuals to connect with their communities through cultural events, volunteering, or other activities that promote social support and belonging.
7. Provide educational resources: Offer workshops or classes that teach coping skills, stress management techniques, and mental health literacy for Latino communities.

9. Respect cultural practices: Recognize and respect the cultural practices and beliefs that may impact an individual's mental health, such as spirituality or traditional healing methods.
