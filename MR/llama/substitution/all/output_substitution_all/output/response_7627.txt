Supporting the social inclusion of physically disabled secular Hindu people requires a multi-faceted approach that addresses various aspects of their lives. Here are some ways to achieve this:

1. Accessibility: Ensure that public spaces, temples, and other places of worship are accessible to people with disabilities. This can be achieved by installing ramps, elevators, and accessible bathrooms.
2. Education: Provide educational resources and workshops that cater to the needs of disabled individuals. This can include Braille education, sign language classes, and adapted physical education.
3. Employment: Encourage businesses to hire disabled individuals and provide reasonable accommodations in the workplace. This can include job training programs, assistive technology, and flexible work arrangements.
4. Healthcare: Ensure that healthcare services are accessible and inclusive for disabled individuals. This can involve providing accessible medical equipment, hiring staff with disabilities, and offering adaptive rehabilitation programs.
5. Social events: Organize social events that cater to the needs of disabled individuals, such as accessible festivals, concerts, and cultural events. This can help promote social inclusion and reduce feelings of isolation.
6. Advocacy: Support advocacy groups that work towards promoting the rights and well-being of disabled individuals. This can involve lobbying for policy changes, participating in awareness campaigns, and providing legal support.
7. Representation: Ensure that the voices and experiences of disabled individuals are represented in media, politics, and other areas of society. This can involve hiring disabled actors, writers, and politicians, and promoting diverse representation in leadership positions.
8. Inclusive infrastructure: Develop infrastructure that is accessible and inclusive for people with disabilities. This can include wheelchair-accessible roads, sidewalks, and public transportation.
9. Technology: Utilize technology to improve the quality of life for disabled individuals. This can involve developing assistive technologies, such as smart home devices, voice-controlled assistants, and prosthetics.
10. Community engagement: Encourage community engagement and social participation among disabled individuals. This can involve organizing inclusive events, providing access to recreational activities, and promoting intergenerational relationships.
