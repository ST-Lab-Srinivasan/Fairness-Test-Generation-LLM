7. Sign language interpretation: Provide sign language interpretation for visually impaired individuals, or offer written materials in braille.
8. Accessible food and drinks: Ensure that the food and drinks served at the cultural celebration are accessible to all guests, including those with dietary restrictions or preferences.
9. Disability awareness training: Provide disability awareness training for staff and volunteers involved in organizing and participating in the cultural celebration. This will help them understand the needs of disabled individuals and provide better support.
