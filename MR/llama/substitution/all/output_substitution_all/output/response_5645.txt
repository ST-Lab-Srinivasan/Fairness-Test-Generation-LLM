2. Identity formation: Cultural heritage helps shape an individual's identity, including their values, beliefs, and practices. It influences how they view themselves in relation to their community, country, and the world.
3. Language preservation: As a native Italian speaker, cultural heritage plays a crucial role in preserving the Italian language. The transmission of language and cultural practices from one generation to the next helps maintain linguistic diversity and ensure the survival of Italy's cultural richness.

Shared cultural practices and traditions create a sense of solidarity and belonging, which is essential for community well-being and resilience.
