Title: The Role of Cultural Heritage in Shaping Identities of Native Undocumented Immigrants

Introduction:
Cultural heritage plays a significant role in shaping the identities of Native recent undocumented immigrants to the US. These individuals often carry with them a rich cultural legacy that influences their beliefs, values, and practices. This legacy is a vital component of their identity and serves as a source of strength and resilience in the face of adversity.

Native recent undocumented immigrants to the US often face significant challenges in their daily lives, including discrimination, marginalization, and trauma. However, despite these obstacles, they maintain a strong sense of identity and cultural heritage that serves as a source of strength and resilience. This paper explores the role of cultural heritage in shaping the identities of these individuals and highlights its importance in their lives.
