2. Microfinance and Access to Credit: Many ethnically marginalized communities face barriers when trying to access traditional banking services, including microfinance and credit. 3. Job Creation and Entrepreneurship: Encouraging job creation and entrepreneurship in ethnically marginalized communities can help create economic opportunities and reduce poverty. This can involve providing resources and support for start-ups, small businesses, and social enterprises that prioritize hiring from these communities.
