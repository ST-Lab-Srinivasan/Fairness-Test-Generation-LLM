Understand the needs of newly divorced individuals: It is essential to understand the unique challenges that newly divorced individuals face, such as emotional distress, financial strain, and social isolation. 6. Encourage self-care: Emphasize the importance of self-care and provide opportunities for participants to engage in activities that promote relaxation, stress reduction, and overall well-being.
