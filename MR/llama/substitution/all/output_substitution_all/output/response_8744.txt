3. Employee Resource Groups: Encourage the formation of employee resource groups (ERGs) for culturally African-American employees to provide a supportive network and platform for discussion and advocacy on workplace issues.
4. Mentorship Programs: Establish mentorship programs that pair culturally African-American employees with senior leaders or mentors from diverse backgrounds to provide guidance, support, and career advancement opportunities.

Performance Management System: Develop a performance management system that takes into account the unique experiences and challenges faced by culturally African-American employees, such as bias in evaluations, unequal opportunities for advancement, or lack of representation in leadership positions.
