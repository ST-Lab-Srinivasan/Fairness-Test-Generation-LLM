As a beginner English speaker, there may be several challenges that you face in the job market. Here are some common ones:

1. Language barrier: The most significant challenge for many beginner English speakers is the language barrier. You may struggle to communicate effectively in English, which can limit your ability to express yourself and understand others.
2. Limited vocabulary: As a beginner, you may not have a comprehensive vocabulary, making it difficult to convey your ideas and opinions.
3. Grammar and syntax mistakes: Beginner English speakers may make frequent grammar and syntax mistakes, which can undermine their credibility and professionalism in the workplace.
4. Limited cultural knowledge: As an English speaker from a non-English speaking country, you may have limited knowledge of Western culture and customs, which can lead to misunderstandings or unintended offense.
5. Lack of confidence: Beginner English speakers may feel self-conscious about their language skills, leading to a lack of confidence in the workplace.
6. Difficulty understanding idioms and colloquialisms: Idioms and colloquialisms are common in English, and beginner speakers may struggle to understand them, which can lead to miscommunication.
7. Struggling to understand accents and dialects: Depending on the region or country you are from, you may be unfamiliar with different accents and dialects of English spoken in other parts of the world.
8. Limited access to resources: As a beginner English speaker, you may not have access to the same resources as native speakers, such as English language media, books, and websites.
9. Difficulty following instructions or negotiating: Beginner English speakers may struggle to follow instructions or negotiate in English, which can lead to misunderstandings or missed opportunities.
10. Limited networking opportunities: As a beginner English speaker, you may have limited opportunities to network and connect with other professionals in the industry, which can hinder your career advancement.

It's important to remember that these challenges are common for many beginner English speakers, and with practice, patience, and persistence, you can overcome them.
