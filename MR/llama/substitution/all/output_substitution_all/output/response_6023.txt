The social inclusion of recent undocumented immigrants to the US can be supported through various means. Here are some strategies that can help:

1. Language Access: Provide language classes, interpreters, and translators to help them communicate effectively in their daily lives.
2. Healthcare Access: Ensure they have access to affordable and quality healthcare services, including mental health support.
3. Education and Employment: Offer job training programs, educational resources, and career counseling to help them integrate into the workforce.
4. Legal Support: Provide legal aid and representation to help them navigate the complex US immigration system.
5. Community Engagement: Facilitate opportunities for social interaction and community engagement through events, volunteer work, and cultural activities.
6. Advocacy and Policy Change: Advocate for policies that support the rights and dignity of undocumented immigrants, such as a pathway to citizenship or protection from deportation.
7. Economic Empowerment: Provide financial literacy training, entrepreneurship opportunities, and access to small business loans to help them establish economic stability.
8. Housing Support: Offer affordable and safe housing options, as well as legal support for tenant rights and eviction prevention.
9. Childcare and Family Support: Provide childcare services and family-friendly resources to help them balance work and family responsibilities.
10. Mental Health Services: Offer mental health counseling and psychological support to help them cope with the stress of migration and discrimination.
11. Cultural Competency Training: Provide cultural competency training for employers, educators, and service providers to ensure they are equipped to serve this population effectively.
12. Community Building: Facilitate community-building events and activities that celebrate their culture and promote intercultural understanding.
13. Advocacy Coalitions: Build advocacy coalitions with local organizations, community groups, and religious organizations to amplify their voices and influence policy change.
14. Public Education Campaigns: Launch public education campaigns to raise awareness about the experiences and challenges faced by undocumented immigrants, as well as the importance of inclusion and respect for their rights
