3. Better decision-making: A diverse workforce can lead to more informed decision-making, as professionals can draw upon their collective experiences and knowledge to make more well-rounded choices.

5. Personal growth: Engaging with culturally diverse colleagues can be a transformative experience for professionals, leading to personal growth and self-awareness. It can challenge their assumptions and biases, fostering a more inclusive and empathetic approach to work and life.
