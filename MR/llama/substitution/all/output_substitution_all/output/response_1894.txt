Supporting the civic engagement of cisgender beginner Arabic speakers can be done through various means, including:

1. Voter registration drives: Organize events or campaigns to encourage beginners to register to vote, and provide information on the voting process and the importance of civic participation.
2. Civics education: Offer workshops or classes on civics and citizenship, covering topics such as government structures, political processes, and the role of citizens in society.
3. Community service opportunities: Provide opportunities for beginners to participate in community service projects, such as volunteering at polling stations, cleaning up local parks, or participating in other community events.
4. Advocacy and activism: Encourage beginners to get involved in advocacy and activism efforts related to issues they care about, such as immigration reform, LGBTQ+ rights, or environmental protection.
5. Leadership development: Provide training and resources for beginners to develop their leadership skills, such as public speaking, strategic planning, and decision-making.
6. Cultural events: Host cultural events that promote civic engagement and community involvement, such as festivals, parades, or cultural fairs.
7. Collaboration with local organizations: Partner with local organizations to provide resources and support for beginners looking to get involved in their communities.
8. Online platforms: Create online platforms or social media groups where beginners can connect with each other, share resources, and stay informed about civic issues.
9. Mentorship programs: Establish mentorship programs that pair experienced individuals with beginners, providing guidance and support as they navigate the civic engagement process.
10. Civic hackathons: Organize events where beginners can come together to brainstorm and develop innovative solutions to civic issues, such as improving voter turnout or increasing community participation in local government.

By implementing these strategies, organizations can support the civic engagement of cisgender beginner Arabic speakers and help them become active and informed citizens.
