Title: Examining the Role of Cultural Heritage in Shaping the Identities of Devout Practicing Indian Individuals with Temporary Visas to the US

Introduction:
Cultural heritage plays a significant role in shaping the identities of individuals, particularly those from diverse backgrounds. For Native recent temporary visa holders to the US, their cultural heritage can have a profound impact on their sense of self and belonging. This paper will explore the role of cultural heritage in shaping the identities of devout practicing Indian individuals with temporary visas to the US.

The Importance of Cultural Heritage:
Cultural heritage refers to the shared values, beliefs, customs, and practices that are passed down from one generation to the next within a particular group or community. For individuals with temporary visas to the US, their cultural heritage can provide a sense of belonging and identity, especially during times of transition or uncertainty.

Indian Culture and Identity:
India is a country with a rich and diverse culture, shaped by its history, religion, and geography. Hinduism, the dominant religion in India, has a significant impact on Indian culture and identity. Hinduism emphasizes the importance of dharma (duty), artha (material wealth), kama (pleasure), moksha (liberation), and karma (actions and their consequences). These principles shape Indian values and beliefs, which in turn influence the identities of individuals within the country.

The Role of Cultural Heritage for Temporary Visa Holders:
For temporary visa holders to the US, their cultural heritage can serve as a source of comfort and familiarity in an unfamiliar environment. It can provide a sense of belonging and identity, especially during times of transition or uncertainty. For devout practicing Indian individuals, their cultural heritage can also play a significant role in shaping their spiritual beliefs and practices.

Impact on Identity:
The cultural heritage of India can have a profound impact on the identities of temporary visa holders to the US. It can shape their values, beliefs, customs, and practices, and provide a sense of belonging and identity.
