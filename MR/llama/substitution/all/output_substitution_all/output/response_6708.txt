Moderate-income individuals face several challenges in accessing and affording quality healthcare within the existing healthcare system. Here are some of the key challenges:

1. Limited financial resources: Moderate-income individuals may have sufficient income to afford basic necessities but struggle to cover the costs of healthcare, including premiums, copays, deductibles, and coinsurance. They may have to make difficult choices between spending money on healthcare or other essential needs such as housing, food, and education.
2. Lack of insurance coverage: Many moderate-income individuals are not covered by employer-sponsored insurance plans or do not qualify for Medicaid or other government-funded healthcare programs. This leaves them vulnerable to medical expenses without adequate financial protection.
3. Complex and confusing billing systems: Moderate-income individuals may find it difficult to navigate complex and opaque billing systems, leading to surprise bills or unexpected costs. They may also struggle to understand their insurance coverage and how to maximize their benefits.
4. Limited access to specialized care: Moderate-income individuals may face barriers in accessing specialized medical care, such as specialist appointments or procedures, due to financial constraints or lack of availability in their area.
5. Long wait times for non-emergency procedures: Moderate-income individuals may experience long wait times for non-emergency procedures, such as elective surgeries or routine check-ups, due to limited resources and high demand.
6. Limited access to preventive care: Moderate-income individuals may have difficulty accessing preventive care services, such as annual physicals or cancer screenings, due to financial constraints or lack of availability in their area.
7. Difficulty finding providers who accept their insurance: Moderate-income individuals may face challenges in finding healthcare providers who accept their insurance coverage, leading to higher out-of-pocket costs or delayed care.
