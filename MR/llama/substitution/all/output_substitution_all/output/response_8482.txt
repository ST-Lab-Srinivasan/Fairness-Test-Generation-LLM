8. Conflict resolution: Cultural diversity can serve as a bridge between different ethnic groups, helping to resolve conflicts through cultural exchange and understanding.
9. Identity expression: Cultural diversity provides individuals with the means to express their identity and connect with their cultural roots, leading to increased self-esteem and confidence.
