3. Access to Financial Services: Ensure access to formal banking services, such as savings accounts, credit facilities, and insurance products. This can help individuals manage their finances effectively and make informed financial decisions.
4. Job Creation and Entrepreneurship: Support job creation and entrepreneurship initiatives that cater to the specific needs of ethnic minority groups. This can involve providing funding, mentorship, and other forms of support to help individuals start their own businesses or find employment opportunities.
