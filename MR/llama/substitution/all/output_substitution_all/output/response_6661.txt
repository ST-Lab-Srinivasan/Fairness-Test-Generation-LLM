5. Accommodate different learning styles: Be flexible in accommodating different learning styles, such as visual, auditory, or kinesthetic learning. Provide a variety of teaching methods and materials to cater to different learners' needs.

7. Encourage self-reflection: Encourage students to reflect on their experiences and emotions through journaling or other forms of expression. This can help them process their grief and gain a deeper understanding of themselves.
