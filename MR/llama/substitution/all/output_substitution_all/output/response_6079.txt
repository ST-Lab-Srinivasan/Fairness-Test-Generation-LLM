2. 3. Schedule events around military schedules: Be mindful of the unpredictable nature of military schedules and plan events accordingly. For instance, consider holding events during non-peak hours or on weekends when possible. This can help minimize the likelihood of partners being unable to attend due to last-minute deployments or other military obligations.
4. Offer spousal support: Provide resources and support specifically designed for partners, such as a designated area for spouses to connect with each other or workshops focused on topics relevant to their needs, like managing a household during frequent moves or supporting their partner's military career.
