4. Foster a sense of belonging: Create opportunities for them to connect with their host community and feel a sense of belonging. This can be done through cultural events, volunteer work, or social activities that promote intercultural dialogue.
5. Build trust: Establish trust by providing reliable information, being transparent about your organization's goals, and consistently following through on your commitments.
6. Leverage technology: Utilize digital platforms to reach a wider audience, provide accessible resources, and facilitate communication between community members.

8. Foster peer-to-peer support: Encourage mentorship programs where experienced community members can guide and support newer arrivals in navigating the political landscape.
