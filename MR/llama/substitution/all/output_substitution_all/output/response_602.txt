2. Accessibility: Ensure that your programs are accessible to participants who may have mobility or hearing impairments. Provide interpreters or CART services for those who are deaf or hard of hearing, and make sure that your event locations are wheelchair-accessible.

4. Education and resources: Offer educational workshops or classes on topics relevant to this population, such as financial planning, estate management, or grief counseling. Provide resource materials or referrals for further support, and make sure that your programs are well-publicized within the community.
