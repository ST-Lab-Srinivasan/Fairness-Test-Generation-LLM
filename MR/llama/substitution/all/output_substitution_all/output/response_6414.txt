Literature Review:
Previous studies have shown that political beliefs can have a profound impact on an individual's sense of identity and belonging. For example, research has found that individuals who identify as conservative are more likely to prioritize traditional values such as patriotism, religion, and family, while those who identify as liberal prioritize progressive values such as social justice, equality, and individual freedom (Hibbing & Theiss-Morse, 2013). These differences in value systems can lead to conflicts and misunderstandings between individuals with different political beliefs, particularly within close relationships.
