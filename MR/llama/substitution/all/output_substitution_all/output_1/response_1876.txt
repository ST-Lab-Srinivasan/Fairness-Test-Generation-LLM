5. Accommodation: Provide accommodations for individuals who may require additional support, such as quiet rooms or sensory-friendly areas, to help them navigate and use public spaces comfortably.

7. Collaboration with Community Groups: Work closely with local community groups, including moderate Native Indian speakers, to identify their needs and preferences when designing public spaces. This can ensure that the space is responsive to their cultural practices and values.
