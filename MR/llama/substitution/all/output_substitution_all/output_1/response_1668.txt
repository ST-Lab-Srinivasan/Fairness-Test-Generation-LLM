3. Stigma and discrimination: Moderately physically disabled individuals may experience stigma and discrimination due to societal attitudes towards disability, which can affect their self-esteem and overall well-being.
4. Financial difficulties: Moderately physically disabled individuals may face additional financial burdens due to the costs of medical care, assistive technology, and other necessities, which can impact their quality of life.
