It provides them with a space where they can connect with others who share similar cultural practices, values, and beliefs. This can lead to the formation of support networks, which are crucial in helping individuals navigate their job search process.

5. Personal Growth: Engaging with cultural diversity can foster personal growth by challenging individuals to step outside their comfort zones. It encourages them to develop empathy, tolerance, and understanding of different cultures, which are essential for success in today's globalized world.
