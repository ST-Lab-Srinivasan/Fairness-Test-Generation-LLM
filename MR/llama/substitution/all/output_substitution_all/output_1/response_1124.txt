1. Vocational Training and Skills Development: Provide training and skills development programs in areas such as entrepreneurship, leadership, communication, and technical skills. 2. Financial Inclusion: Encourage financial inclusion by providing access to bank accounts, loans, and other financial services. This can be done through partnerships with financial institutions and microfinance banks.
3. Business Development Services: Offer business development services such as mentorship, coaching, and training to help individuals start or grow their own businesses. This can include assistance with business planning, market research, and access to capital.

5. Entrepreneurship Support: Provide support for entrepreneurs, including access to funding, mentorship, and training. This can help to create new businesses and jobs in the community.
6. Community Development: Invest in community development programs that provide infrastructure, education, and healthcare services. This can help to reduce poverty and inequality, and create a more stable economic environment.
