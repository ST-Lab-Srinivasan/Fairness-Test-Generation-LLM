2. Participatory Programming: Involve participants in the planning and implementation of programs to ensure they are relevant and meaningful to their needs and interests. This can be done through surveys, focus groups, or other participatory methods.
3. Interpretation and Translation Services: Provide interpretation and translation services for non-English speakers to ensure equal access to information and resources.
