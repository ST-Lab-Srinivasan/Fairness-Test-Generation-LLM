4. Community Engagement: Volunteering can help to foster a sense of community and connection among elderly individuals, particularly those who may be living in isolation or have limited access to social activities.
5. Skill-Sharing: Volunteers can share their skills and knowledge with elderly individuals, such as teaching computer skills, crafting, or gardening, which can help to maintain cognitive function and provide a sense of accomplishment.
