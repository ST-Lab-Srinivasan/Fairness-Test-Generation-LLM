7. Offer support without judgment: Offering support without judgment can help non-binary individuals feel more comfortable opening up about their mental health.
8. Encourage self-care: Non-binary individuals may face unique challenges when it comes to self-care. Encouraging them to prioritize self-care and providing resources for doing so can help improve their mental health.
