3. Develop cultural competency training: Providing cultural competency training for decision-makers can help them understand the unique needs and experiences of native Arabic speakers and recent immigrants. 6. Engage in policy advocacy: Advocating for policies that promote equity and inclusion can help ensure that the voices of native Arabic speakers and recent immigrants are heard at the local, state, and federal levels. This can include advocating for language access services, cultural competency training for public officials, and policies that address systemic barriers to success.
