Creating more inclusive social policies for employed cisgender individuals requires considering their unique needs and experiences. Here are some recommendations:

4. Offer training on gender diversity: Provide training on gender diversity and inclusivity for all employees, including management and supervisors. This can help create a more understanding and supportive work environment.
5. Create a diverse and inclusive workplace culture: Foster a workplace culture that values diversity and inclusivity. Encourage open communication, mutual respect, and empathy among all employees.

7. Offer flexible work arrangements: Provide flexible work arrangements for all employees, including those who may need to balance work and family responsibilities. This can help create a more inclusive and supportive work environment.
