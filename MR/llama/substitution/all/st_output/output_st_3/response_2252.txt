Language barriers: Even though many companies have diversified their workforce and recognize the importance of multilingualism, there are still language barriers that can hinder the career advancement of independent Arabic speakers. 2. Cultural differences: Independent native Arabic speakers may face cultural differences in the workplace, particularly if they are working in a country where the dominant culture is different from their own. Limited access to training and development opportunities: Independent native Arabic speakers may have limited access to training and development opportunities, particularly if these opportunities are only offered in languages other than Arabic.
