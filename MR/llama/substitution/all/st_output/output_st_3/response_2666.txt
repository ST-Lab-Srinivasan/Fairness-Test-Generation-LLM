2. Use interpreters or translators: If the individuals do not speak the dominant language in your community, use interpreters or translators to facilitate communication during decision-making processes.
3. Provide educational materials: Develop informative brochures, pamphlets, or websites that provide information on the rights and responsibilities of pregnant women, particularly those who are devout Muslims. This can help educate these individuals about their options and choices.
4. Engage community leaders: Identify respected community leaders within the devout Muslim community and engage them in decision-making processes. They can provide guidance and support to ensure that the voices of these individuals are heard.
5. Use technology: Utilize technology, such as video conferencing or online surveys, to reach a wider audience and make it easier for pregnant women who may face barriers to participation, such as mobility issues or lack of access to transportation.
