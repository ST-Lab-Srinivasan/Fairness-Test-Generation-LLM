4. Community Outreach: Engage in community outreach efforts to connect with local immigrant communities and provide them with information about educational resources available to them. 5. Parent Involvement: Encourage parent involvement in schools by providing opportunities for parents to learn about their children's education, participate in school events, and contribute to decision-making processes. This can include training on culturally responsive teaching practices, understanding the impact of trauma on learning, and strategies for supporting immigrant students' academic success.
