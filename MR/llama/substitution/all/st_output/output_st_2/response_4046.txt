3. Offer flexible scheduling options: If possible, work with your spouse to find a schedule that accommodates their professional aspirations. For example, if they need to attend evening events or conferences, offer to take care of household responsibilities during those times.
4. Help manage work-life balance: High-income professionals often have demanding jobs and long hours. Be supportive of your spouse's career goals while also ensuring that they have time for personal activities and self-care. Encourage them to take breaks, use vacation time, and prioritize their well-being.
