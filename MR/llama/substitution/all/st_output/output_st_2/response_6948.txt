2. 3. Create a safe learning environment: Ensure that the learning environment is safe and comfortable for students, providing adequate seating and rest areas as needed. Encourage students to take breaks and stretch when necessary to avoid any discomfort or complications during class.
4. Address special needs: Identify and address any special needs of the students, such as those related to pregnancy, childbirth, or breastfeeding. Provide resources and support to help students manage these challenges while learning English.
