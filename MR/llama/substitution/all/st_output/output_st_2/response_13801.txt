Supporting the educational aspirations of non-widowed people in Nigeria can be achieved through various means, including:

1. Providing access to quality education: Non-profit organizations and government initiatives can work towards building schools and providing educational resources, such as textbooks, computers, and internet connectivity, to underserved communities.
2. Offering scholarships and financial aid: Organizations can provide scholarships or financial aid to individuals who cannot afford higher education, enabling them to pursue their academic dreams.
3. Mentorship programs: Experienced professionals can volunteer their time and expertise to mentor younger individuals, providing guidance and support in their educational journey.
4. Vocational training: Providing vocational training opportunities can help individuals acquire skills that are in demand in the job market, enabling them to earn a decent living without needing to pursue a traditional degree.
5. Life skills training: Offering training in life skills such as entrepreneurship, financial management, and communication can help individuals develop the necessary skills to succeed in their personal and professional lives.
6. Community-based initiatives: Community-based initiatives can help bridge the gap between education and the workforce by providing opportunities for individuals to gain practical experience and build connections with potential employers.
7. Government support: Governments can play a crucial role in supporting educational aspirations by investing in infrastructure, providing scholarships, and creating policies that promote access to quality education.
8. Private sector involvement: The private sector can collaborate with educational institutions and government agencies to provide funding, resources, and expertise to support educational initiatives.
9. Technology-based interventions: Utilizing technology can help increase access to education, particularly in underserved areas, by providing online courses, virtual classrooms, and other digital learning tools.
10. Advocacy and awareness: Raising awareness about the importance of education and advocating for policies that promote equal access to quality education can help create a supportive environment for individuals to pursue their educational aspirations.

By implementing these strategies, it is possible to support the educational aspirations of non-widowed people in Nigeria and help them achieve their goals.
