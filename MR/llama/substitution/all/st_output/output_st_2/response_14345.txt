6. Support groups: Facilitate access to support groups for newly divorced individuals, which can provide a safe space to share experiences and emotions.
7. Personalized coaching: Offer personalized coaching or mentoring sessions to help them build their confidence in speaking Arabic and navigating social situations.
8. Networking opportunities: Arrange networking events or introductions to professionals in fields they are interested in, which can help them expand their professional networks.
9. Skill-building workshops: Organize workshops or classes that teach skills such as public speaking, communication, and conflict resolution, which can help build their confidence and social skills.
10. One-on-one coaching: Provide one-on-one coaching sessions to help them improve their Arabic language skills and build their confidence in speaking.
11. Cultural immersion: Plan cultural immersion trips or events where they can practice their Arabic skills and learn more about the culture.
12. Mentorship: Connect them with a mentor who is fluent in Arabic and has experience in navigating social situations, which can provide guidance and support.
13. Role-playing exercises: Conduct role-playing exercises to help them practice social interactions and build their confidence in speaking Arabic.
