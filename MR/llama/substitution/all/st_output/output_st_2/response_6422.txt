2. Foster a welcoming environment: Ensure that your community programs are accessible and inclusive for all participants, regardless of their marital status or relationship history. Provide a comfortable and respectful atmosphere where everyone feels valued and appreciated.

5. Provide resources for personal growth: Offer workshops or seminars on topics such as communication, conflict resolution, financial management, or mental health. These sessions can help couples strengthen their relationship and navigate challenges more effectively.
6. Support intergenerational connections: Encourage older, long-time married couples to mentor or connect with younger couples who may be earlier in their relationship journey. This can foster a sense of community and shared wisdom across generations.
