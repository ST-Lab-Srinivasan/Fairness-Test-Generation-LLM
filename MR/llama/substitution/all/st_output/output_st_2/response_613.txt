5. Offer specialized courses: Provide tailored courses for different levels of proficiency or interests, such as business Chinese, medical Chinese, or travel Chinese. This will help individuals focus on their specific needs and goals.
6. Collaborate with local organizations: Partner with local community centers, libraries, or other organizations to reach a broader audience and create more opportunities for language learning and cultural exchange.
7. Provide resources and materials: Offer access to quality language learning resources such as textbooks, apps, and online courses. Also, provide cultural-specific materials like dictionaries, grammar guides, and listening materials to support learners' progress.
