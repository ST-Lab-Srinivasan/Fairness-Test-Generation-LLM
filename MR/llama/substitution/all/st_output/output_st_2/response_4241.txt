3. Increased access to care: By being culturally competent, healthcare providers can better understand the cultural beliefs and practices of their patients. Enhanced patient outcomes: Cultural competence can lead to more accurate diagnoses and treatment plans, as healthcare providers are better able to understand the cultural context of their patients' symptoms and behaviors.
