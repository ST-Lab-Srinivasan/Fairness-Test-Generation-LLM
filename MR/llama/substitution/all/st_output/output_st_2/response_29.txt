6. Representation in Decision-Making Bodies: Ensure that native Arabic speakers are represented in decision-making bodies such as government agencies, community organizations, or advocacy groups. This can help ensure that their voices are heard and their perspectives are taken into account.
7. Capacity Building: Provide capacity building opportunities for native Arabic speakers to develop leadership and decision-making skills. This can help individuals take on leadership roles and participate in decision-making processes at the local, state, or national level.
