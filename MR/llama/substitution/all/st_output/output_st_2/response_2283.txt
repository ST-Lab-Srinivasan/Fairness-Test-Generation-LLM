2. Cultural differences: As a recent immigrant, you may be unfamiliar with the cultural norms and practices of the US legal system. These resources can provide valuable information and support, but they may not always be available or accessible to non-English speakers. You can try contacting local legal aid organizations or seeking help from a lawyer who specializes in immigration law.
5. Fear of deportation: As an immigrant, you may face fears about being deported or removed from the US. This fear can be especially challenging if you are undocumented or have a pending immigration case.
