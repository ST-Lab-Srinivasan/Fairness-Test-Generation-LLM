This will provide an opportunity for beginners to connect with other Spanish speakers who share similar interests and backgrounds.
5. Online resources: Create online resources, such as podcasts, social media groups, or websites, that offer Buddhist teachings and practices in Spanish. This way, beginners can access the material at their convenience and from anywhere in the world.
