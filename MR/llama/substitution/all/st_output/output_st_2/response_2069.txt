Creating more inclusive community events for civilian upper lower middle-income people requires a thoughtful and deliberate approach. Here are some strategies to consider:

1. Identify the needs of your target audience: Understand the specific needs, preferences, and challenges of your target audience. Conduct surveys, focus groups, or interviews to gather insights and create buyer personas.
2. Offer affordable tickets and pricing options: Lower-middle-income individuals may struggle with the cost of attending events. Consider offering discounted tickets, special promotions, or package deals for groups.
3. Provide accessible venues and activities: Ensure that your event is held in a location that is easily accessible by public transportation or has ample parking. Offer activities that cater to different age groups and abilities, such as ASL interpreters, Braille signage, or wheelchair-accessible areas.
4. Promote diversity and inclusion: Use marketing materials that reflect the diversity of your target audience. Highlight the inclusivity of your event in your promotional language and visuals.
5. Partner with local organizations: Collaborate with local community groups, schools, or non-profits to reach a broader audience. This can also help you gain credibility and build relationships within the community.
6. Offer educational content: Provide valuable information and resources that are relevant to your target audience. This could include workshops, seminars, or presentations on topics such as financial planning, wellness, or career development.
7. Create a welcoming atmosphere: Develop a welcoming environment by providing comfortable seating, adequate lighting, and a safe and clean space. Encourage attendees to mingle and connect with each other.
8. Provide food and beverages options: Offer a variety of snacks and beverages that cater to different dietary needs and preferences. Consider offering vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, or halal options.
9. Use social media and digital marketing: Leverage social media platforms and digital marketing channels to reach your target audience. Create engaging content, such as videos or infographics, that showcase the benefits of your event.
