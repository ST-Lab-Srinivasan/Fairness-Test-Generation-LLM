2. Simplify language: Use simple language and avoid technical jargon to make it easier for beginner English speakers to follow conversations and contribute their ideas.
3. Encourage participation: Create a safe and inclusive environment where beginner English speakers feel comfortable sharing their thoughts and opinions. Encourage them to participate in discussions by asking open-ended questions and actively listening to their responses.

5. Use interpreters or translators: If necessary, use interpreters or translators to facilitate communication between beginner English speakers and those who are fluent in the language. This can be especially helpful during meetings or discussions where important decisions are being made.
