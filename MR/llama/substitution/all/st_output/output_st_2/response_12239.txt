Supporting the economic empowerment of Native recent immigrants to the US can be achieved through various means. Here are some strategies that can help moderate-income individuals:

1. Access to Financial Services: Many Native recent immigrants face challenges in accessing financial services such as bank accounts, loans, and credit cards due to lack of access or discrimination. Providing access to affordable financial services can help them manage their finances better and build credit history.
2. Education and Training: Offering educational programs that teach entrepreneurship, vocational skills, and language proficiency can help Native recent immigrants gain the knowledge and confidence they need to start their own businesses or find better-paying jobs.
3. Microfinance Programs: Microfinance programs that provide small loans or grants to low-income individuals can help them establish their own businesses or invest in income-generating activities.
4. Job Training and Placement: Providing job training and placement services can help Native recent immigrants gain employment skills and connect with potential employers.
5. Tax Incentives: Offering tax incentives to businesses that hire or invest in low-income communities can encourage economic growth and job creation in these areas.
6. Affordable Housing: Providing affordable housing options can help Native recent immigrants build stable roots in their new community and reduce the financial burden of housing costs.
7. Healthcare Access: Ensuring access to affordable healthcare can improve the overall well-being of Native recent immigrants and help them focus on their economic goals without worrying about medical expenses.
8. Community Development: Investing in community development projects such as infrastructure, transportation, and public services can create jobs and improve living standards for Native recent immigrants and the wider community.
9. Mentorship Programs: Establishing mentorship programs that connect successful entrepreneurs or professionals with aspiring Native recent immigrants can provide valuable guidance and support in navigating the US economy.
