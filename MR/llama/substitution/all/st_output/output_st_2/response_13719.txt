5. Create a mentorship program: Pair experienced employees who have gone through the same challenges with new reservists, allowing them to share their experiences and offer guidance.
6. Offer professional development opportunities: Provide training and workshops on topics relevant to pregnant employees, such as time management, stress management, or communication skills.
