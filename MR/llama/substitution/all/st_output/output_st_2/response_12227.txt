Supporting the economic empowerment of ethnically full African American middle-class income individuals requires a multi-faceted approach that addresses the historical and systemic barriers that have hindered their economic advancement. Here are some strategies that can help:

1. Education and Skills Development: Provide access to quality education and skills development programs, including vocational training, apprenticeships, and degree programs that lead to in-demand fields such as technology, healthcare, and finance.
2. Entrepreneurship Support: Offer resources and support for aspiring entrepreneurs, including mentorship, networking opportunities, and access to capital. This can help create jobs and wealth within the community.
3. Professional Networking: Foster professional networking opportunities to help individuals connect with potential employers, investors, and partners. This can lead to new job opportunities, business collaborations, and access to valuable resources.
4. Access to Capital: Increase access to capital for African American-owned businesses through funding programs, grants, and low-interest loans. This can help individuals start or grow their businesses, which can create jobs and wealth within the community.
5. Wealth Building Strategies: Implement wealth building strategies such as financial literacy training, retirement savings plans, and homeownership programs. These can help individuals build intergenerational wealth and achieve long-term financial stability.
6. Advocacy and Policy Change: Advocate for policy changes that address systemic barriers to economic empowerment, including discrimination, unequal access to education and job opportunities, and inadequate social safety nets.
7. Community Development Initiatives: Support community development initiatives that promote economic growth and stability within the African American community. This can include community land trusts, cooperative businesses, and other alternative forms of ownership and wealth building.
8. Mentorship and Leadership Development: Provide mentorship and leadership development opportunities to help individuals build their skills and confidence, which can lead to greater economic empowerment.
