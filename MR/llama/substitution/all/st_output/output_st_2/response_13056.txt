Leadership development is essential for the growth and success of any organization, including military units. Here are some ways to support the leadership development of active-duty Nigerian people:

1. Leadership Training Courses: Provide training courses that focus on developing leadership skills such as communication, decision-making, problem-solving, and strategic planning. These courses can be tailored to the specific needs of the military and delivered by experienced instructors.
2. Mentorship Programs: Pair experienced leaders with junior officers or enlisted personnel for mentorship and coaching. This will provide them with guidance and support as they navigate their careers.
3. Leadership Workshops: Organize workshops that focus on specific leadership skills such as time management, team building, and conflict resolution. These workshops can be interactive and include practical exercises and case studies.
4. Leadership Circles: Establish a leadership circle where officers and enlisted personnel can share their experiences, challenges, and successes. This will provide a platform for them to learn from each other and develop their leadership skills.
5. Coaching and Feedback: Provide regular coaching and feedback to military personnel on their leadership style and performance. This can help them identify areas for improvement and develop their skills further.
6. Leadership Assessments: Conduct regular assessments of military leaders to evaluate their strengths and weaknesses. This will provide valuable insights into their leadership abilities and help identify areas for development.
7. Promote from Within: Encourage the promotion of talented officers and enlisted personnel from within the military. This will provide them with opportunities for advancement and help retain top talent.
8. Foster a Culture of Leadership: Encourage a culture of leadership throughout the military by recognizing and rewarding leaders who demonstrate exceptional leadership skills.
9. Provide Resources: Provide resources such as books, articles, and videos on leadership development to military personnel. This will help them stay up-to-date with the latest thinking and best practices in leadership development.
10. Collaborate with Civilian Organizations: Collaborate with civilian organizations that specialize in leadership development to provide additional resources and expertise to military leaders.
