5. Provide context: Give beginners context for cultural practices by explaining why certain traditions are important to the culture and how they relate to daily life.

7. Be patient and respectful: Be patient with beginners and respectful of their abilities and cultural backgrounds. Avoid making assumptions about their understanding of Arabic culture based on their age or level of proficiency.
8. Involve the community: Involve the local Arabic-speaking community in the celebration by inviting them to participate or share their experiences with beginners. This can help create a more immersive and inclusive environment.
