Supporting the leadership development of recently retired veterans and recent immigrants to Canada can be a valuable investment in their personal and professional growth. Here are some ways to support their leadership development:

1. Mentorship Programs: Pair them with experienced professionals who can provide guidance, support, and mentorship. This can help them navigate the Canadian job market and understand the cultural nuances of the country.
2. Skills Training: Offer training programs that focus on developing their leadership skills, such as communication, problem-solving, and decision-making. These skills are essential for success in any profession.
3. Networking Opportunities: Provide opportunities for them to connect with other professionals in their field, attend industry events, and participate in professional organizations. This can help them build a network of contacts and establish themselves in their new community.
4. Career Coaching: Offer one-on-one coaching sessions to help them identify their strengths, set career goals, and develop a plan to achieve those goals.
5. Entrepreneurship Support: Encourage and support them in starting their own businesses. This can be particularly beneficial for retired veterans who have extensive experience and knowledge that they can leverage to create a successful venture.
6. Volunteer Opportunities: Encourage them to get involved in volunteer work, which can help them build connections within the community, develop new skills, and gain leadership experience.
7. Language Training: If they are recent immigrants, provide language training to help them improve their proficiency in English or French, which is essential for success in the Canadian job market.
8. Career Fairs: Host career fairs that bring together employers and job seekers. This can provide an opportunity for them to meet potential employers, learn about job opportunities, and showcase their skills and experience.
9. Internship Programs: Offer internship programs that provide them with hands-on work experience and the opportunity to build their professional network.
10. Leadership Development Courses: Provide courses or workshops that focus on leadership development, such as time management, team building, and conflict resolution. These skills are essential for success in any profession.
