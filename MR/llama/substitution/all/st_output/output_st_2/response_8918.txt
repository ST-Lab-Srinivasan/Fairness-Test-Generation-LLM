These laws provide benefits such as protection from discrimination, the right to request a leave of absence without jeopardizing their job or benefits, and the ability to terminate leases or contracts without penalty during deployment. Ensure that these laws are enforced and that military spouses are aware of their rights under them.

3. Military Lending Act: The Military Lending Act (MLA) provides protection against predatory lending practices targeting military members. Ensure that all financial institutions operating on military installations comply with the MLA's provisions, such as capping interest rates and fees for military-specific loans.
