	* Encourage the creation of gender-inclusive clubs or organizations that provide a platform for students to connect and support each other.

	* Offer counseling services specifically tailored to the needs of unmarried gender-nonconforming students.

	* Provide resources and support for parents and families of unmarried gender-nonconforming students, including workshops on how to support their child's education and well-being.
	* Encourage open communication and collaboration between schools and families to ensure the best possible outcomes for students.
6. Celebrate gender diversity:
	* Organize events and activities that celebrate and promote gender diversity, such as gender-inclusive proms or other school-wide events.
