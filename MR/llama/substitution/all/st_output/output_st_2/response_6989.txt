Creating more inclusive cultural celebrations for early teen Native Spanish speaker individuals involves incorporating their cultural practices and language into the celebration, as well as actively involving them in the planning and execution of the event. Here are some suggestions:

4. Foster cross-cultural understanding: Plan activities that promote cross-cultural understanding and exchange between early teen Native Spanish speakers and other participants. 6. Make it accessible: Ensure that the celebration is accessible to all participants, including those with disabilities. Provide accommodations such as American Sign Language (ASL) interpretation, braille materials, or audio descriptions for visually impaired individuals.
