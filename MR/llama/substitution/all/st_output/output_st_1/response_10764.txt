2. Entrepreneurship support: Encourage entrepreneurship by providing resources such as business mentorship, access to capital, and networking opportunities. This can help individuals start their own businesses or pursue innovative ideas that can create economic opportunities.
3. Job placement services: Offer job placement services that connect individuals with employers who are committed to hiring and promoting Native Americans. This can include job fairs, resume building workshops, and interview preparation resources.

This can include initiatives such as economic development corporations, housing renovation programs, and community facilities construction.
