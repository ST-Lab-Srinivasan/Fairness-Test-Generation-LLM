4. Cultural Preservation: Many non-liberal, non-middle-class communities have rich cultural heritage and traditions. Volunteers can help preserve these cultural practices by documenting them, teaching younger generations, or supporting community-based initiatives that promote cultural preservation.

This can help build resilience and self-sufficiency within these communities, enabling them to address their own challenges and advocate for their rights more effectively.
