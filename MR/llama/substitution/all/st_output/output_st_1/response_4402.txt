2. Identity-based learning: Create learning opportunities that allow students to explore their cultural identity and its intersection with other identities. This can involve discussions, reflections, and creative expressions such as writing, art, or music.

6. Student-centered learning: Involve students in the design and implementation of educational programs that reflect their cultural background and identity. This can include student-led workshops, projects, or events that celebrate mixed Hispanic heritage.
