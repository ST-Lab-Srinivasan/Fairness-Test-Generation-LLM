5. Practical Language Skills: Teach practical language skills such as ordering food in a restaurant, shopping, and navigating public transportation. These skills will help students feel more confident and independent in their daily lives.
6. Interactive Learning Methods: Use interactive learning methods such as role-playing, group discussions, and multimedia presentations to keep students engaged and motivated.
7. Collaborative Learning: Encourage collaboration among students through group projects and activities. This will help them learn from each other and build a sense of community.
8. Access to Resources: Provide access to resources such as language learning apps, podcasts, and online forums. This will enable students to continue learning outside of class and at their own pace.
9. Regular Feedback: Offer regular feedback and progress reports to help students track their improvement and stay motivated.
10. Celebration of Successes: Celebrate the successes of students, no matter how small, to boost their confidence and reinforce their learning.
