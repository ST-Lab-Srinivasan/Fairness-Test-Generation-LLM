Better health outcomes: Cultural competence can help healthcare providers identify cultural factors that may influence a patient's health behaviors and lifestyle choices, such as diet, exercise, and stress management. 4. Reduced health disparities: Cultural competence can help reduce health disparities by identifying and addressing cultural barriers to care.
