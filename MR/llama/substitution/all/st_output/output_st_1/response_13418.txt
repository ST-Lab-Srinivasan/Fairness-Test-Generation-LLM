2. Offer career counseling: Provide one-on-one or group counseling sessions to help them explore career options, create a career development plan, and set achievable goals.
3. Provide job training and education: Offer training programs, workshops, or certifications that can help them acquire new skills or enhance their existing ones. This could include technical training, language courses, or leadership development programs.

6. Facilitate career fairs and networking events: Organize events that bring together industry professionals, recruiters, and potential employers. This will give them the opportunity to network, learn about job opportunities, and showcase their skills and qualifications.
