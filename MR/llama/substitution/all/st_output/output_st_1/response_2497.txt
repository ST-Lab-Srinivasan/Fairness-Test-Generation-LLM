2. Microfinance: Offer microfinance options such as small loans or grants to help them start or grow their businesses. This can provide them with the financial resources they need to invest in their ideas and grow their wealth.
3. Entrepreneurship Support: Provide mentorship, networking opportunities, and resources to support entrepreneurs who are part of the mixed African-American community. This can help them start and grow successful businesses that create jobs and economic opportunities for others in their community.
4. Job Training and Placement: Offer job training programs that focus on in-demand skills such as technology, healthcare, and advanced manufacturing. This can help them compete in the job market and secure better-paying jobs.
5. Wealth Building Strategies: Provide workshops and resources on wealth building strategies such as investing, budgeting, and saving. This can help them build generational wealth and achieve long-term financial stability.
