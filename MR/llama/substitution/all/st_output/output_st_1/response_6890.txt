2. Mentorship and Networking: Connect them with successful professionals and entrepreneurs who can provide guidance, mentorship, and networking opportunities. This can help them build valuable connections and gain insights into how to navigate the professional world.
3. Access to Capital: Provide access to capital through microfinance programs, crowdfunding platforms, or other financial instruments that can help them start or grow their businesses.
4. Entrepreneurship Support: Offer training, resources, and support for entrepreneurs, including access to incubators, accelerators, and co-working spaces. This can help them turn their ideas into successful businesses.
