Creating more inclusive community events for active-duty military personnel and their families requires careful consideration of their unique needs and challenges. Here are some suggestions on how to make such events more accessible and welcoming:

1. Offer flexible pricing: Offering sliding scale or pay-what-you-can options can make your event more accessible to military families who may have limited financial resources.
2. Provide childcare and transportation options: Military families often have limited access to childcare and transportation, so consider providing these services at your event to make it more convenient for them.
3. Use inclusive language and marketing: Be mindful of the language you use in promoting your event to avoid unintentionally excluding military families. Use language that is inclusive and welcoming, such as "all are welcome" or "family-friendly."
4. Offer military discounts: Consider offering discounts or special offers to military personnel and their families to show appreciation for their service.
5. Provide on-site support services: Military families may have unique needs related to things like mental health, financial planning, or legal assistance. Consider providing on-site support services at your event to help address these needs.
6. Create a military-friendly atmosphere: Take steps to create a comfortable and welcoming atmosphere for military personnel and their families. This could include playing patriotic music, displaying military flags or symbols, or having a designated area for military families to connect with each other.
7. Partner with military organizations: Consider partnering with local military organizations, such as the USO or the Armed Services YMCA, to help promote your event and make it more accessible to military personnel and their families.
8. Offer virtual attendance options: For military personnel who are deployed or living overseas, consider offering virtual attendance options so they can still participate in your event. This could include live-streaming the event or providing online access to content or resources.
