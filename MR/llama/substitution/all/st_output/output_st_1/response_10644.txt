2. Job Fairs and Workshops: Organize job fairs and workshops in different locations throughout the city to provide opportunities for individuals to connect with potential employers, learn about job opportunities, and receive training and coaching.
3. Mentorship Programs: Develop mentorship programs that pair experienced professionals with late 30s out-of-work individuals. This will provide them with valuable guidance, support, and networking opportunities.
4. Training and Skills Development: Offer training and skills development programs in areas such as digital literacy, coding, data analysis, and project management. This will help individuals acquire the skills that are in demand in the job market.
