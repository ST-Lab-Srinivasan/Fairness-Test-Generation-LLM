Analyze the data to identify patterns and trends, which can help policymakers design targeted interventions.
2. Consultation with Stakeholders: Engage in consultations with long-term widowed individuals, NGOs, community groups, and other stakeholders to understand their needs, challenges, and preferences. 3. Multi-Sectoral Approach: Develop policies that address the interconnected issues faced by long-term widowed individuals, such as poverty, social isolation, poor health, and lack of access to education and employment opportunities.
