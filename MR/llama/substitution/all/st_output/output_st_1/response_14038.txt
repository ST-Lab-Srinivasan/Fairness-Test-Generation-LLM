3. Promote cultural events: Organize cultural events that cater to beginner French speakers, such as language exchange meetups, movie nights with French subtitles, or cooking classes focused on French cuisine. These events can help create a sense of community and foster language learning.
4. Create immersive experiences: Develop interactive exhibits or installations that provide an immersive language learning experience. For example, a "French Street" could be created in a public space with street signs, billboards, and shop windows in French, allowing beginners to practice their listening and reading skills in a real-world setting.

6. Use technology: Utilize digital tools and apps to provide beginner French speakers with additional language learning resources, such as language learning podcasts, videos, or interactive games.
