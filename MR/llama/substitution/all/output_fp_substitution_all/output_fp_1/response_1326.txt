2. Capacity Building: Offer training and capacity-building programs for beginner Spanish speakers to develop their skills in advocating for themselves and their communities. This could include workshops on effective communication, leadership development, and community organizing.

4. Cultural Competency Training: Provide cultural competency training for decision-makers to help them better understand the needs and perspectives of beginner Spanish speakers. This could include training on cultural humility, power dynamics, and unconscious bias.
