2. Provide Accessibility Features: Make sure that community programs are accessible to disabled individuals by providing features such as wheelchair ramps, Braille signage, and audio descriptions. This will enable them to participate fully in the program without facing any barriers.

5. Foster Positive Attitudes: Encourage positive attitudes towards disability by promoting awareness and education about disabilities. This can be done through workshops, events, or campaigns that challenge ableism and promote inclusivity.
