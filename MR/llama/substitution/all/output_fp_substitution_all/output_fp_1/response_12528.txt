4. Participatory governance: Incorporate participatory governance mechanisms such as citizen juries, focus groups, or public consultations to ensure that a diversity of voices are heard in decision-making processes.
5. Language support: Provide language support for native Chinese speakers who may not be fluent in the dominant language of the organization or community. This can include translation services, interpreters, or bilingual staff members.
6. Community outreach: Conduct community outreach and engagement activities to reach out to native Chinese speakers and ensure that they are aware of decision-making processes and opportunities to participate.
7. Evaluation and monitoring: Regularly evaluate and monitor decision-making processes to ensure that they are inclusive and that the voices of native Chinese speakers are being heard.
