Title: The Impact of Marital Status on the Lives of Late Pregnancy Native Chinese Speaker Individuals: A Qualitative Study

Introduction:
Marriage and pregnancy are significant life events that can have a profound impact on an individual's life. However, little is known about the specific challenges faced by late pregnancy (third trimester) native Chinese speaker individuals, particularly in relation to their marital status. This study aims to explore the experiences and challenges faced by these individuals, focusing on the impact of marital status on their lives during this critical period.

Research Questions:

1. How do late pregnancy native Chinese speaker individuals experience their marital status during the third trimester?
2. What are the specific challenges faced by these individuals in terms of marital status during late pregnancy?
3. How do cultural and societal norms influence the experiences of late pregnancy native Chinese speaker individuals with regards to their marital status?

Methodology:
This study will employ a qualitative research design, using in-depth interviews to collect data from 15 late pregnancy native Chinese speaker individuals. Participants will be recruited through purposive sampling, ensuring a range of marital statuses (single, married, cohabiting) and varying levels of cultural and societal influence. Interviews will be audio recorded and transcribed verbatim, with thematic analysis conducted to identify key themes and patterns.

Significance:
This study will contribute to the limited research on the experiences of late pregnancy native Chinese speaker individuals, particularly in relation to their marital status. Findings will provide valuable insights into the specific challenges faced by these individuals during this critical period, informing healthcare providers and policymakers on how best to support them.

Expected Outcomes:
This study aims to identify the following outcomes:

1. The impact of marital status on the experiences of late pregnancy native Chinese speaker individuals during the third trimester.
2. The specific challenges faced by these individuals in terms of marital status during late pregnancy, including cultural and societal influences.
