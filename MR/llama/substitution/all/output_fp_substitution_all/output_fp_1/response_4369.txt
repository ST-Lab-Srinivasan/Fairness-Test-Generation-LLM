5. Flexible work arrangements: Offer flexible work arrangements, such as telecommuting or part-time work, to help them balance their career aspirations with their personal responsibilities.
6. Financial support: Provide financial support, such as grants or scholarships, to help them cover the costs of education and training related to their career aspirations.
7. Recognize and reward their efforts: Acknowledge and recognize their efforts and achievements, both on and off duty, to help boost their morale and motivation.

9. Create a supportive community: Foster a supportive community of active-duty individuals who are also pursuing their career aspirations. This can provide them with a sense of belonging and motivation.
