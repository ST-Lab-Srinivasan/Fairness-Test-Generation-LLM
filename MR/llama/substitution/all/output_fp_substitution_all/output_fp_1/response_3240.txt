7. Difficulty following medical advice: Children who are beginners in French may struggle to follow complex medical instructions, such as taking medication or adhering to a treatment plan.

10. Limited advocacy support: Beginner French speaker children may have limited access to advocacy support, such as interpreters or cultural liaisons, which can make it more challenging for them to access healthcare services.
