Introduction:
Marriage and relationships can have a significant impact on an individual's life, including their mental health, social interactions, and overall well-being. In this study, we examined the impact of marital status on the lives of mentally able-bodied native French speakers. We used a mixed-methods approach, combining both quantitative and qualitative data, to explore how marital status affects various aspects of these individuals' lives.

Quantitative Data:
We collected data from 500 mentally able-bodied native French speakers using an online survey. The survey included questions on demographics, mental health, social interactions, and overall well-being. We found that:

1. Marital status is significantly associated with mental health. Individuals who are married or in a long-term relationship tend to have better mental health compared to those who are single or divorced/separated.
2. Marital status is also associated with social interactions. Individuals who are married or in a long-term relationship tend to have more social interactions and feel more connected to their community compared to those who are single.
3. Marital status affects overall well-being. Individuals who are married or in a long-term relationship tend to report higher levels of overall well-being compared to those who are single.

Qualitative Data:
We conducted interviews with 20 mentally able-bodied native French speakers to gain a deeper understanding of how marital status affects their lives. Some key themes that emerged from the interviews include:

1. Social support: Many participants mentioned that being married or in a long-term relationship provides emotional and practical support, which can be particularly important during times of stress or difficulty.
2. Identity and self-esteem: Marital status can impact an individual's sense of identity and self-esteem. Some participants reported feeling more secure and confident within their relationships, while others felt that their marital status affected their self-worth.
