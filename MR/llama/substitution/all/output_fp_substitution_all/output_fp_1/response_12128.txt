2. Career Planning and Development: Provide career planning and development opportunities that are tailored to their current stage of life. Help them identify their strengths, set realistic goals, and explore new career paths that align with their values and priorities.

4. Professional Development: Offer professional development opportunities such as workshops, webinars, or conferences that can help them enhance their skills and knowledge in their current role or prepare for new challenges after pregnancy.
5. Financial Planning: Provide financial planning resources and tools to help them manage their finances during this time. This may include budgeting, saving, and investing strategies that can help them achieve their long-term career goals.
