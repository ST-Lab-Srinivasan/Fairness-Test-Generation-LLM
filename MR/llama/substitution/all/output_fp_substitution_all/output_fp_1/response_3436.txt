Supporting the social inclusion of Devout Muslim Native Arabic Speaker (DMNAS) people requires addressing the systemic and institutional barriers that prevent them from fully participating in society. Here are some ways to achieve this:

1. Language Support: Provide language support services, such as translation and interpretation, to ensure that DMNAS individuals have equal access to information and services in their native language.
2. Cultural Sensitivity Training: Offer cultural sensitivity training to government employees, teachers, and other professionals to help them understand and respect the customs and practices of DMNAS communities.
3. Access to Education: Ensure that DMNAS individuals have access to quality education that caters to their religious beliefs and practices. This may involve providing Islamic studies programs or incorporating Islamic teachings into mainstream curricula.
4. Employment Opportunities: Create job opportunities for DMNAS individuals in fields where they are underrepresented, such as government, education, and healthcare. Provide mentorship and support to help them advance in their careers.
5. Community Outreach: Engage in community outreach programs that promote social inclusion and address the specific needs of DMNAS communities. This may involve organizing cultural events, providing access to community facilities, and establishing partnerships with local organizations.
6. Civic Engagement: Encourage DMNAS individuals to participate in civic life by voting, serving on juries, and holding public office. Provide them with the necessary resources and support to exercise their civic rights.
7. Legal Protections: Advocate for legal protections that promote the social inclusion of DMNAS individuals. This may involve challenging discriminatory laws and policies, advocating for hate crime legislation, and ensuring that DMNAS individuals have equal access to legal services.
8. Healthcare Access: Ensure that DMNAS individuals have access to quality healthcare that caters to their cultural and religious beliefs. Provide interpretation services, halal food options, and gender-segregated facilities in healthcare settings.
9. Housing and Urban Planning: Advocate for inclusive housing policies that address the specific needs of DMNAS communities. Ensure that there is adequate provision of affordable housing
