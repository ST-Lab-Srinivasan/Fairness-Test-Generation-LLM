4. Encourage retirement savings: Many single, African American individuals may not have access to an employer-sponsored retirement plan. Encourage them to save for retirement through other means, such as an individual retirement account (IRA) or a 401(k) plan.
5. Provide financial planning resources: Offer access to financial planning resources, such as books, websites, and workshops, that can help these individuals learn how to manage their finances effectively.
