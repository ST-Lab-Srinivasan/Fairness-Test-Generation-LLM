Help them identify achievable goals that align with their values and interests. Break down these goals into smaller, manageable steps, and provide ongoing support throughout the process.

Offer financial assistance, such as helping with rent or utility bills, to help them get back on their feet. You could also connect them with local resources, such as non-profit organizations or government agencies, that provide financial aid to those in need.

5. Help them develop a new social network: Divorce can lead to feelings of isolation and disconnection from their previous social circle.
