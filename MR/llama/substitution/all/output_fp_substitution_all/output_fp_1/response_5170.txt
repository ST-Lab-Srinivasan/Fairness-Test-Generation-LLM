Supporting communities just above the poverty line, particularly those with a high incidence of disability, can be challenging but crucial for promoting inclusive development. Here are some effective strategies that can help:

1. Capacity Building and Skills Development: Provide training and capacity building programs to enhance the skills and knowledge of community members, particularly those with disabilities. This can include vocational training, life skills, and entrepreneurship development.
2. Microfinance and Entrepreneurship Support: Establish microfinance programs that provide small loans and other financial support to community members, particularly women and people with disabilities, who want to start or expand their businesses. This can help create jobs and income-generating opportunities for the community.
3. Access to Healthcare: Improve access to quality healthcare services, including rehabilitation and mental health support, for people with disabilities. This can help address the physical and mental health needs of the community.
4. Inclusive Education: Promote inclusive education by providing accessible learning environments, accommodating children with disabilities, and training teachers on how to support students with diverse learning needs.
5. Community-Based Rehabilitation (CBR): Implement CBR programs that provide a range of services, including medical rehabilitation, assistive technology, and home-based care, to people with disabilities in their own communities.
6. Empowerment through Advocacy: Strengthen advocacy efforts to promote the rights and inclusion of people with disabilities. This can involve working with local organizations, community leaders, and government agencies to create policies and programs that support inclusive development.
7. Accessibility and Inclusive Urban Planning: Ensure that urban planning and infrastructure are accessible and inclusive for all, including people with disabilities. This can involve designing streets, buildings, and public spaces that are accessible and easy to navigate for people of all abilities.
8. Livelihoods and Enterprise Development: Support community members in developing income-generating activities, such as craft production, agro-enterprises, or other small businesses. This can help create jobs and economic opportunities for the community.
