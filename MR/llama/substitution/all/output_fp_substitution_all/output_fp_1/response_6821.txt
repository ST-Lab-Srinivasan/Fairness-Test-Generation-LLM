Promoting the artistic talents of native English speakers in their late middle age (ages 18-19) can be a rewarding and fulfilling endeavor. Here are some ways to do so:

1. Encourage creative expression: Provide opportunities for these individuals to express themselves through various art forms such as painting, drawing, writing, music, or dance. Encourage them to explore their creativity and showcase their talents.
2. Recognize and celebrate their achievements: Acknowledge and celebrate the achievements of these individuals in their field of art. This can be done through public recognition, awards, or exhibitions.
3. Provide resources and support: Offer resources such as workshops, training, and mentorship programs to help them develop their skills and knowledge in their chosen art form. Additionally, provide emotional support and encouragement to help them stay motivated and inspired.
4. Create opportunities for collaboration: Pair these individuals with younger or more experienced artists to collaborate on projects. This can help them learn new techniques, gain exposure, and build connections within the art community.
5. Foster a sense of community: Create a supportive and inclusive community of artists where they can share their experiences, provide feedback, and learn from each other. This can be done through regular meetups, workshops, or online forums.
6. Encourage intergenerational collaboration: Pair these individuals with younger artists to collaborate on projects. This can help them learn new techniques, gain exposure, and build connections within the art community.
7. Provide a platform for showcasing their work: Offer platforms such as exhibitions, galleries, or online portals to showcase their artwork to a wider audience. This can help increase their visibility and recognition in the art world.
8. Offer professional development opportunities: Provide opportunities for these individuals to develop their skills and knowledge in areas such as marketing, branding, and entrepreneurship. This can help them build a successful career in their chosen art form.
9. Encourage experimentation and risk-taking: Encourage these individuals to experiment with new techniques, styles, and mediums. This can help them stay creative and innovative throughout their lives.
