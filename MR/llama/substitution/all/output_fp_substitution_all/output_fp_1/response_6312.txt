3. Respect for beliefs and practices: Cultural competence recognizes the importance of Islamic beliefs and practices in the lives of young Muslim patients. Reduced cultural misunderstandings: Cultural competence can help healthcare providers avoid cultural misunderstandings or miscommunications that can lead to adverse events or poor patient outcomes.
