6. Foster a sense of community: Organize events or activities that bring temporary visa holders and recent green card holders together with other students, such as cultural festivals, study groups, or social events. This can help create a sense of belonging and community among these students.
7. Address systemic barriers: Advocate for policies and practices that address systemic barriers to educational success faced by temporary visa holders and recent green card holders, such as lack of access to financial aid or discrimination in educational settings.
