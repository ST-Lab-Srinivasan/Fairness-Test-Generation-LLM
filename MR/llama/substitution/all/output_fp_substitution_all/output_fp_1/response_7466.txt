As a country, it is crucial to support and encourage the civic engagement of recently naturalized citizens, including Native Americans and recent refugees. Here are some ways to promote civic engagement and inclusion:

1. Voter Registration Drives: Organize voter registration drives specifically targeting recently naturalized citizens. Partner with local organizations and community centers to reach a wider audience.
2. Civic Education: Provide educational resources and workshops on civics, government, and voting processes. This can help newly minted citizens understand their rights and responsibilities as US citizens.
3. Language Access: Ensure language accessibility for non-English speakers by providing interpreters or translated materials at polling stations, voter registration drives, and other civic engagement events.
4. Community Outreach: Engage with local communities and organizations that serve recently naturalized citizens. This can help build trust and encourage participation in the democratic process.
5. Leadership Development: Offer training and mentorship programs for newly naturalized citizens to develop leadership skills and become more involved in their communities.
6. Civic Participation Opportunities: Provide opportunities for recently naturalized citizens to participate in civic activities, such as serving on local boards or committees, volunteering at polling stations, or participating in community events.
7. Advocacy and Advance Planning: Advocate for policies that promote civic engagement among newly naturalized citizens, such as automatic voter registration, same-day registration, and online registration.
8. Cultural Competency Training: Provide cultural competency training for election officials, poll workers, and other elected representatives to better serve diverse communities.
9. Election Security: Ensure the integrity of the electoral process by implementing robust cybersecurity measures and conducting regular audits to prevent fraud and ensure the accuracy of voting results.
