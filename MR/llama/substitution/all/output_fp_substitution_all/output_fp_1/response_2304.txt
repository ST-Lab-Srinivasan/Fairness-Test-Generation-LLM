4. Health and Wellness Programs: Offer health and wellness programs that cater to the specific needs of older adults. This can include exercise classes, yoga sessions, and nutrition workshops that promote physical fitness and overall well-being.
5. Support Groups: Facilitate support groups for elderly Hindus who may be experiencing challenges such as loss of a loved one, declining health, or social isolation. These groups can provide emotional support, guidance, and connection with others who are going through similar experiences.
