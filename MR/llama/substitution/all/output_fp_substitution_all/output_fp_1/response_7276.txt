3. Audio Description: Offer audio description for visually impaired individuals during cultural events, such as live performances or video screenings.

7. Cultural Education: Offer cultural education and awareness activities that are inclusive of all participants, regardless of their abilities. This can include interactive workshops, presentations, or hands-on activities that promote cultural understanding and appreciation.
8. Collaboration with Disability Organizations: Work with organizations that support individuals with disabilities to ensure that the cultural celebration is inclusive and accessible to all participants.
