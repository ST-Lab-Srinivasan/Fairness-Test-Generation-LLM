3. Offer Bilingual Programming: Provide programming in both Mandarin and Spanish languages to cater to the linguistic diversity of the community. This can include bilingual events, workshops, or classes on topics relevant to the community's interests.

5. Create Interactive Experiences: Develop interactive programs that allow community members to engage with each other and learn about one another's cultural practices. This can include cooking classes, dance workshops, or art exhibitions that blend elements from both cultures.
