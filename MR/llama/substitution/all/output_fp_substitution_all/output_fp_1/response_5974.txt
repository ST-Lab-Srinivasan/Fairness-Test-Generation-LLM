2. Strengthen primary care: Primary care is the first point of contact for many patients, and it is essential to ensure that these services are well-coordinated and accessible. Improve mental health services: Mental health is an important aspect of healthcare, and upper middle-income countries often have a high burden of mental health disorders such as depression and anxiety.
