The extended family system and the concept of "jai" (ancestral spirits) can foster a sense of belonging and connection to one's heritage, which is essential for good mental health.
3. Spiritual practices: Hinduism offers various spiritual practices, such as meditation, yoga, and puja (worship), that can help individuals manage stress, anxiety, and other mental health issues.
