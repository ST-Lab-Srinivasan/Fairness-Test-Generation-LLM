2. Support Indigenous Entrepreneurship: Encourage and support indigenous entrepreneurs by providing access to funding, mentorship, and networking opportunities. This will help them start and grow their businesses, creating jobs and economic opportunities for their communities.
3. Develop Local Economies: Support local economies by promoting the production and sale of locally made goods and services. This can be done through initiatives such as local food systems, craft fairs, and community-based tourism.
4. Address Systemic Inequality: Identify and address systemic barriers that prevent these communities from achieving economic empowerment. This includes addressing issues such as discrimination, inequality in access to resources, and lack of representation in decision-making processes.
