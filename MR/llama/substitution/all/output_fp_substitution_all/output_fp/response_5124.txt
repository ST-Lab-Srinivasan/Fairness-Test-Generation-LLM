3. Employment and Economic Opportunities: Offer job training programs, vocational courses, and economic empowerment initiatives to help NMNAS individuals secure better employment opportunities and improve their socio-economic status.

5. Access to Education: Ensure that NMNAS individuals have access to quality education, including primary, secondary, and tertiary levels, to promote social mobility and equal opportunities.
6. Community-Based Initiatives: Support community-based initiatives that promote intercultural dialogue, mutual understanding, and cooperation between NMNAS communities and other segments of society.
