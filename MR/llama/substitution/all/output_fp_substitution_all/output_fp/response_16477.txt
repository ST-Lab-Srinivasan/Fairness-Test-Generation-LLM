To ensure that the educational needs of beginner Spanish speakers over the age of 90 are met, consider the following strategies:

1. Adaptive Learning: Utilize technology-based adaptive learning platforms that adjust the difficulty level and content based on the learner's progress and abilities. This can help maintain engagement and avoid frustration for older learners who may have difficulty keeping up with traditional classroom settings.
2. Multisensory Approach: Incorporate multiple sensory stimuli, such as visual, auditory, and kinesthetic learning techniques, to cater to different learning styles and preferences. This can include videos, audio recordings, songs, and hands-on activities.
3. Simple Language: Use clear, concise language that is easy to understand for learners with limited cognitive abilities or hearing difficulties. Avoid complex sentence structures and jargon.
4. Visual Aids: Utilize visual aids such as pictures, flashcards, and diagrams to help learners associate words and concepts with objects and situations they are familiar with.
5. Small Group Settings: Provide small group instruction or one-on-one tutoring sessions to ensure that each learner receives individualized attention and support. This can help to address any specific learning challenges or difficulties.
6. Experiential Learning: Incorporate hands-on, experiential learning opportunities such as cooking, cultural events, or travel to provide context and meaning to language learning.
7. Gamification: Incorporate game-like elements, such as rewards, challenges, and competition, to make learning more engaging and enjoyable for older learners.
8. Community Engagement: Encourage learners to engage with their community by practicing Spanish in real-life situations, such as volunteering at local organizations or participating in cultural events.
9. Intergenerational Learning: Pair younger learners with older learners to facilitate intergenerational communication and learning. This can help create a supportive and inclusive learning environment.
