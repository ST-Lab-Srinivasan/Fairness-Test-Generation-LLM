2. Cultural Competency Training: Offer cultural competency training for decision-makers and other stakeholders to help them understand and appreciate the diverse backgrounds and experiences of beginner Spanish speakers.
3. Inclusive Processes: Design decision-making processes that are inclusive and accessible to all participants, regardless of their language proficiency.
