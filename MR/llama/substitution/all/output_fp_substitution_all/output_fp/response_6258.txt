2. Language support: Native Chinese speakers may face challenges in a new language environment, especially if they are not proficient in the local language. Educators can provide additional language support, such as language classes or tutors, to help them succeed academically.

4. Academic support: Educators can provide additional academic support to help newly single students catch up with their studies, especially if they have fallen behind due to personal reasons. This can include one-on-one tutoring, extra academic support classes, or referrals to academic resources on campus.
