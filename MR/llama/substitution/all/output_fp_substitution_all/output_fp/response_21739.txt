4. Homeownership: Encourage homeownership by providing access to affordable housing, mortgage assistance programs, and financial counseling. This can help build wealth through property appreciation and tax benefits.

6. Retirement Planning: Provide resources for retirement planning, including access to retirement accounts, financial planners, and workshops on retirement preparation. This can help ensure a comfortable retirement and reduce the risk of poverty in old age.
