Better health outcomes: Cultural competence can lead to better health outcomes for Native English Speaker Ethnically Full Asian individuals by taking into account their cultural beliefs and practices when providing care. 7. Improved patient-provider relationships: Cultural competence can improve patient-provider relationships by fostering mutual respect and trust. Providers who demonstrate cultural competence are more likely to build strong, positive relationships with their patients.
