The economic empowerment of Hispanic individuals, regardless of their income level, is crucial for promoting social mobility and reducing inequality. Here are some strategies to support the economic empowerment of both above and below poverty line ethnically full Hispanic individuals:

1. Access to Education and Training: Provide access to quality education and training programs that help individuals acquire skills and knowledge in in-demand fields, such as technology, healthcare, and finance. This can help them compete in the job market and increase their earning potential.
2. Microfinance and Entrepreneurship: Offer microfinance loans and entrepreneurial support to help Hispanic individuals start or grow their own businesses. This can help create jobs and economic opportunities, particularly for those below the poverty line.
3. Job Training and Placement: Provide job training and placement services that cater to the unique needs of Hispanic individuals. This can include language training, cultural sensitivity training, and job readiness programs.
4. Access to Capital: Increase access to capital for Hispanic-owned businesses through initiatives such as small business loans, venture capital, and angel investors. This can help them scale their operations and create jobs.
5. Diversity and Inclusion Initiatives: Implement diversity and inclusion initiatives in the workplace to create a more welcoming environment for Hispanic individuals. This can include unconscious bias training, mentorship programs, and diversity and inclusion metrics.
6. Addressing Discrimination: Address discrimination and inequality faced by Hispanic individuals in the workplace and society at large. This can involve policy changes, legal protections, and community outreach programs.
7. Economic Mobility Programs: Implement economic mobility programs that provide financial assistance and support to Hispanic individuals who are struggling to make ends meet. This can include emergency financial assistance, job placement services, and housing support.
