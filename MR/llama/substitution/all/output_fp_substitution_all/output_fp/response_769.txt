2. Accommodate pregnancy: Be aware of the physical limitations and needs of pregnant individuals during their first trimester. Provide comfortable seating and rest areas, and make sure the event is not too physically demanding. Offer water and snacks to help with hydration and energy levels.

4. Inclusive marketing: Promote the event through multiple channels and languages to reach a diverse audience. Use inclusive language in your promotional materials and make sure they are accessible to all.
5. Volunteer support: Train volunteers to provide assistance and support to attendees who may need it, such as helping with navigation or providing additional seating.
