Cultural exchange plays a significant role in fostering understanding between native English speaker, Native American, and Native Hawaiian groups in the United States. Cultural exchange refers to the sharing and exchange of cultural practices, values, beliefs, and traditions between different groups. In the context of intercultural communication, cultural exchange can help promote mutual understanding and respect between diverse groups.

1. Shared learning experiences: Cultural exchange programs provide opportunities for people from different backgrounds to learn from each other. 5. Community engagement: Cultural exchange programs can facilitate community engagement between different groups, promoting mutual understanding and respect.
