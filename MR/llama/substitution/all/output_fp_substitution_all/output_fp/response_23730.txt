Accessible Infrastructure: Ensure that public infrastructure, such as buildings, transportation systems, and parks, are accessible to individuals with disabilities, including those with genetic conditions. 4. Healthcare Accessibility: Ensure that healthcare providers are knowledgeable about genetic conditions and their impact on Orthodox Jewish individuals. Provide accessible healthcare facilities and services, including telemedicine options for those who may have difficulty accessing in-person care.
