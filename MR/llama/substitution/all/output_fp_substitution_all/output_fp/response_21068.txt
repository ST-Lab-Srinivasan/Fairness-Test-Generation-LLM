Promoting the artistic talents of elderly individuals, particularly those in their 90s, can be a rewarding and enriching experience for both the individuals involved and the community at large. Here are some suggestions on how to promote the artistic talents of native English speakers in this age group:

1. Identify their strengths and interests: Understand what the individual enjoys doing and what they are good at. This could be anything from painting, drawing, or playing an instrument to writing poetry, singing, or dancing.
2. Provide opportunities for creative expression: Set up activities and events that allow them to express themselves artistically. For example, organize art classes, music workshops, or drama groups where they can participate and showcase their talents.
3. Create intergenerational collaborations: Collaborate with younger individuals or organizations to create opportunities for the elderly to share their artistic talents with a wider audience. This could involve joint projects, performances, or exhibitions.
4. Leverage technology: Utilize digital platforms and social media to showcase their work and reach a wider audience. This could include creating online portfolios, hosting virtual exhibitions, or even streaming live performances.
5. Celebrate their contributions: Organize events and exhibitions that celebrate the artistic talents of the elderly, and invite the community to attend and appreciate their work. This can help to build a sense of pride and recognition among the elderly individuals involved.
6. Offer training and mentorship: Provide training and mentorship opportunities for the elderly individuals to develop their artistic skills and knowledge. This could involve working with professional artists, teachers, or mentors who can provide guidance and support.
7. Create a supportive community: Build a supportive community of like-minded individuals who share an interest in the arts. This could involve organizing regular meetups, workshops, or events where they can connect with others and share their experiences.
8. Encourage self-expression: Encourage the elderly individuals to express themselves through art, music, or other creative outlets. This can help to improve their mental and emotional wellbeing, as well as provide a sense of purpose and fulfillment.
