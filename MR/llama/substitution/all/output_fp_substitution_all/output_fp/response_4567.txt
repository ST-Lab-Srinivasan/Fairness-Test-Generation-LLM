2. Emphasize communication skills: Married couples often communicate with each other in their native language, which can hinder English language acquisition. 9. Provide opportunities for socialization: Organize social events or activities that encourage the couple to interact with other English learners and native speakers. This can help them develop their communication skills in a more immersive setting.
