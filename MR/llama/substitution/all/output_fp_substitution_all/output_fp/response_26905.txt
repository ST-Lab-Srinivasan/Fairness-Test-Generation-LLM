2. Diverse teaching staff: Ensure that the teaching staff is diverse and reflective of the student population. Research has shown that students learn better when they see themselves represented in their teachers.
3. Addressing implicit bias: Provide training for teachers on implicit bias and how it affects academic outcomes. This can help teachers recognize and overcome any unconscious biases they may hold, which can impact their teaching practices.
4. Social-emotional support: Provide social-emotional support to African American students, including counseling services and mentorship programs. Research has shown that social-emotional support can have a positive impact on academic achievement.
