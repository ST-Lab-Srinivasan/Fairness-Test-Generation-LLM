2. Building empathy: Cultural exchange promotes empathy by encouraging learners to step into the shoes of their peers from different cultural backgrounds. This helps build bridges between groups and fosters a sense of shared humanity, which is essential for effective communication and understanding.
3. Debunking stereotypes: Cultural exchange can help debunk stereotypes and misconceptions that may exist between different cultural groups. By engaging in dialogue and learning about each other's cultures, learners can challenge their preconceived notions and develop a more nuanced understanding of the target culture.
