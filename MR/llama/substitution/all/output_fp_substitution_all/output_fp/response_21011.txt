8. Watch Spanish media: Watch Spanish TV shows, movies, or listen to Spanish podcasts together with your spouse. This will provide an opportunity to practice listening and comprehension skills while having fun together.
9. Play language games: Play language games such as "I Spy" or "20 Questions" in Spanish to practice vocabulary and grammar in a fun and interactive way.
10. Use visual aids: Use visual aids such as flashcards, pictures, or videos to help you learn new vocabulary and phrases in a visually engaging way.
