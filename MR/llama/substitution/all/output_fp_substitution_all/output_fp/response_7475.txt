Supporting the social inclusion of recent temporary visa holders in India can be achieved through various means. Here are some strategies that can help promote their integration into Indian society:

1. Language classes: Providing language classes for recent temporary visa holders can help them learn Hindi or other local languages, enabling them to communicate more effectively with the locals.
2. Cultural orientation programs: Organizing cultural orientation programs can help newcomers understand the cultural nuances of India and adapt to the local way of life.
3. Social events: Hosting social events, such as potlucks or picnics, can provide opportunities for temporary visa holders to interact with their hosts and other members of the community.
4. Community service: Encouraging temporary visa holders to participate in community service activities can help them connect with the local population and develop a sense of responsibility towards the society.
5. Mentorship programs: Pairing recent temporary visa holders with experienced individuals who can provide guidance and support can help them navigate the challenges of living in India.
6. Job training and placement: Offering job training and placement services can help temporary visa holders acquire new skills and find employment opportunities in India, which can enhance their social inclusion.
7. Access to healthcare: Ensuring that temporary visa holders have access to quality healthcare can address their medical needs and promote their overall well-being.
8. Legal aid: Providing legal aid services can help recent temporary visa holders navigate the Indian legal system and address any issues related to their immigration status.
