This leads to better patient outcomes and fewer misunderstandings.
3. Enhanced patient engagement: When healthcare providers demonstrate cultural competence, they encourage their patients to take an active role in their care. Patients are more likely to participate in medical decision-making and follow treatment plans when they feel that their provider understands and respects their cultural values.
4. Increased patient adherence: When healthcare providers tailor their communication style and treatment plans to patients' cultural backgrounds, patients are more likely to adhere to their treatment plans. This can lead to better health outcomes and reduced health disparities.
