4. Inclusive Communication: Effective communication is crucial during disaster response efforts. Disaster responders should use inclusive language and provide information in multiple languages and formats to ensure that all individuals are aware of the available resources and services.
5. Support Networks: Building support networks within the transgender Orthodox Jewish community can help create a sense of belonging and reduce feelings of isolation during times of crisis.
