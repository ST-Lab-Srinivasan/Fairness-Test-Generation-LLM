2. Accessible Communication Channels: Offer alternative communication channels, such as email, text messaging, or video conferencing, for those who prefer or cannot use sign language. Make sure these channels are accessible and free of any barriers.
3. Deaf Representation: Ensure that deaf individuals are represented in decision-making bodies, such as advisory boards, task forces, or committees. This can be done by actively seeking out and recruiting deaf candidates.

6. Accessible Venues: Ensure that any event or meeting space is accessible to deaf individuals. This includes having wheelchair accessibility, Braille signage, and amplification systems for speech and music.
