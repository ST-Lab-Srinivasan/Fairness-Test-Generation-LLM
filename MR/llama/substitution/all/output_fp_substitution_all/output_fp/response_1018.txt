6. Employment Accommodations: Ensure that public institutions are equipped to provide reasonable accommodations in the workplace for individuals with genetic conditions, including those from Orthodox Jewish communities.
7. Transportation Accessibility: Provide accessible transportation options for individuals with genetic conditions, including those from Orthodox Jewish communities. This can include wheelchair-accessible buses and trains, as well as personalized transportation services.
