5. Encourage professional help: If your partner is experiencing severe mental health challenges during the first trimester, encourage them to seek professional help from a mental health provider or prenatal care provider.
6. Attend prenatal appointments together: Attending prenatal appointments together can provide an opportunity for bonding and support during this time.
