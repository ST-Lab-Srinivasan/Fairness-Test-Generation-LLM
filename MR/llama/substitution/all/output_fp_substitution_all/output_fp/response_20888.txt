6. Offer mental health resources: Non-binary students may face additional stress and anxiety due to their family situation. Ensure that they have access to mental health resources such as counseling and therapy.

8. Provide education and awareness: Offer training and resources for teachers and staff to better understand the experiences of non-binary students and how to support them. Educate the broader community on the importance of inclusivity and respect for all gender identities.
