The concept of cultural heritage:
Cultural heritage refers to the traditions, customs, beliefs, values, and practices that are passed down from one generation to the next within a particular culture or society. It includes intangible elements such as language, music, dance, art, literature, and religion, as well as tangible elements such as landscapes, buildings, and artifacts. Cultural heritage shapes the identity of individuals by providing them with a sense of belonging and connection to their cultural roots.
