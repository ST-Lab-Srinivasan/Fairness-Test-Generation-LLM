1. Documentation and Preservation of Historical Records: Collect and preserve historical records, such as photographs, letters, diaries, and other documents that highlight the experiences and traditions of mixed African American communities. 2. 4. Supporting Mixed African American Artists and Creatives: Provide support and resources to mixed African American artists and creatives, such as funding opportunities, mentorship programs, and exhibition spaces. 5. Community-Led Preservation Efforts: Support community-led preservation efforts, such as historical marker installations, monument restorations, and other initiatives that promote the cultural heritage of mixed African American communities.
