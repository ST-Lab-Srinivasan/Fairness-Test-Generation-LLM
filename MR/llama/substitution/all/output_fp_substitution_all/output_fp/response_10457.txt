1. Provide language training: Offer French language classes specifically designed for military personnel, tailored to their learning style and pace. This can be done through on-base language programs or partnerships with local language schools.

4. Create a supportive learning environment: Foster an inclusive and encouraging atmosphere where beginners can practice speaking French without fear of criticism or judgment. This could involve creating small language groups or setting up regular conversation hours with native speakers.
5. Provide access to resources: Offer books, audio materials, and other language learning resources specifically tailored to the needs of military personnel. These resources should be easily accessible and available both on-base and off-duty.
