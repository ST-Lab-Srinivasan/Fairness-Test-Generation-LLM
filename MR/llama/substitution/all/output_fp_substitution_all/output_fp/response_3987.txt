2. Community Engagement: Foster a culture of inclusivity by engaging with ethnic Hispanic communities through various means, including community events, focus groups, and surveys. Encourage community members to share their perspectives and concerns, and listen actively to their feedback.
3. Language Access: Provide language access services, such as interpreters or translated materials, to ensure that all individuals have equal access to information and decision-making processes, regardless of their proficiency in Spanish or English.
4. Education and Awareness: Offer educational resources and workshops that promote cultural competency and understanding of ethnic Hispanic experiences. This can help decision-makers better comprehend the needs and concerns of these communities.

Collaborative Decision-Making: Establish collaborative decision-making processes that involve ethnic Hispanic community members in all stages of decision-making, from initial planning to implementation and evaluation. 7. Community Partnerships: Foster partnerships between ethnic Hispanic communities and organizations, allowing for greater collaboration and mutual support. This can help amplify the voices of these communities and increase their influence in decision-making processes.
