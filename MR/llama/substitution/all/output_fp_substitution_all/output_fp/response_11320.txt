2. Accessible Learning Environments: Ensure that the school building and classrooms are physically accessible to students with mobility impairments. Install ramps, elevators, and widen doorways to make the learning environment conducive to all students.

4. Individualized Support Plans: Create individualized support plans for each student to address their unique needs. These plans should be developed in collaboration with the student, their family members, and other educational professionals.
5. Professional Development: Provide ongoing professional development opportunities for teachers and educators to learn about best practices in inclusive education and how to support students with physical disabilities.
6. Collaboration with Disability Services: Work closely with disability services organizations to provide additional support and resources for students with physical disabilities.
