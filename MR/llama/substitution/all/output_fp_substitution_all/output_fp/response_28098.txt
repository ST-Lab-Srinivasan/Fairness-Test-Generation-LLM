2. Education and awareness: Cultural exchange can involve educating and raising awareness about the issues and concerns facing different GND groups. By engaging in cultural exchange, individuals can gain a deeper appreciation for the challenges faced by marginalized groups and develop more effective strategies for advocating on their behalf.
