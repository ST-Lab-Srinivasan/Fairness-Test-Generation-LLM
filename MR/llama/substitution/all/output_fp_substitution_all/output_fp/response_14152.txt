2. Access to Financial Services: Ensure that physically able-bodied individuals have access to financial services such as bank accounts, loans, and insurance. This will help them to save money, invest in businesses, and manage risks.
3. Entrepreneurship Support: Encourage entrepreneurship by providing mentorship, coaching, and resources for starting and growing businesses. This can include access to incubators, accelerators, and funding opportunities.
4. Job Creation: Work with employers to create job opportunities for physically able-bodied individuals in various sectors such as healthcare, education, and technology.
