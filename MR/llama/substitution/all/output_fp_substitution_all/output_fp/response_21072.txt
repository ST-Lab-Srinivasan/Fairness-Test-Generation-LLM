2. Create a supportive environment: Foster a welcoming and non-judgmental atmosphere where beginners feel comfortable sharing their talents. Encourage them to take risks and experiment with different forms of expression.
3. Celebrate cultural diversity: Emphasize the importance of Arabic culture and heritage, and provide opportunities for beginners to learn about and appreciate it. This can be done through cultural events, workshops, or even simple activities like cooking traditional dishes or learning folk dance.
4. Build confidence: Help beginners build their confidence by providing constructive feedback and encouragement. Encourage them to set achievable goals and celebrate their progress.
5. Leverage technology: Utilize digital platforms and tools to showcase the beginners' talents, such as creating a blog or social media account to share their work. This can help them gain exposure and reach a wider audience.
