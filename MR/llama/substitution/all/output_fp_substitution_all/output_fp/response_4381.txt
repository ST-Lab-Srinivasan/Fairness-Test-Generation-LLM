Creating more inclusive community events for active-duty military personnel and their families, regardless of income level, requires careful planning and consideration. Here are some strategies to help make your events more accessible and inclusive:

1. Offer free or reduced-cost admission: Provide free or discounted tickets for active-duty military personnel and their families to ensure that everyone can participate, regardless of their income level.
2. Schedule events during non-peak hours: Consider scheduling events during times when it's easier for military personnel to attend, such as during the week or later in the evening. This can help minimize conflicts with work and other responsibilities.
3. Provide childcare and transportation: Offer free or discounted childcare and transportation services to make it easier for military families to participate in events.
4. Offer flexible payment options: Allow participants to pay for events using a variety of methods, such as credit cards, mobile payments, or even cryptocurrency, to make it easier for those with limited financial resources to participate.
5. Promote events through military channels: Advertise events through military channels, such as military bases, units, and organizations, to ensure that active-duty personnel are aware of the events and can attend easily.
6. Offer virtual options: Provide virtual options for events, such as live streaming or online participation, to make it easier for military personnel who may be deployed or stationed in different locations to participate.
7. Create a sense of community: Foster a sense of community among attendees by providing opportunities for networking and socializing. This can help create a supportive environment where everyone feels included and valued.
8. Involve military personnel in planning and execution: Involve active-duty military personnel in the planning and execution of events to ensure that their needs and preferences are taken into account. This can help create a more inclusive and welcoming environment for all attendees.
