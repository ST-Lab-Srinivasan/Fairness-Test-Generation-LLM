2. Mentorship: Pair them with experienced professionals who can provide guidance, support, and valuable insights into civilian life. This mentorship program can help them navigate the transition from military to civilian life and build a network of peers and professionals.
3. Networking Opportunities: Organize networking events that bring together retired military personnel and their spouses with successful civilians in various industries.
