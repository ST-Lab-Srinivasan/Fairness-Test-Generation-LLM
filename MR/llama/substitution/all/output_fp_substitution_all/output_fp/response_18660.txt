6. Inclusive event planning: When hosting events or activities within the community, make sure they are inclusive and accessible to all participants. This can involve providing accommodations such as American Sign Language (ASL) interpretation, audio descriptions, or braille materials.

8. Celebrating diversity: Embrace and celebrate the diversity of the community by promoting a culture of inclusion and acceptance. Encourage community members to share their experiences and perspectives, and provide opportunities for cross-cultural exchange.
9. Empowerment through leadership: Empower individuals with lifelong, non-known genetic conditions to take leadership roles within the community. This can help build confidence, self-esteem, and a sense of belonging.
