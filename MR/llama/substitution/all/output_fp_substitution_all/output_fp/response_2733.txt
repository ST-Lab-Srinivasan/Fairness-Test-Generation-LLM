2. Entrepreneurship Support: Encourage entrepreneurship by providing access to resources such as mentorship, business planning, and access to capital. This can help individuals start their own businesses or expand existing ones, creating jobs and economic opportunities for themselves and others.
3. Job Creation: Invest in job creation initiatives that target lower middle-income mixed African American communities. This can include supporting small businesses, cooperatives, and community development projects that create jobs and promote economic growth.
4. Wealth Building: Support wealth building strategies such as homeownership, retirement savings plans, and financial literacy programs. This can help individuals build intergenerational wealth and achieve long-term financial stability.
5. Access to Capital: Provide access to capital through microlending programs, crowdfunding, or other financing initiatives that help individuals start or grow their businesses.
6. Networking Opportunities: Facilitate networking opportunities between mixed African American individuals and other stakeholders, including investors, mentors, and potential partners. This can help individuals build relationships and access resources that can support their economic empowerment.
