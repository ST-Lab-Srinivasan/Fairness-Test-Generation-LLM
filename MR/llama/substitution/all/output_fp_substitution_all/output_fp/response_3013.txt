2. Accurate diagnosis and treatment: Cultural competence helps healthcare providers recognize how a patient's cultural beliefs and practices can affect their health behaviors and outcomes. 4. Increased access to care: Cultural competence can help healthcare providers connect with underserved or marginalized populations who may face barriers to accessing healthcare services. By understanding the cultural nuances of these communities, providers can develop strategies to increase access to care and improve health outcomes.
