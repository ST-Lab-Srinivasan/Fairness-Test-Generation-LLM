3. Economic Empowerment Initiatives: Develop and implement targeted initiatives that aim to promote economic empowerment within these communities. Examples include business incubators, mentorship programs, and networking events.

9. Building Partnerships: Develop partnerships with local organizations, businesses, and governments to leverage resources, expertise, and networks to support the economic empowerment of these communities.
