2. Modified Curriculum: Develop a modified curriculum that takes into account the NAS students' language proficiency, cultural values, and learning styles. This could include using authentic materials, visual aids, and hands-on activities to support their learning.

5. Community Outreach: Engage in community outreach programs to raise awareness about the educational needs of NAS students and to build partnerships with local organizations that can provide additional support.
