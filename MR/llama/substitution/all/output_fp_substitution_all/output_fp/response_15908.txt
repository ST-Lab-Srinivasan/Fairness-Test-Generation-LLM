3. Patient-Centered Care: Focus on providing patient-centered care that takes into account the unique needs and preferences of each individual. This includes involving patients in decision-making processes, respecting their autonomy, and addressing their concerns and priorities.
4. Community Outreach: Engage in community outreach and engagement activities to reach out to native Spanish speaker Mexican populations.
