Title: The Role of Cultural Heritage in the Lives of Native Arabic Speaking Hardcore Democrats

Introduction:
Cultural heritage plays a significant role in shaping the identities and beliefs of individuals and communities. In the case of native Arabic speaking hardcore democrats, cultural heritage is deeply intertwined with their political beliefs and values. This study aims to explore the role of cultural heritage in the lives of these individuals and how it influences their political views.

Methods:
The study employed a qualitative research approach, using semi-structured interviews with 20 native Arabic speaking hardcore democrats from various countries in the Middle East and North Africa. The participants were selected through purposive sampling, based on their self-identification as hardcore democrats and their proficiency in Arabic.

Results:
The study revealed that cultural heritage plays a crucial role in shaping the political beliefs and values of native Arabic speaking hardcore democrats. The participants believed that their cultural heritage had instilled in them a strong sense of justice, equality, and compassion, which are core values of democracy. They also noted that their cultural traditions emphasized the importance of community and collective action, which aligns with the democratic principle of active citizenship.

Moreover, the participants believed that their cultural heritage had provided them with a rich intellectual tradition that informs their political beliefs. Conclusion:
The study demonstrates that cultural heritage plays a significant role in shaping the political beliefs and values of native Arabic speaking hardcore democrats. The participants' cultural traditions and intellectual traditions inform their commitment to democratic principles and their belief in the importance of community and collective action. These findings highlight the need to consider the cultural context when engaging with political discourse, particularly in regions where cultural heritage is deeply intertwined with political beliefs and values.
