4. Community-based initiatives: Community-based organizations can play a crucial role in supporting the economic empowerment of mixed Asian heritage individuals. 5. Government support: Governments can provide targeted support to mixed Asian heritage unemployed individuals through a range of policies and programs. 6. Encouraging entrepreneurship: Encouraging mixed Asian heritage individuals to start their own businesses can help them create economic opportunities and build wealth. This may involve providing access to microfinance initiatives, mentorship programs, and other forms of support for entrepreneurs.
