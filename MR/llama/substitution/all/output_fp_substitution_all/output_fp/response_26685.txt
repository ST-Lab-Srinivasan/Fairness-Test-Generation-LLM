2. Accommodate prayer and worship: Make provisions for Muslim students to practice their daily prayers and worship during school hours. This may involve designating a specific area for prayer or providing flexible scheduling options for students who need to pray at specific times of the day.
3. Support halal food options: Provide halal food options in the school cafeteria or offer vegetarian or vegan alternatives for Muslim students who cannot consume non-halal food.
4. Create an inclusive atmosphere: Foster an atmosphere of inclusivity and respect for all students, regardless of their religious beliefs. Encourage dialogue and understanding between Muslim students and their peers, and provide opportunities for cultural exchange and learning.
