3. Civic Education: Offer workshops and classes on civics, voting, and democratic participation. This can help refugees understand the electoral process and their rights as citizens.

5. Civic Participation Opportunities: Provide opportunities for refugees to participate in local governance and decision-making processes. This can include participating in community meetings, serving on advisory boards, or volunteering with local organizations.
