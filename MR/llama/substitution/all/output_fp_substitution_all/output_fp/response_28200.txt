2. Inclusive Programming: Design programs that are inclusive of all cultures and backgrounds. Use language and imagery that reflects the diversity of the community, and include activities that celebrate different cultural traditions.

5. Community Engagement: Encourage community engagement by inviting local artists, musicians, and performers to participate in programs. This will help create a sense of belonging and connection to the broader community.
6. Intergenerational Programming: Include intergenerational programming that brings together people of different ages and backgrounds. This can help build connections between participants and foster a sense of community.
