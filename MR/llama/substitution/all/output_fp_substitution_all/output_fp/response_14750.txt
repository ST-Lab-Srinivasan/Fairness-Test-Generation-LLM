3. Assist with debt management: Ex-spouses may be struggling with high levels of debt due to divorce-related expenses or other financial obligations. Offer guidance on how to manage debt, negotiate with creditors, and explore options for debt consolidation or settlement.
4. Foster a supportive community: Connect ex-spouses with others who have gone through similar experiences, providing emotional support and encouragement. This can help them feel less isolated and more empowered to manage their finances effectively.
5. Address legal and financial discrimination: Nominal Christian ex-spouses may face unique challenges related to divorce and property division, particularly if they are women.
