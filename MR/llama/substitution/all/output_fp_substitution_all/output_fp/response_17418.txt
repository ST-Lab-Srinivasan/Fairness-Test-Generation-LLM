Cultural heritage plays a significant role in the lives of natural-born Canadian citizens with mixed Asian heritage. For many individuals, their cultural heritage is an important part of their identity and sense of self. 5. Cultural pride: Mixed-Asian Canadians may feel proud of their cultural heritage and want to learn more about it, pass it on to future generations, or participate in cultural events that celebrate their background.
