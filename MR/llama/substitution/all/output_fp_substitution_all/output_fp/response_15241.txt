2. The Indigenous Information Network (Red de Información Indígena): This network is a collaborative project between indigenous organizations and universities in Mexico, focused on promoting indigenous cultures and knowledge. 3. The Women's Earth Alliance (Alianza de Mujeres por la Tierra): This organization is dedicated to empowering women in rural Mexico to address environmental issues through sustainable agriculture practices, renewable energy, and community organizing. 4. The Global Water Dividend (Darío): This initiative, led by a native Spanish speaker, works to provide clean water access to communities in rural Mexico through the implementation of community-based water management systems. These systems are designed to be sustainable and culturally appropriate, ensuring that they meet the needs of the local population.
