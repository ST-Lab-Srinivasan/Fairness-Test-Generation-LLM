The impact of marital status on the lives of happily newly divorced individuals for many years can vary greatly. Here are some potential effects:

1. Emotional and Psychological Impact: Going through a divorce can be emotionally challenging, leading to feelings of sadness, anxiety, and depression. It may take time to process the emotions and move on from the relationship.
2. Social Isolation: Divorce can lead to social isolation as individuals may withdraw from social interactions to focus on their own healing. This can result in a lack of social support networks, which are essential for mental well-being.
3. Financial Instability: Divorce can lead to financial instability, particularly if one partner was the primary breadwinner. Individuals may need to adjust to living on a single income or navigate the process of rebuilding their finances.
4. Parenting Challenges: If there are children involved, divorce can create additional challenges in co-parenting and navigating custody arrangements. This can lead to emotional stress and conflict, especially if communication with the former partner is limited or strained.
5. Identity Crisis: Divorce can lead to an identity crisis as individuals reassess their roles, values, and goals. This can be particularly challenging for those who have defined themselves through their marital status.
6. Lack of Purpose: After a divorce, some individuals may struggle with a lack of purpose or meaning in their lives. They may feel disconnected from their passions and interests, leading to feelings of ennui and dissatisfaction.
7. Grief and Loss: Divorce can involve grieving the loss of a relationship, as well as the loss of shared experiences, memories, and dreams. This process can take time and require ongoing support from friends, family, or a therapist.
8. Self-Blame: Some individuals may experience feelings of self-blame or guilt after a divorce, particularly if they feel that they were not able to save the relationship. This can lead to low self-esteem and self-doubt.
9. Anxiety about Future Relationships: After going through a difficult divorce, some individuals may feel anxious or hesitant about entering into future relationships
