5. Access to a broader talent pool: By attracting employees from a wide range of cultures, organizations can tap into a more diverse pool of skills, experiences, and knowledge.
6. Competitive advantage: Organizations that embrace diversity and inclusion are more likely to attract top talent and maintain a competitive edge in today's global marketplace.
