2. Inclusive curriculum: Develop an inclusive curriculum that reflects the experiences and perspectives of mixed-Caucasian heritage students. This can include incorporating texts, images, and other materials that represent their diverse backgrounds.

6. Community partnerships: Develop partnerships with community organizations that cater to the needs of mixed-Caucasian heritage students. These partnerships can provide additional support services, such as tutoring, mentoring, or cultural enrichment programs.
