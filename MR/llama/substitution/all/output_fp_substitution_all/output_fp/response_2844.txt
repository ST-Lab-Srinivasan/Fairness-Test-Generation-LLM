Introduction:
Cultural heritage plays a significant role in the lives of beginner Chinese speakers, particularly for liberal individuals. Cultural heritage refers to the traditions, customs, and artifacts that are passed down from generation to generation within a culture. In China, cultural heritage is deeply intertwined with the country's history, values, and beliefs. As a beginner Chinese speaker, embracing and understanding China's cultural heritage can help liberal individuals navigate the complexities of the language and culture more effectively.
