Supporting the social inclusion of Nominal Muslim Native Arabic Speaker (NMNAS) people involves addressing the systemic and institutional barriers that prevent them from fully participating in society. Here are some ways to support their social inclusion:

1. Education and Awareness: Provide education and awareness programs that promote cultural sensitivity and understanding of NMNAS communities. This can help break down stereotypes and biases, and create a more inclusive environment.
2. Language Support: Offer language support services to help NMNAS individuals improve their proficiency in the dominant language of the country they are living in. This can help them navigate official channels and access essential services.
3. Community Outreach: Engage in community outreach programs that promote social inclusion and provide a platform for NMNAS individuals to share their experiences and perspectives. This can help build bridges between different communities and foster mutual understanding.
4. Employment Support: Provide employment support services, such as job training and placement, to help NMNAS individuals access decent work opportunities. This can help them achieve economic stability and independence.
5. Healthcare Access: Advocate for improved healthcare access for NMNAS individuals, including language support in medical settings and culturally sensitive healthcare services.
6. Legal Support: Provide legal support services, such as interpretation and translation services, to help NMNAS individuals navigate the legal system. This can help them access justice and protect their rights.
7. Housing Support: Advocate for affordable and culturally sensitive housing options for NMNAS individuals, including language support in housing applications and cultural sensitivity in housing design.
8. Access to Technology: Provide access to technology, such as smartphones and internet connectivity, to help NMNAS individuals stay connected with their communities and access essential services.
9. Cultural Preservation: Support the preservation of NMNAS cultures and traditions through cultural events, festivals, and educational programs. This can help promote cross-cultural understanding and respect for diversity.
10. Community Leadership: Support the development of community leaders from NMNAS communities, who can advocate for their rights and interests within society.
