Increased access to care: Culturally competent healthcare providers can better navigate the complexities of diverse patient populations, which can lead to increased access to care for marginalized communities. 5. Better identification of health disparities: Culturally competent healthcare providers can identify health disparities within their patient populations more easily. By understanding the cultural factors contributing to these disparities, they can develop targeted interventions to address them.
