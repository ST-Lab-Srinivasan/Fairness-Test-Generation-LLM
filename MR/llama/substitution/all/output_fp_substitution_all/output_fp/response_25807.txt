2. Increased trust: When healthcare providers demonstrate cultural competence, they are more likely to build trust with their patients. 4. Enhanced patient satisfaction: When healthcare providers are culturally competent, they are better able to address the social and cultural factors that impact a patient's health. This can lead to higher patient satisfaction and better overall well-being.
