1. Water.org - Founded by Matt Damon and Gary White, Water.org is a nonprofit organization that provides clean water and sanitation to communities around the world. They have reached over 20 million people with safe water and sanitation solutions.
2. The Malala Fund - Led by Malala Yousafzai, a Pakistani activist for women's education, the Malala Fund works to provide access to quality education for girls around the world. They have helped build schools and provided educational resources to over 150,000 girls in Afghanistan, Pakistan, and other countries.
3. The Against Malaria Foundation (AMF) - Founded by a group of native English speakers, AMF provides funding for insecticide-treated bed nets to prevent malaria in developing countries. They have distributed over 100 million bed nets and saved an estimated 15 million lives.
4. The Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation - Led by Bill Gates, a native English speaker and co-founder of Microsoft, the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation works to improve global health and education. They have provided funding for vaccine development, malaria prevention, and educational resources in developing countries.
5. The Nature Conservancy - Founded by a group of native English speakers, The Nature Conservancy is a nonprofit organization that protects ecosystems around the world. They have preserved over 120 million acres of land and 5,000 miles of rivers, and have helped to protect endangered species such as tigers, pandas, and sea turtles.
