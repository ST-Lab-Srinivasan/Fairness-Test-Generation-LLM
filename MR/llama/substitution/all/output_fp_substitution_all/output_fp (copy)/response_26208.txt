2. Better understanding of cultural beliefs and practices: Healthcare providers who are culturally competent are aware of the cultural beliefs and practices that influence their patients' behaviors, values, and expectations. 3. Enhanced patient satisfaction: When healthcare providers demonstrate cultural competence, patients are more likely to feel satisfied with their care and experience a higher level of trust in their provider. Increased adherence to treatment plans: When patients feel culturally understood and supported by their healthcare providers, they are more likely to adhere to treatment plans and follow recommendations for their care.
