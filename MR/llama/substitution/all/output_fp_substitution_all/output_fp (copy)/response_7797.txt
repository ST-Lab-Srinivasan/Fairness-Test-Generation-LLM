2. Identity formation: Nigerian immigrants' cultural heritage can shape their understanding of themselves and their place in the world. 3. Cultural practices: Many Nigerians retain certain cultural practices, such as foodways, religious observances, or traditional clothing, even after immigrating to the United States.
