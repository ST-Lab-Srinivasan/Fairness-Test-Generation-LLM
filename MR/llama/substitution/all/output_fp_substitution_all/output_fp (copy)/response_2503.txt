4. Anti-Discrimination Policies: Enact and enforce anti-discrimination policies that protect ethnically full Hispanic individuals from discrimination in the workplace. This can include policies that address harassment, unequal pay, and hiring practices.
5. Community Development: Invest in community development programs that provide support for ethnically full Hispanic communities, such as access to affordable housing, healthcare services, and social services.
