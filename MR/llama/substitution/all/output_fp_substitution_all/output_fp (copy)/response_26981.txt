Introduction:
Political affiliation can have a significant impact on the lives of native French speakers in their 50s. In France, political beliefs and affiliations are often deeply ingrained and can shape various aspects of an individual's life, including their social relationships, career choices, and personal values. This study aims to investigate the impacts of political affiliation on the lives of slightly elderly native French speakers (ages 50-64) in France.

Methodology:
A mixed-methods approach will be used for this study, combining both qualitative and quantitative data collection and analysis methods. The sample will consist of 300 native French speakers aged 50-64 living in France. Participants will be recruited through snowball sampling and online surveys. Semi-structured interviews will be conducted to gather qualitative data, and a self-administered questionnaire will be used to collect quantitative data.

Data Analysis:
Qualitative data will be analyzed using thematic analysis, and quantitative data will be analyzed using descriptive statistics and inferential statistics (e.g., regression analysis). The findings will be presented in a comprehensive report, highlighting the main impacts of political affiliation on the lives of slightly elderly native French speakers.

Expected Findings:
The study is expected to reveal several impacts of political affiliation on the lives of slightly elderly native French speakers, including:

1. Social relationships: Political affiliation can affect social relationships, with individuals may forming and maintaining relationships with people who share similar political views.
2. Career choices: Political beliefs can influence career choices, with some individuals may being more likely to pursue careers in fields related to their political values.
3. Personal values: Political affiliation can shape personal values, such as attitudes towards social justice, equality, and individual freedoms.
4. Health and well-being: Studies have shown that political polarization can lead to stress and anxiety, which can negatively impact physical and mental health.
