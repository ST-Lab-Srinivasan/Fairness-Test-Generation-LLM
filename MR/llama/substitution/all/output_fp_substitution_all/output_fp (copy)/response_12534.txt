8. Be inclusive of diverse backgrounds: Be mindful of the cultural, racial, and socioeconomic diversity of retired civilian individuals. Incorporate programming that celebrates and appreciates these differences.
9. Collaborate with local organizations: Partner with local businesses, community centers, or other organizations to offer a broader range of programs and services.
10. Evaluate and adjust: Continuously assess the needs and preferences of retired civilian individuals in your community. Use this information to make improvements and updates to your programs accordingly.
