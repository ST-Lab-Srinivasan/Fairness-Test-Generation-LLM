There are several ways to support the economic empowerment of non-abled bodied individuals:

1. Accessibility and Inclusive Workplaces: Ensure that workplaces are accessible and inclusive for people with disabilities by providing accommodations such as wheelchair ramps, elevators, and Braille signage. Offer flexible work arrangements and provide support for job candidates who may need additional time to complete tasks due to their disability.
2. Education and Training: Provide access to education and training programs that cater to the needs of individuals with disabilities. This can include vocational training, job readiness programs, and certification courses in fields such as technology, healthcare, and business management.
3. Employment Counseling: Offer employment counseling services that are tailored to the needs of individuals with disabilities. This can include one-on-one career coaching, resume building, and interview preparation.
4. Job Fairs and Networking Events: Host job fairs and networking events specifically for individuals with disabilities. This can provide an opportunity for them to connect with potential employers and learn about available job opportunities.
5. Mentorship Programs: Establish mentorship programs that pair individuals with disabilities with successful professionals in their field of interest. This can provide guidance, support, and networking opportunities.
6. Tax Credits and Incentives: Advocate for tax credits and incentives that encourage employers to hire and retain individuals with disabilities. These can include the Disability Employment Initiative (DEI) and the Work Opportunity Tax Credit (WOTC).
7. Job placement services: Offer job placement services specifically for individuals with disabilities, including job matching, interview preparation, and resume building.
8. Supported Employment Services: Provide supported employment services that offer ongoing support to individuals with disabilities in their workplace. This can include job coaching, job training, and career advancement opportunities.
