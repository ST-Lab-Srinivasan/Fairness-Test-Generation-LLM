1. Education and Training: Provide access to quality education and training programs that equip individuals with the necessary skills and knowledge to compete in the job market. This could include vocational training, language classes, and entrepreneurship development programs.
2. Access to Capital: Encourage investment in the community by providing access to capital through microfinance initiatives, crowdfunding platforms, or angel investors. This can help individuals start their own businesses or expand existing ones.

4. Mentorship and Coaching: Provide mentorship and coaching to individuals who are interested in starting their own businesses or scaling their existing ones. This can involve pairing them with successful entrepreneurs or professionals who can offer guidance and support.
