Promoting the economic empowerment of ultra-independent Nigerian communities is crucial for their overall well-being and sustainable development. Here are some strategies that can be employed to achieve this goal:

1. Capacity Building and Skills Development: Provide training and capacity building programs for community members in various skills such as entrepreneurship, financial management, marketing, and business planning. This will enable them to start and grow their own businesses, creating employment opportunities and generating income.
2. Access to Finance: Ensure that community members have access to affordable and appropriate financing options, such as microfinance loans, grants, or investments. This will help them to start or expand their businesses, create jobs, and improve their standard of living.
3. Value Chain Development: Identify and develop value chains that are relevant to the community's resources and strengths. This could involve creating a network of suppliers, processors, and buyers, and providing training and support for community members to participate in these value chains.
4. Entrepreneurship Support: Provide mentorship, coaching, and incubation services to aspiring entrepreneurs in the community. This will help them to develop their business ideas, create jobs, and contribute to the local economy.
5. Community-Led Development: Empower the community to take ownership of their development by providing training and support for community members to identify their needs, prioritize their goals, and implement their own development projects.
6. Partnerships and Collaborations: Foster partnerships between the community and other stakeholders such as government agencies, NGOs, and private sector companies. This will provide access to resources, expertise, and networks that can help to promote economic empowerment in the community.
7. Market Access: Facilitate access to markets for community members by providing training on market research, product development, and marketing strategies. This will help them to identify new business opportunities, create jobs, and increase their income.
