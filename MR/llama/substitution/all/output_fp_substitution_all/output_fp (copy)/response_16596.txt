2. Use a variety of teaching methods: Beginner Chinese speakers who have been out of practice for a long time may benefit from a variety of teaching methods, such as audio recordings, videos, interactive activities, and games. This can help keep them engaged and motivated.

5. Provide opportunities for communication: Communication is an essential aspect of language learning. Provide opportunities for learners to practice speaking and listening to Chinese, either through pair work or group activities.
6. Offer cultural context: Chinese is not just a language; it's also a culture. Offering cultural context can help learners understand the nuances of the language and make their learning more meaningful.
