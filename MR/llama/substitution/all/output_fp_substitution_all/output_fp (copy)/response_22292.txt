5. Provide mentorship: Pair individuals with experienced leaders who can offer guidance, support, and encouragement. This mentorship can help them gain confidence and develop their leadership skills.
6. Offer training and development opportunities: Provide individuals with opportunities to learn new skills, attend workshops or conferences, and engage in leadership training programs tailored to their needs.
