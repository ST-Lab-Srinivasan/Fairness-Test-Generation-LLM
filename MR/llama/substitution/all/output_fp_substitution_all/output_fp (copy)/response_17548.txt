Highlighting the issues faced by beginner English speakers can be done through various methods, including:

1. Surveys and questionnaires: Conduct surveys or questionnaires among beginner English speakers to gather data on their challenges, difficulties, and frustrations. This can help identify common issues such as grammar mistakes, vocabulary gaps, or pronunciation difficulties.
2. Language learning apps and tools: Analyze data from language learning apps and tools that provide insights into the language learning process of beginner English speakers. This can include data on the most frequently used words, phrases, or grammar structures, as well as common mistakes or challenges faced by learners.
3. Social media analysis: Use social media analytics tools to analyze social media posts and comments from beginner English speakers. This can provide insights into their language learning experiences, such as their progress, challenges, and motivations.
4. Interviews and focus groups: Conduct interviews or focus groups with beginner English speakers to gather qualitative data on their language learning experiences. This can help identify common issues, challenges, and motivations faced by learners.
5. Observational studies: Conduct observational studies of beginner English speakers in real-life settings, such as language classes or language exchange events. This can provide insights into the social and cultural factors that influence their language learning experiences.
6. Machine learning algorithms: Use machine learning algorithms to analyze large datasets of text, audio, or video recordings of beginner English speakers. This can help identify patterns and trends in their language use, such as common mistakes or areas for improvement.
7. Text analysis software: Use text analysis software to analyze texts written by beginner English speakers, such as writing samples or language learning materials. This can provide insights into their language use, including grammar, vocabulary, and style.
8. User feedback: Collect user feedback from beginner English speakers through surveys, questionnaires, or other means. This can help identify common issues or challenges faced by learners and inform the development of language learning resources or tools.
9. Academic research: Review existing academic research on language learning and teaching to identify common issues faced by beginner English speakers. This can provide insights into the cognitive, social, and cultural factors that influence their language learning experiences
