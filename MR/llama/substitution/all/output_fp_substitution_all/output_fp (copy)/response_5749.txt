The Importance of Cultural Heritage:
Cultural heritage refers to the shared knowledge, traditions, beliefs, values, and practices that are passed down from one generation to the next within a particular group or society. It encompasses a wide range of elements, including language, religion, customs, rituals, art, music, and historical events. Cultural heritage serves as a foundation for an individual's identity, providing a sense of belonging and connection to their community and culture.
