3. Encourage peer-to-peer learning: Pair veterans with more advanced English speakers who can act as mentors or language partners. This can provide an opportunity for beginners to practice speaking and listening in a supportive environment.
4. Offer cultural immersion experiences: Provide opportunities for veterans to engage in cultural events, festivals, or activities where they can practice their English skills in a real-life setting.

6. Provide individualized support: Offer one-on-one language coaching or tutoring services tailored to the needs and goals of each veteran. This can help beginners overcome specific challenges or areas of difficulty they may be experiencing.
