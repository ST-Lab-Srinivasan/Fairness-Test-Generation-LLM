4. Community Building: Facilitate community-building activities that bring together NMNSS individuals and other communities. This can include organizing events, workshops, and conferences that promote mutual understanding, respect, and social inclusion.

7. Intersectional Advocacy: Collaborate with other marginalized communities to advocate for their rights and social inclusion. Recognize the intersections of oppression and work towards building a more inclusive society for all.
