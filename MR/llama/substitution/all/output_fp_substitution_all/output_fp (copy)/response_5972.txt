4. Address social determinants of health: Social determinants of health, such as housing, food security, and employment, can have a significant impact on health outcomes. Addressing these factors through policies and programs that support lower middle-income individuals can help improve overall health.
5. 6. Improve language access: Providing language access services, such as interpreters and translated materials, can help ensure that lower middle-income native English speakers have equal access to healthcare.
