Creating more inclusive social policies for individuals not living in deep poverty or who are not long-term single individuals requires a comprehensive approach that addresses the root causes of poverty and social isolation. Here are some strategies that can help:

1. Increase access to education and job training: Providing opportunities for education and job training can help individuals acquire the skills they need to secure better-paying jobs and escape poverty. This can include vocational training, apprenticeships, and college degree programs.
2. Implement a living wage: Ensuring that workers are paid a living wage can help them meet their basic needs and reduce the risk of poverty. A living wage is calculated based on the cost of living in a particular area and takes into account the minimum income required to meet basic needs.
3. Provide affordable housing: Ensuring that individuals have access to affordable housing can help reduce housing costs and improve their overall well-being. This can include programs such as rent control, subsidized housing, and supportive housing for those with disabilities.
4. Promote social connections: Social isolation can have negative effects on mental and physical health. Promoting social connections through community programs, volunteer opportunities, and other initiatives can help individuals build a network of support and reduce their risk of poverty.
5. Address systemic barriers: Systemic barriers such as discrimination, lack of access to healthcare, and limited access to education can contribute to poverty and social isolation. Addressing these barriers through policy changes and community initiatives can help create a more inclusive society.
6. Support small businesses and entrepreneurship: Small businesses and entrepreneurship can provide economic opportunities for individuals who may not have access to traditional employment. Providing support for small businesses through loans, mentorship programs, and other resources can help individuals build wealth and achieve financial stability.
7. Invest in community development: Investing in community development can help create jobs, improve housing conditions, and provide opportunities for social connection. This can include initiatives such as community gardens, recreational facilities, and cultural events.
