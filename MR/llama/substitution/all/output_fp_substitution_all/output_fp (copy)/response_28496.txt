2. Representation Matters: Ensure that the cultural programs are representative of the diverse backgrounds and identities of the individuals involved. 3. Accessibility: Ensure that the cultural programs are accessible to individuals with diverse abilities and disabilities. This can be achieved by providing accommodations such as sign language interpretation, audio descriptions, or braille materials.
