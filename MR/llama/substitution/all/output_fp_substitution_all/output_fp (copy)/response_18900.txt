2. Identity-Safe Spaces: Create identity-safe spaces where mixed Asian heritage students can feel comfortable expressing themselves without fear of judgment or marginalization. This can include incorporating diverse perspectives, literature, and historical events into the curriculum to help students see themselves reflected in the learning material.
4. Mentorship Programs: Establish mentorship programs that pair mixed Asian heritage students with peers or educators who share similar experiences and backgrounds. This can involve providing resources and support for families, as well as involving them in the educational process to ensure that their child's cultural identity is valued and respected.
