Promoting the artistic talents of beginner Arabic speakers in their late 30s can be a rewarding and enriching experience. Here are some suggestions on how to do it:

1. Encourage them to explore different forms of expression: Invite them to try various art forms such as writing, painting, drawing, singing, or playing an instrument. This will help them discover their hidden talents and interests.
2. Provide language support: Offer language classes or resources to help them improve their Arabic skills, which can enhance their ability to express themselves creatively.
3. Create a supportive community: Gather like-minded individuals who share similar interests and levels of proficiency in Arabic. This will give them a sense of belonging and motivation to continue learning and creating.
4. Organize cultural events: Host events that showcase Arabic culture, such as poetry readings, concerts, or art exhibitions. This will provide opportunities for them to network with other creatives and gain exposure for their work.
5. Provide performance opportunities: Look for platforms where they can showcase their talents, such as open mic nights, cultural festivals, or online communities. Encourage them to share their work with others and get feedback.
6. Offer mentorship: Connect them with experienced artists or professionals in their field of interest. This will provide valuable guidance, support, and inspiration for their creative journey.
7. Emphasize the importance of practice: Encourage them to set aside time each day to practice their art, whether it's writing, drawing, or practicing an instrument. Consistency is key to improving skills and developing creativity.
8. Celebrate their progress: Acknowledge and celebrate their achievements, no matter how small they may seem. This will help build their confidence and motivation to continue creating.
9. Provide resources and tools: Offer access to materials or software that can help them improve their craft, such as writing apps, art supplies, or recording equipment.
10. Encourage collaboration: Pair them with other creatives who share similar interests or skill levels. This will provide opportunities for learning, growth, and mutual support.
