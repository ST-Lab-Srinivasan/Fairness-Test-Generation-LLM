2. Increased trust: When healthcare providers demonstrate cultural competence, they build trust with their patients. 3. Better communication: Cultural competence training can help healthcare providers communicate more effectively with mixed African American patients. This is critical for understanding the patient's needs and concerns and providing appropriate care.

5. Enhanced patient satisfaction: When healthcare providers demonstrate cultural competence, patients are more likely to be satisfied with their care. This can lead to higher patient satisfaction rates and improved overall health outcomes.
6. Better understanding of comorbidities: Mixed African Americans may experience a higher prevalence of comorbid conditions due to the interplay between genetic and environmental factors.
