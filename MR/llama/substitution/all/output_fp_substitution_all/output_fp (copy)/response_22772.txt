5. Promoting inclusivity: Cultural exchange programs can promote inclusivity by bringing together individuals from diverse backgrounds. This can help create a more inclusive and welcoming environment for everyone.
6. Fostering creativity: Cultural exchange can foster creativity by exposing people to new ideas, customs, and perspectives. This can lead to innovative thinking and problem-solving.
