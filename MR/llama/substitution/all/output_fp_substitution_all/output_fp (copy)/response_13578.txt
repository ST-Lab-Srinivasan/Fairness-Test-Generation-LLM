3. Financial difficulties: Individuals with disabilities may face financial challenges, including reduced income, increased healthcare costs, and limited access to resources and support.

7. Lack of representation and voice: People with disabilities may have limited opportunities to participate in decision-making processes and advocate for their rights, due to systemic barriers and discrimination.

9. Limited access to leisure activities: People with disabilities may encounter barriers in accessing leisure activities, such as sports, recreation, and cultural events, due to lack of accessibility or discrimination.
