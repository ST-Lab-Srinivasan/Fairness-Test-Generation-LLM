6. Media Representation: Encourage media representation that reflects the diversity of native Chinese speakers in the US. This can help challenge stereotypes and promote a more nuanced understanding of these communities.
7. Education and Awareness: Educate decision-makers, community leaders, and the general public about the experiences and contributions of native Chinese speakers. This can involve sharing stories, data, and other information that highlights their importance and value to society.
