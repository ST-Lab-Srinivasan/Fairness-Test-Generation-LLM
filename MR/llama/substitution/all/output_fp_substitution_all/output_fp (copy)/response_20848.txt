As an instructor, it's essential to recognize these stereotypes and actively challenge them in the classroom. Encourage students to explore the diversity of the Arab world and its rich cultural heritage.
2. Use inclusive language: Avoid using language that could be offensive or alienating to beginner Arabic speakers. Use language that is respectful and inclusive, and encourage students to do the same when communicating with their peers.
