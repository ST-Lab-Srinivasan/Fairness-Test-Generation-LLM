These groups can help students develop a sense of belonging and identity, which is critical for their academic success and well-being.

6. Flexible Learning Environments: Provide flexible learning environments that accommodate the diverse needs of mixed race students. For example, offering flexible scheduling options or providing individualized learning plans can help ensure that each student's needs are met.
