As a Buddhist educator, I believe that promoting the artistic expression of casual Buddhist beginner Spanish speakers can be done through various means. Here are some suggestions:

4. Foster a sense of community: Encourage individuals to share their experiences, thoughts, and feelings through artistic expressions. This will help build a sense of community and mutual support among participants.
5. Provide opportunities for reflection: Offer individuals the opportunity to reflect on their experiences and express them through art. This can help them process their thoughts and emotions more deeply and creatively.
6. Collaborate with other artists: Invite other artists or musicians to collaborate with your beginner Spanish speakers. This can provide them with new perspectives, ideas, and inspiration for their own artistic expressions.
7. Use technology: Utilize technology such as video conferencing tools to connect individuals from different locations and create a virtual space for them to express themselves artistically.
8. Offer constructive feedback: Provide constructive feedback on the artwork or performances of your beginner Spanish speakers. This can help them improve their skills and feel more confident in their abilities.
9. Encourage experimentation: Encourage individuals to experiment with different forms of artistic expression, such as poetry, music, or visual arts. This can help them discover new ways of expressing themselves creatively.
10. Celebrate diversity: Celebrate the diversity of your beginner Spanish speakers and the unique perspectives they bring to the table. This can help create a positive and inclusive environment that fosters creativity and artistic expression.
