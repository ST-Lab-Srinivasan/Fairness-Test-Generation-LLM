Promoting the financial stability of nominal Muslim long-term recently married individuals requires a multi-faceted approach that addresses various aspects of their financial lives. Here are some strategies that can help:

1. Financial Planning and Budgeting: Encourage them to develop a comprehensive financial plan that includes budgeting, saving, and investing. This will help them manage their finances effectively and make informed decisions about their money.
2. Increase Financial Literacy: Provide them with access to financial education resources, such as books, articles, and workshops, to help them understand basic financial concepts and how to apply them in their daily lives.
3. Emergency Fund: Encourage them to establish an emergency fund to cover unexpected expenses, such as medical bills or car repairs. This will help them avoid going into debt when faced with unexpected costs.
4. Insurance Coverage: Encourage them to obtain adequate insurance coverage, including health, life, and disability insurance, to protect themselves and their dependents from financial losses due to unforeseen events.
5. Career Development: Support their career development by providing access to training and professional development opportunities, which can help them increase their earning potential and achieve financial stability.
6. Debt Management: Encourage them to manage their debt effectively by paying off high-interest loans and credit card balances, and avoiding new debt accumulation.
7. Retirement Planning: Help them plan for their retirement by starting a retirement savings plan, such as a 401(k) or IRA, and contributing regularly to it.
8. Estate Planning: Encourage them to create a comprehensive estate plan, including a will, trusts, and powers of attorney, to ensure that their assets are distributed according to their wishes after they pass away.
9. Financial Counseling: Provide them with access to financial counseling services, which can help them make informed decisions about their finances and overcome financial challenges.
10. Community Support: Encourage them to connect with other Muslims in their community who are facing similar financial challenges, and provide a support network for them to share experiences and advice.
