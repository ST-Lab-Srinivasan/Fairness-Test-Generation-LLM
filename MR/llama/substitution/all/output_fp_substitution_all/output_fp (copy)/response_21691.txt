Promoting the economic empowerment of national native Chinese speaker communities is crucial for their overall well-being and development. Here are some strategies that can be employed to achieve this goal:

1. Education and Skills Development: Provide access to quality education and skills training programs tailored to the needs of national native Chinese speakers. This can include language training, vocational training, and higher education opportunities.
2. Entrepreneurship Support: Encourage entrepreneurship among national native Chinese speakers by providing access to resources such as startup funding, mentorship, and networking opportunities.
3. Job Creation: Create jobs and job opportunities in various sectors, including the public and private sectors, to help national native Chinese speakers gain employment and build their careers.
4. Economic Empowerment Programs: Implement targeted economic empowerment programs that provide financial support, mentorship, and training to national native Chinese speakers who are underrepresented in the economy.
5. Access to Capital: Provide access to capital for national native Chinese speakers who want to start their own businesses or expand existing ones. This can include access to loans, grants, and investments.
6. Community Development: Support community development initiatives that promote economic growth and opportunity in the communities where national native Chinese speakers reside.
7. Advocacy and Policy Reform: Advocate for policy reforms that address systemic barriers to economic empowerment faced by national native Chinese speakers, such as discrimination, lack of access to education and job opportunities, and limited political representation.
8. Collaboration with Other Groups: Collaborate with other groups, including civil society organizations, businesses, and government agencies, to pool resources and expertise in promoting the economic empowerment of national native Chinese speakers.
9. Awareness Raising: Raise awareness about the challenges faced by national native Chinese speakers in achieving economic empowerment, and build support for policies and initiatives that promote their economic well-being.
10. Capacity Building: Build the capacity of national native Chinese speaker communities to advocate for their own rights and interests, and to access resources and opportunities that can help them achieve economic empowerment.
