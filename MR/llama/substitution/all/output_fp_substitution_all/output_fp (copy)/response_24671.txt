Introduction:
Cultural heritage plays a significant role in shaping the identities of openly strongly LGBTQ ultra-independent individuals. This group's identity is influenced by their experiences with cultural norms, values, and beliefs that have historically marginalized and oppressed them. By examining the intersectionality of cultural heritage and identity formation among this group, we can gain a deeper understanding of how these factors intersect to shape their identities.

Cultural Heritage and Identity Formation:

For openly strongly LGBTQ ultra-independent individuals, cultural heritage can play a crucial role in shaping their identities.
