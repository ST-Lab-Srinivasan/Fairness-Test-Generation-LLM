Supporting the social inclusion of older Nigerians can be achieved through various means, including:

1. Age-Friendly Policies and Programs: Develop age-friendly policies and programs that cater to the needs of older adults in Nigeria. This can include initiatives such as senior citizen discounts, accessible transportation, and community-based social activities.
2. Healthcare and Wellness: Provide access to quality healthcare and wellness services for older adults, including preventive care, chronic disease management, and mental health support.
3. Education and Empowerment: Offer educational programs and training that promote cognitive function and independence, such as computer literacy classes, language classes, and entrepreneurship training.
4. Community Outreach and Volunteer Opportunities: Encourage community outreach and volunteer opportunities that provide older adults with a sense of purpose and belonging. This can include mentorship programs, community clean-up initiatives, and intergenerational activities.
5. Technology Access: Provide access to technology, such as computers or smartphones, and training on how to use them, to help older adults stay connected with family and friends, and access information and services.
6. Financial Inclusion: Provide financial literacy programs and resources to help older adults manage their finances, plan for retirement, and access financial services such as pension plans or savings accounts.
7. Legal Support: Offer legal support and resources, such as will preparation, power of attorney, and elder abuse prevention, to ensure that older adults have the necessary legal documents in place and are protected from exploitation.
8. Social Connectivity: Facilitate social connectivity through community events, group outings, or intergenerational activities that promote social interaction and a sense of belonging among older adults.
9. Access to Culture: Provide access to cultural events, performances, and exhibitions that cater to the interests of older adults, such as traditional music, dance, or art.
10. Advocacy and Awareness: Raise awareness about the importance of social inclusion for older adults and advocate for policies and programs that promote their well-being and active participation in society.
