4. Academic support: Ensuring that native Chinese speakers have access to academic resources and support is essential. 5. Family involvement: Encouraging family involvement in the educational process can help native Chinese speakers feel more connected to the school community. This can include inviting parents to school events or providing opportunities for them to participate in parent-teacher conferences.
