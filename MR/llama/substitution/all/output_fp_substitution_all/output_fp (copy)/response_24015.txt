2. Use visual aids: Visual aids such as diagrams, pictures, or videos can help beginner Chinese speaker spouses communicate their ideas more effectively. This can be especially helpful if they are struggling to find the right words to express themselves.
3. Provide language support: Offer language support and resources to beginner Chinese speaker spouses who may need additional assistance in communicating their thoughts and opinions. This could include providing translation services, language classes, or using interpreters during decision-making processes.
4. Encourage participation: Make an effort to involve beginner Chinese speaker spouses in decision-making processes by asking for their input and ideas. Use phrases like "What do you think?" or "How do you see this situation?" to encourage their participation.
