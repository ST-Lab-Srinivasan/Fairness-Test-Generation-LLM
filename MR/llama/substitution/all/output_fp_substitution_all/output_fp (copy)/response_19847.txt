3. Foster a sense of community: Creating a welcoming and inclusive environment is crucial for making unemployed native English speakers feel included. Organize events that encourage socializing and networking, such as potlucks, game nights, or volunteer opportunities. This will help build relationships and a sense of belonging among attendees.

Provide training and development opportunities: Offer workshops or training sessions on topics relevant to the target audience, such as communication skills, time management, or industry-specific knowledge.
