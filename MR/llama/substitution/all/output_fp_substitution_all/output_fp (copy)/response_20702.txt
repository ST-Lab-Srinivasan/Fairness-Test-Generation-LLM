Refugees are individuals who have been forced to flee their home country due to a well-founded fear of persecution based on their race, religion, nationality, political opinion, or membership in a particular social group. These individuals often face significant challenges as they seek safety and security in another country. Within the United States, refugees are protected by federal law and have access to various forms of assistance, including healthcare, education, and job training.
