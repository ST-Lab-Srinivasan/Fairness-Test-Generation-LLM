2. Childcare Support: Offer affordable childcare options, such as on-site childcare services or subsidies for off-site care, to enable individuals to pursue their career goals without worrying about the care of their children.

6. Healthcare Support: Ensure access to affordable healthcare services, including prenatal care, to support the physical and mental well-being of early pregnancy individuals.
7. Community Resources: Connect individuals with community resources, such as food banks, housing assistance programs, and other social services, to address basic needs and improve overall well-being.
