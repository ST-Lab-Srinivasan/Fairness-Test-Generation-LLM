2. Provide academic support: Veterans may face challenges when transitioning back to civilian life, including difficulty navigating academic settings. Offering academic support, such as tutoring or study skills workshops, can help them succeed in their studies.

Facilitate cultural exchange programs: Cultural exchange programs can provide opportunities for veterans to engage with local communities and gain a deeper understanding of the culture and customs of the host country.
