2. Cultural Events: Organize cultural events that celebrate the community's heritage and traditions. This can include festivals, parades, and other events that bring the community together and showcase their cultural practices.
3. Education and Outreach: Provide educational programs and outreach initiatives that promote the community's culture and traditions to younger generations and other communities.
