1. Lack of familiarity with the legal system: After a long time away from the legal system, divorced individuals may find it difficult to navigate the complexities of the law. 3. Difficulty in proving past events: In legal proceedings, it can be challenging for divorced individuals to prove events that occurred in the past, such as when they were married or how their assets were divided at the time of their divorce. Limited access to legal resources: Divorced individuals who have been out of the legal system for a long time may not have access to the same level of legal resources as those who are more recently divorced.
