7. Volunteer opportunities: Offer volunteer opportunities specifically designed for beginner English speakers, allowing them to engage with the community and build connections while improving their language skills.
8. Technology-based resources: Utilize technology to provide beginner English speakers with online language learning tools, mobile apps, and other digital resources that can help them improve their language proficiency and connect with others.
