This will help educators understand and address the unique needs of these students in the classroom.
2. Identity-Based Curriculum: Develop curricula that reflect the diverse identities and experiences of mixed race individuals, including their historical and contemporary experiences as well as their cultural practices and traditions. Encourage open dialogue and mutual understanding among all students, regardless of their racial identity.
