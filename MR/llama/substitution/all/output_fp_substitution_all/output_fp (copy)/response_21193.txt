As a community developer, here are some effective strategies for supporting Just Turned 65 (JT65) Native French Speaker Communities:

1. Language and Cultural Immersion Programs: Offer language and cultural immersion programs specifically designed for JT65 communities. These programs can help bridge the gap between the younger generation and the older generation, fostering intergenerational connections and a sense of belonging.
2. Community Outreach and Engagement: Develop targeted outreach and engagement strategies to reach JT65 communities. This can involve partnering with local organizations, community centers, and religious institutions to build relationships and offer tailored support.
3. Technology Training and Access: Provide training and access to technology, such as tablets or laptops, for JT65 individuals who may not be familiar with these tools. This can help them stay connected with their communities and family members, as well as access important resources and services.
4. Mental Health and Wellness Support: Offer mental health and wellness support specifically tailored to the needs of JT65 communities. This can involve partnering with local mental health professionals to provide counseling, therapy, and other support services.
5. Intergenerational Programs: Develop intergenerational programs that bring together younger and older community members for mutual learning and socialization. Examples include mentorship programs, intergenerational book clubs, or collaborative art projects.
6. Community Events and Celebrations: Organize community events and celebrations that are tailored to the cultural and linguistic needs of JT65 communities. This can involve organizing festivals, concerts, or other cultural events that promote intergenerational connections and a sense of belonging.
7. Advocacy and Policy Change: Advocate for policy changes that support the needs of JT65 communities, such as linguistic and cultural preservation, access to healthcare and social services, and protection from discrimination.
