Creating more inclusive public spaces for extreme, moderate, and independent native Arabic speakers requires careful consideration of their needs and preferences. Here are some strategies that can help:

1. Accessibility: Ensure that the public space is accessible to people with disabilities by providing ramps, elevators, and accessible restrooms. This will enable individuals with mobility impairments to fully participate in the public space.
2. Signage: Provide clear and accurate signage in Arabic, including braille and large print options, to assist individuals who are visually impaired or have reading difficulties. This can help them navigate the public space more easily.
3. Language Support: Offer language support services such as interpretation, translation, and bilingual staff to help individuals communicate effectively in their preferred language.
4. Cultural Sensitivity: Be sensitive to the cultural practices and beliefs of the Arabic-speaking community. For example, provide separate areas for prayer or accommodate prayer times during the day.
5. Education and Awareness: Educate the public on the importance of inclusivity and how to interact with individuals from diverse backgrounds. This can help break down barriers and promote a more inclusive environment.
6. Technology: Utilize technology such as audio descriptions, closed captioning, and tactile graphics to make the public space more accessible to individuals with visual impairments.
7. Inclusive Design: Design the public space with inclusivity in mind, incorporating features such as adaptable furniture, ergonomic design, and flexible spaces that can accommodate different group sizes and configurations.
8. Access to Information: Provide information in Arabic, including brochures, maps, and wayfinding signs, to ensure that individuals have access to the same information as the rest of the public.
9. Community Engagement: Engage with the local Arabic-speaking community through outreach programs, surveys, and focus groups to better understand their needs and preferences when designing the public space.
10. Inclusive Programming: Offer programming and activities that cater to the diverse needs of the Arabic-speaking community, such as cultural events, language classes, and social gatherings.
