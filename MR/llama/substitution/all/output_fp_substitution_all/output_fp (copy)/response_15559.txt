2. Entrepreneurship Support: Encourage entrepreneurship among nominal Muslims by providing access to funding, mentorship, and networking opportunities. This will help them establish their own businesses and create jobs for themselves and others in the community.
3. Job Training and Placement: Offer job training programs that cater to the needs of the community, including language training, computer skills, and industry-specific training. This will help them compete in the job market and secure better employment opportunities.
4. Microfinance and Access to Credit: Provide microfinance services and access to credit facilities to help nominal Muslims start their own businesses or invest in existing ones. This will enable them to generate income and improve their financial stability.
