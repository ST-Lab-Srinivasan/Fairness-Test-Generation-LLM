Their cultural heritage can influence how they communicate with others, as they may adopt certain linguistic features or communication strategies that are specific to their own culture.
5. Intercultural communication: As individuals interact with native speakers and other cultures, they may encounter communication barriers and misunderstandings. Their cultural heritage can help them navigate these challenges by providing a framework for understanding and responding appropriately to different cultural cues.
