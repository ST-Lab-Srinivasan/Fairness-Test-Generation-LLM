7. Lack of support: Ethnic Asian professionals may not have access to the same level of support and resources as their white counterparts, such as mentorship programs or professional networks.

10. Limited access to education and training: Ethnic Asian individuals may have limited access to educational and training opportunities, which can impact their ability to advance in their careers.
