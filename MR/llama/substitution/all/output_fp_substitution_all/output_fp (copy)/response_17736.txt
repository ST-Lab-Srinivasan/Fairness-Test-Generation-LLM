There are several ways to support the social inclusion of non-wealthy, non-independent individuals:

1. Advocacy and awareness-raising: Raise awareness about the challenges faced by non-wealthy, non-independent individuals in society, and advocate for policies and practices that promote their inclusion.
2. Education and skills training: Provide education and skills training opportunities to help individuals acquire the knowledge and skills they need to participate fully in society.
3. Healthcare access: Ensure that individuals have access to quality healthcare services, including mental health support, to help them manage any challenges they may face.
4. Affordable housing: Work towards providing affordable housing options for individuals who may struggle to access decent and safe living spaces.
5. Employment opportunities: Create employment opportunities that are inclusive and accessible to individuals from all backgrounds, including those who may not have the means to start their own businesses.
6. Community-based programs: Develop community-based programs that provide support and resources to individuals who may be struggling with social isolation or other challenges.
7. Legal aid: Provide legal aid services to help individuals navigate the legal system and access the resources they need to address any challenges they may face.
8. Mentorship and peer support: Pair individuals with mentors or peers who can provide emotional support, guidance, and encouragement.
9. Access to technology: Provide access to technology, such as computers or smartphones, to help individuals stay connected and engaged with society.
10. Intergenerational programs: Develop intergenerational programs that bring together individuals of different ages and backgrounds to learn from each other and build relationships.
11. Community gardening: Create community gardening projects that provide opportunities for individuals to work together, learn new skills, and access fresh produce.
12. Cultural events: Organize cultural events that celebrate diversity and promote social inclusion, such as festivals, concerts, or exhibitions.
13. Health and wellness programs: Develop health and wellness programs that provide individuals with the resources they need to maintain their physical and mental health.
14. Language classes: Offer language classes to help individuals learn new languages and connect with diverse communities.
