2. Cross-cultural dialogue: Cultural exchange programs can facilitate cross-cultural dialogue and communication between Native Americans and veterans. This can help break down stereotypes and misconceptions and promote mutual understanding and respect.
3. Community engagement: Cultural exchange programs can encourage community engagement and collaboration between Native American and veteran communities.
