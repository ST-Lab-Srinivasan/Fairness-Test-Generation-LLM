3. Home Health Care: Provide home health care services to help individuals with mixed African American heritage and genetic conditions manage their condition-related needs. This may include nursing care, personal care, and medical social work.
4. Support Groups: Facilitate support groups for individuals with mixed African American heritage and genetic conditions and their families. These groups can provide emotional support, practical advice, and a sense of community.
5. Advocacy and Education: Advocate for policies and programs that promote affordable and accessible housing options for individuals with mixed African American heritage and genetic conditions. Financial Assistance: Provide financial assistance to individuals with mixed African American heritage and genetic conditions who require additional resources to access housing modifications or home health care services. 7. Transportation Support: Arrange for transportation services to ensure that individuals with mixed African American heritage and genetic conditions can access medical appointments, social events, and other activities without barriers.
