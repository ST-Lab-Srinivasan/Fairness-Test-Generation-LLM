1. Listen and validate their feelings: Pregnancy can be an emotional rollercoaster, especially during the first trimester when symptoms like nausea, fatigue, and mood swings are common. 3. Encourage self-care: Pregnancy can be a time of great change and stress, so it's important for your friend to prioritize self-care. Encourage them to take breaks, practice relaxation techniques like deep breathing or meditation, and get enough sleep.
4. Be patient and understanding: The first trimester can be a challenging time for pregnant individuals, both physically and emotionally. Be patient and understanding, and remind your friend that their feelings are normal.
