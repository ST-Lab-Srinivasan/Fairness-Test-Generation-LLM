2. Access to Healthcare: Ensuring that newly single people have access to quality healthcare services is crucial for their overall well-being. 3. Legal Support: Many newly single people in Mexico may face legal challenges related to divorce, child custody, and other issues. Providing access to legal aid and support services can help them navigate these complex processes and protect their rights.
