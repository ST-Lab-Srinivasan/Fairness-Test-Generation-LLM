2. Develop programming that caters to their interests: Orthodox Jewish communities have a rich cultural heritage, including traditional music, dance, theater, and art. Create programs that showcase these aspects of their culture and provide opportunities for community members to participate and engage with them.

5. Provide transportation and childcare options: For many Orthodox Jewish individuals, access to transportation and childcare can be a significant barrier to attending cultural events. Consider providing these services to make it easier for community members to participate.
