Supporting the social inclusion of recent refugees to Canada can be achieved through various means. Here are some ways to promote their integration:

1. Language Training: Providing language training programs for refugees can help them improve their communication skills and navigate everyday life in Canada. This can include English or French classes, as well as training in cultural competency and understanding Canadian customs.
2. Job Readiness Programs: Offering job readiness programs can help refugees develop the skills and knowledge needed to find employment in Canada. This can include training in resume writing, interview preparation, and job search strategies.
3. Mentorship Programs: Pairing recent refugees with experienced Canadians can provide them with valuable guidance and support during their transition. This can include mentors who can offer advice on navigating Canadian culture, finding employment, and building a social network.
4. Community Integration Programs: Organizing community integration programs can help refugees connect with other members of the community and build social connections. This can include events that promote intercultural dialogue and understanding, such as potluck dinners, cultural festivals, or volunteer opportunities.
5. Access to Healthcare Services: Ensuring access to healthcare services is crucial for refugees' well-being and integration. Providing information on available healthcare services, as well as offering interpretation and translation support can help them navigate the system more easily.
6. Education and Training: Offering education and training programs can help refugees acquire new skills and knowledge to enhance their employability in Canada. This can include vocational training, academic courses, or certification programs.
7. Advocacy and Legal Support: Providing advocacy and legal support can help refugees navigate the complex immigration system and access their rights and entitlements. This can include legal clinics, advocacy groups, or individualized support from a lawyer or advocate.
8. Housing Support: Ensuring access to safe and affordable housing is crucial for refugees' well-being and integration. Providing information on available housing options, as well as offering financial support for rent or mortgage payments can help them establish a stable living situation.
