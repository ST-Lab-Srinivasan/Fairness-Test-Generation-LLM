To combat this, focus on highlighting your transferable skills and experience, and emphasize your ability to learn quickly and adapt to new situations.

5. Limited job opportunities: Depending on where you live, there may be limited job opportunities available, especially if you're in a rural area or small town. To increase your chances of finding a job, consider broadening your job search to include nearby cities or regions with more job openings.
