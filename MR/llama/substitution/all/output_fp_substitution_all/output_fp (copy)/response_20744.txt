5. Engage with policymakers: Meet with elected officials and government representatives to discuss the concerns of older adults. Be sure to provide specific examples of how policies can be improved to better support this population.
6. Provide education and training: Offer workshops, seminars, and other educational opportunities that teach older adults about their rights and how to advocate for themselves. This will empower them to take action and make their voices heard.
