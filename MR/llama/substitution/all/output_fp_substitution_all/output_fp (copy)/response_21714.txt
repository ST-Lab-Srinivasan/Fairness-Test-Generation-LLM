4. Financial Assistance: Offer financial assistance, such as emergency loans or grants, to help out-of-work Native Americans cover basic needs like housing, food, and clothing. This can help alleviate financial stress during times of crisis.

This could involve partnering with local organizations, conducting door-to-door outreach, or hosting community events.
