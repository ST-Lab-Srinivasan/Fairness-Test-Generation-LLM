4. Financial assistance: Offering financial assistance programs, such as mortgage assistance or rental assistance, can help former soldiers purchase or rent homes in areas with good job opportunities.
5. Housing counseling: Providing housing counseling services specifically tailored to the needs of older veterans can help them navigate the complex process of finding and securing affordable housing.
