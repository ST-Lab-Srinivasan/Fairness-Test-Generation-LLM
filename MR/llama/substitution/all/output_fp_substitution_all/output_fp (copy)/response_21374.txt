4. Community Connectivity: Facilitate connections with other refugee families in the area, organizing playdates or outings to help them build social networks and feel less isolated.
5. Cultural Adaptation: Offer cultural orientation programs that provide information on US customs, laws, and social norms, as well as resources for navigating daily life in the US.
