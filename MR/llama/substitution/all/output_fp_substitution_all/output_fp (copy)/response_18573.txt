Promoting the social inclusion of Native natural-born US citizen poor communities is essential for reducing poverty and improving overall well-being. Here are some strategies that can help:

1. Address systemic barriers: Identify and address systemic barriers that prevent these communities from accessing basic services such as healthcare, education, and job opportunities. This may involve advocating for policy changes at the local, state, or federal level.
2. Community-based initiatives: Support community-based initiatives that promote social inclusion, such as language and cultural programs, job training and placement services, and mentorship opportunities. These initiatives can help build a sense of belonging and connection to society among community members.
3. Economic empowerment: Foster economic empowerment by providing access to capital, microfinance, and entrepreneurial training programs. This can help community members start their own businesses or invest in existing ones, creating job opportunities and generating income.
4. Health and wellness programs: Implement health and wellness programs that address the unique needs of these communities, such as diabetes prevention, mental health services, and nutrition education.
5. Education and job training: Provide access to quality education and job training programs that prepare community members for in-demand jobs and careers. This can help increase their earning potential and reduce poverty.
6. Advocacy and awareness: Raise awareness about the challenges facing these communities and advocate for policies that promote social inclusion. Engage with local media, policymakers, and other stakeholders to build support for initiatives that benefit these communities.
7. Intergenerational programs: Develop intergenerational programs that bring together community members of different ages to share knowledge, skills, and experiences. This can help build relationships and promote social inclusion across generations.
8. Cultural preservation: Support cultural preservation efforts that promote the unique traditions, customs, and practices of these communities. This can help foster a sense of identity and belonging among community members.
9. Community spaces: Create community spaces that provide a safe and welcoming environment for community members to gather, socialize, and access services. This can help build a sense of community and promote social inclusion.
