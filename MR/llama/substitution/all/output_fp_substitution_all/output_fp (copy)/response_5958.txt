5. Community development initiatives: Support community development initiatives that promote economic growth and job creation within the ethnic Hispanic community. This can include initiatives such as business incubators, cooperatives, and community-based enterprises.
6. Advocacy and policy change: Advocate for policies that address systemic barriers to economic empowerment facing ethnic Hispanics, such as discrimination, lack of access to resources, and limited opportunities for education and employment.

8. Addressing language barriers: Provide language training and interpretation services to help ethnic Hispanics communicate effectively in the mainstream economy.
