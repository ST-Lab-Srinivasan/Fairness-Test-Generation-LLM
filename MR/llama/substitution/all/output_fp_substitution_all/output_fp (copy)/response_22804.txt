2. Capacity Building: Provide training and capacity building opportunities for individuals and organizations to enhance their skills and knowledge in areas such as program management, volunteer coordination, and fundraising.
3. Inclusive Design: Ensure that programs are designed to be inclusive of all individuals, regardless of their abilities or disabilities.
