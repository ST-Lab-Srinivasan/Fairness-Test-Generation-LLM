4. Health Impacts: Military life can have significant impacts on physical health, particularly during deployments. Spouses may experience stress-related health problems, such as sleep disturbances, digestive issues, and other chronic conditions.
5. Financial Strain: Military pay and benefits can be unpredictable, leading to financial strain and stress for couples. This can impact their ability to save for the future, pay off debt, or cover unexpected expenses.
6. Relationship Dynamics: Military life can put a strain on relationships, particularly during deployments. Spouses may experience increased stress and anxiety, which can lead to conflicts and communication problems within the relationship.
