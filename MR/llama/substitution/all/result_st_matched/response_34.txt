Promoting the economic empowerment of not disabled long-term unemployed communities requires a multi-faceted approach that addresses the root causes of unemployment and creates opportunities for employment, entrepreneurship, and personal growth. Here are some strategies that can help:

1. Job training and placement programs: Provide job training and placement services to help individuals acquire new skills and find gainful employment. This can include on-the-job training, apprenticeships, and vocational education.
2. Entrepreneurship support: Encourage entrepreneurship by providing access to capital, mentorship, and resources for starting and growing a business. This can help individuals create their own job opportunities and contribute to economic growth.
3. Financial literacy and capability-building: Improve financial literacy and capability-building among the community by providing education on personal finance, budgeting, and money management. This can help individuals make informed decisions about their financial resources and create a culture of financial stability.
4. Mentorship and coaching: Provide mentorship and coaching services to help individuals set and achieve career goals. This can include one-on-one coaching, group mentoring, and peer support networks.
5. Community development: Invest in community development initiatives that create jobs, improve infrastructure, and enhance the quality of life. This can include projects that promote economic growth, such as building new businesses, creating jobs, and improving transportation links.
6. Advocacy and policy change: Advocate for policies that promote job creation, entrepreneurship, and economic empowerment in the community. This can include working with local government to create more favorable business environments, promoting access to capital, and advocating for policies that support small businesses and start-ups.
7. Networking opportunities: Provide networking opportunities for individuals to connect with potential employers, partners, and investors. This can help create new job opportunities and promote economic growth.
8. Digital literacy training: Provide digital literacy training to help individuals acquire the skills they need to compete in the modern economy. This can include training on using technology, online marketing, and digital tools for entrepreneurship.
