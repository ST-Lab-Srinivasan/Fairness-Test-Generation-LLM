4. Traditional Arts and Crafts: Promote the practice and preservation of traditional arts and crafts, such as beadwork, pottery, and weaving. This can help to maintain cultural traditions and provide economic opportunities for community members.
5. Land and Resource Management: Support land and resource management initiatives that prioritize the preservation of Native American lands and resources. This can involve collaborating with tribal governments and organizations to develop sustainable land use plans and protect cultural heritage sites.
